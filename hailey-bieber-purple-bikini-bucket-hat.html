Hailey Bieber Paired Her Purple Bikini with a Checkered Crochet Bucket Hat

Publish date: 2024-02-16

Hailey Bieber is beating the winter blues in the best way — in a bikini. On a beach somewhere warm.

On Saturday, the model shared a carousel of snapshots from a recent tropical getaway on Instagram. Captioning the post only with a red heart and palm tree emoji, Hailey posed in the sand wearing a purple string bikini underneath a sheer pink patterned cover-up. She accessorized with a coordinating checkered bucket hat and layers of beaded necklaces.

In the second slide, Hailey posted a closeup photo of her husband Justin Bieber planting a kiss on her cheek, and in the following shot, she went makeup-free while debuting a new braided hairstyle. She also included a magnified image of a full moon and a photo of her lounging around with friend Justine Skye.

While her vacation destination is unknown, Hailey was spending time in Miami during Art Basel just last month. There, she attended the Saint Laurent Rive Droite Art Basel Miami Beach event, and wore a skintight floral catsuit with a plunging halter neck. Based on her wardrobe alone, we'd say Hailey is skipping winter entirely and moving straight to spring.
