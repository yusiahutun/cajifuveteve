Celebrity weddings 2015: The stars who married this year

Publish date: 2024-02-02
    This year is turning out to be a very special one for some of the biggest celebs as they finally tie the knot. Take a trip down memory lane and look at some of the best celeb wedding moments from 2015. Looking for winter wedding inspiration? TV host Daisy Fuentes, 49, and 'Right Here Waiting' singer Richard Marx, 52, showed how to do cold weather luxe in style when they tied the knot in Aspen, Colorado just two days before Christmas. When the pair went to get their marriage license on December 22 (inset), Daisy kept warm in a cruelty-free faux fur from fabulousfurs.com.
    Photos: Instagram/@daisyfuentes It was the biggest wedding party of the year! Sofia Vergara and Joe Manganiello got married, in November, in front of 400 of their family and closest friends at the Breakers Resort in Palm Beach, Florida. The bride wore a custom made Zuhair Murad gown, and the couple danced the night away with the help of Pitbull. Photo: Instagram/@sofiavergara Jamie Chung and Bryan Greenberg tied the knot on Halloween during an outdoor ceremony in Santa Barbara, California. The bride wore two dresses for her big day: a Monique Lhuillier dress for the ceremony and a Victorian-style gown for the official wedding photos in Martha Stewart Weddings magazine. Photo: Getty Images Usher confirmed to Ellen DeGeneres that he and his longtime love, and manager, Grace Miguel tied the knot. The couple have been dating since 2009 and got engaged at the beginning of the year. Photo: Instagram/@enmemetemps Allison Williams and Ricky Van Veen's September wedding was a star-studded event. The couple said "I do" in front of 250 guests, including Alison's 'Girls' co stars Lena Dunham, Jemima Kirke and Zosia Mamet. The country-style wedding, held at Bush Creek Ranch in Wyoming was officiated by Tom Hanks. Allison also wore an Oscar de la Renta gown, inspired by Princess Kate Middleton. Photo: Instagram/@aw Jason Mraz wed his longtime girlfriend Tina Carano in a small ceremony in Virginia on October 25.
