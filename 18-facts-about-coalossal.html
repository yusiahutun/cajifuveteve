Coalossal is a unique Pokémon!

Coalossal is a Fire/Rock type Pokémon and is known for its imposing size and powerful abilities.

It evolves from Carkol at level 34!

Carkol, a Pokémon resembling a coal mine cart, evolves into Coalossal once it reaches level 34.

Its ability is Steam Engine!

Coalossal’s unique ability, Steam Engine, boosts its Speed stat when it is hit by a Fire or Water type move.

Coalossal is known for its Gigantamax form!

It can generate intense heat from its body!

Coalossal has the ability to generate high temperatures from its coal-covered body, making it a formidable opponent in battle.

Coalossal can learn powerful fire-based moves!

With its Fire typing, Coalossal can learn a variety of devastating fire-based moves such as Flamethrower and Heat Crash.

Coalossal’s appearance is reminiscent of ancient mythical creatures like dragons or golems, adding to its allure.

As its name suggests, Coalossal’s body is primarily composed of coal and rock, giving it a sturdy and durable constitution.

Coalossal stands at a towering height of over 28 feet, making it an imposing figure on the battlefield.

Coalossal excels in Defense and Special Defense!

With its solid rock-like exterior, Coalossal boasts great Defense and Special Defense stats, allowing it to withstand heavy hits.
