TALK OF THE TOWN: How Rosanna Konarzewski overcame her fear of needles

Publish date: 2024-08-11

She’s a society darling with brains, a lovingly restored Riva classic sports boat and a string of high profile pals – not to mention an adorable maltipoo dog named Gidget McFluff.

Yet life isn’t all roses for Rosanna Konarzewski.

Rosanna underwent torturous treatment to fix a debilitating fear of needles just before the pandemic. The outcome? She volunteered as a St John Ambulance Covid vaccinator of course! And they’re so proud to have Rosanna, they shared a video of her jabbing away at the Science Museum in their famous green attire, without any jitters or signs of her former phobia.

The ex-model has form for helping out good causes.

She’s also collaborated with the Duchess of Cornwall on the Booker Prize, when she worked for the former sponsor.
