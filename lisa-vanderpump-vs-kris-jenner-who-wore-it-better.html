Lisa Vanderpump vs. Kris Jenner!

Publish date: 2024-05-22

It's two popular reality TV stars in the same Alexander McQueen dress -- so who wore it better?

Lisa Vanderpump sported the gorgeous gown to the "Real Housewives of Beverly Hills" premiere party on Wednesday, in Los Angeles.

The Bravo star wore her hair in loose curls and finished her look with tear-drop earrings and a black clutch. She held her adorable pup Jiggy as she posed for photos on the red carpet.

But Lisa isn't the first celeb to sport the sexy dress -- Kris Jenner wore the same lavish ensemble earlier this week for Kim Kardashian's engagement celebration at AT&T baseball stadium in San Francisco.

The "Keeping Up with the Kardashian" star went with minimal accessories and stuck to her traditional hairstyle.

While both the reality stars can pull off the low-cut look, we have to give the nod to Lisa. We love her added touch of bling and her decolletage!
