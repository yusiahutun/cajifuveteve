Madonna proves she's still a Bad Girl with sultry lace corset selfie

Publish date: 2024-09-24

Madonna is back and better than ever after a health scare earlier this year which forced her to postpone the start of her world tour. However, she’s set to kick the festivities off in London in just 10 days, and from her latest corset selfie, we can tell it’s going to be a blast.

The music star has had an incredible career of over three decades, and it doesn’t look like the Queen of Pop is going to be stopping anytime soon, as her latest Instagram snap proves she’s still a ‘Bad Girl’.

Madonna stuns in lace corset selfie

Taking to her 19.2 million followers, the 65-year-old posted a sultry corset selfie, and fans can’t get enough.

Known for her great fashion, the star did not disappoint as she showed off her timeless appeal in a tight black corset.

In the first snap of the photo dump, Madonna can be seen looking directly into the camera with a full face of flawless makeup, showing off her lace glove to match the corset.

Further along the slides, fans can see the lacy number in more detail as she stares down the camera with her bright green eyes, sporting two pigtail plaits.

The low-cut corset top shows off the Material Girl singer’s toned figure. However, the star can rock any style as in the same post, also captures herself in a tour hoody and sunglasses. Honestly, she looks equally as great in both.

As the highly anticipated tour gets closer and closer, the artist also asked fans in the post what songs they want to see her perform, as she penned in the caption: “Now Loading………….♥️ #madonnacelebrationtour”

The star is always looking great, as many fans said she was looking “better than ever” in her first snap since leaving ICU back in July.

Fans say ‘the best is yet to come’

Upon seeing the post on their timeline, fans rushed to compliment the star on how good she looks, as well as expressing their excitement for her upcoming Celebration Tour.

“No matter how much the queen has done (she’s done it all FIRST)..she is still underrated and not given the crown she deserves. Bow down! The best is yet to come. It’s a celebration,” penned one.

“I am so excited for your tour. I felt the same at my first M concert 30 years ago,” wrote one dedicated fan.

One excited fan exclaimed: “We are ready!!!!!”

“Stunning!!!! So excited to celebrate!” wrote another.
