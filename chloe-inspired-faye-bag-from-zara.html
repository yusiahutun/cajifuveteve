Designer look for less! Chloe Faye style backpack from ZARA, 29.99

Publish date: 2024-02-28

Get Alessandra Ambrosio's Chloe Faye look on a budget at high-street chain ZARA

    If you have had your eye on the gorgeous Chloe Faye backpack, worn by Brazilian Victoria's Secret model Alessandra Ambrosio, we have some seriously great news for you! Zara has produced an incredible double of the iconic designer bag, for just £29.99! The Chloe bag in question is made in classic shades of grey, black and tan, and high-street favourite Zara has produced a carbon copy which is near on identical in structure, size and colour! It features a central, ring-shaped fastening, two handles, a detachable strap, striking gold metal hardware and snap button fastening. And if grey isn't your colour and you prefer a darker shade, the bag is also available in black, which is equally chic. Grab a bargain!

    Get the Chloe look (left) for less with this ZARA steal!

    The French designer brand Chloe is hugely famous for its diverse bag offerings, and the 'Faye' is fast becoming one of its signature pieces. The mini leather backpack is made from suede and leather and boasts two shoulder straps, a lobster clasp fastening, a detachable top handle and of course, the iconic silver ring at the centre.

    Alessandra Ambrosio running errands carrying her Chloe Faye backpack

    Busy supermodel mum Alessandro, 36, has been photographed wearing the backpack on numerous occasions, particularly whilst doing errands. She has the designer bag in a whole array of shades, including tan, with each backpack setting her back around £1,000 from the high-end French fashion house.
