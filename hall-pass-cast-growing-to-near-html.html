Hall Pass Cast Growing to NearValentines Day Levels

Publish date: 2024-05-17

Eighties teen steamers Christina Applegate and Alyssa Milano have joined the burgeoning cast of the Farrelly Brothers’ Hall Pass, a comedy about two guys whose wives give them weeklong “hall passes” to sleep with whomever, but then decide they want in on the action as well. The comedy starts filming on Tuesday and already stars Owen Wilson, Jason Sudeikis, Jenna Fischer, Amanda Bynes, Stephen Merchant, J.B. Smoove, Nicky Whelan, Derek Waters, Larry Campbell, and Vanessa Angel. So maybe with a huge cast like that it’ll be a poor man’s Valentine’s Day, but — hopefully — funny and/or not horrible.
