10 Pret A Manger Nutrition Facts

Publish date: 2024-07-05
Source: Newyorker.com

Pret A Manger is a popular fast-casual chain known for its diverse menu options and emphasis on fresh, high-quality ingredients. Whether you’re grabbing a quick bite on your lunch break or looking for a nutritious meal on-the-go, Pret A Manger has you covered. In this article, we will delve into the nutrition facts of some of Pret A Manger’s most popular menu items. From sandwiches and salads to soups and snacks, we will break down the calorie count, macronutrient profile, and key vitamins and minerals found in each item. So, if you’re curious about the nutritional content of your favorite Pret A Manger dishes, grab a cup of coffee and let’s dig in!

Avocado & Egg Protein Pot

The Avocado & Egg Protein Pot from Pret A Manger is a nutritious option packed with protein and healthy fats. It contains 18 grams of protein and is made with sliced avocado, boiled free-range egg, cherry tomatoes, and a sprinkle of black pepper. It makes for a satisfying and nutritious breakfast or snack.

Smoked Salmon & Soft Cheese Sandwich

Pret A Manger’s Smoked Salmon & Soft Cheese Sandwich offers a delicious and nutritious option for lunch. Made with smoked Scottish salmon, cream cheese, cucumber, and a sprinkle of dill, this sandwich is not only tasty but also a good source of omega-3 fatty acids and protein.

Super Greens & Reds Salad

The Super Greens & Reds Salad is a nutrient-packed option that contains a variety of superfoods. Made with baby spinach, roasted beets, broccoli, mixed greens, avocado, pomegranate seeds, and a tangy vinaigrette dressing, it is rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals.

Veggie Box

The Veggie Box from Pret A Manger is a wholesome and satisfying meal option. It includes a mix of roasted vegetables, falafel, hummus, and a side of quinoa. Packed with fiber, protein, and essential nutrients, it is a great choice for those following a vegetarian or vegan diet.

Chicken and Avocado Flatbread

Pret A Manger’s Chicken and Avocado Flatbread is a flavorful and balanced option. It features grilled chicken breast, sliced avocado, mixed salad leaves, and a light dressing, all wrapped in a soft flatbread. This protein-rich wrap is perfect for a quick and satisfying lunch.

Coconut Porridge

Start your day with the delicious and nutritious Coconut Porridge from Pret A Manger. Made with jumbo oats, coconut milk, and topped with a sprinkle of toasted coconut flakes, this creamy porridge is a great source of fiber and provides sustained energy throughout the morning.

Posh Cheddar & Pickle Baguette

The Posh Cheddar & Pickle Baguette is a classic choice for cheese lovers. It features mature cheddar cheese, tangy pickle, and a mixed salad with Pret’s homemade vinaigrette, all packed into a freshly baked baguette. It’s a satisfying option for a quick and tasty lunch.

Mango Coconut Chia Pot

The Mango Coconut Chia Pot is a delicious and nutritious snack option. It contains layers of creamy coconut chia pudding and sweet mango compote, topped with a sprinkle of toasted coconut flakes. This dairy-free and gluten-free treat is packed with fiber, omega-3 fatty acids, and vitamins.

Pret’s Tuna Nicoise Salad

If you’re looking for a protein-packed salad, Pret’s Tuna Nicoise Salad is a great choice. It features flaked tuna, boiled free-range egg, green beans, cherry tomatoes, olives, mixed salad leaves, and a tangy lemon dressing. This salad is not only delicious but also a good source of omega-3 fatty acids and vitamins.

Classic Super Club Sandwich

The Classic Super Club Sandwich from Pret A Manger is a satisfying and flavorful choice. Made with roasted turkey breast, maple-cured bacon, Emmental cheese, tomatoes, mixed salad leaves, and mayo, all stacked between toasted malted bread, it’s a delicious option for lunch or a hearty snack.

FAQs

1. Does Pret A Manger offer vegetarian and vegan options?

Yes, Pret A Manger offers a variety of vegetarian and vegan options. They have delicious plant-based sandwiches, wraps, salads, and soups that cater to different dietary preferences.

2. Are Pret A Manger’s products free from artificial additives?

Yes, Pret A Manger takes pride in using natural and fresh ingredients in their products. They avoid artificial additives, preservatives, and flavor enhancers.

3. Can I find gluten-free options at Pret A Manger?

Yes, Pret A Manger offers a range of gluten-free options, including sandwiches wrapped in lettuce, gluten-free bread, and salads that are free from gluten-containing ingredients.

4. Are the nutrition facts available for all Pret A Manger products?

Yes, Pret A Manger provides detailed nutrition information for all their products. You can find these facts on their website, menu boards, or by scanning the QR codes available in-store.

5. Can I customize my order to suit my dietary needs?

Absolutely! Pret A Manger offers customization options, allowing you to adapt their products to your specific dietary needs or preferences. You can choose to add or remove ingredients to create a meal that suits you.

6. Does Pret A Manger offer low-calorie options?

Yes, Pret A Manger has a range of low-calorie options, including salads, lighter sandwiches, and soups. These options are perfect for those looking to watch their calorie intake while enjoying a delicious meal.

7. Is the nutritional content of Pret A Manger products regularly updated?

Yes, Pret A Manger is committed to providing accurate and up-to-date nutritional information. They regularly review and update their menu and nutrition facts to ensure they are providing the most current information.

8. Does Pret A Manger use sustainable and ethically sourced ingredients?

Yes, Pret A Manger is dedicated to using sustainably and ethically sourced ingredients. They work with trusted suppliers and farmers who meet their strict quality and sustainability standards.

9. Are Pret A Manger products suitable for those with food allergies?

Pret A Manger is aware of common allergens and takes steps to minimize cross-contamination. However, they cannot guarantee that their products are completely free from allergens. It’s always advisable to check the ingredients and allergen information before making a purchase.

10. How can I find the nearest Pret A Manger store?

You can easily find the nearest Pret A Manger store by using their store locator feature on their website. Simply enter your location, and it will provide the closest stores along with their addresses and opening hours.
