Notable ladies in T-Mobile commercials

The best thing about T-Mobile commercials is that, in most cases, they feature actors and actresses with whom people are already familiar. These performers humorously express their points, and various notable women have been part of the adverts. How many of the actresses do you know?

Carly Foulkes

Carly Foulkes' commercials are fantastic. She’s featured in the telecommunication company’s myTouch 4G TV advertisements. She adorns a pink dress in most of them and takes the role of a T-Mobile spokeswoman quite well.

Carly is an acclaimed actress and model. She gained fame by presenting T-Mobile commercials. Foulkes is renowned for her movies like Momentary, Halfway Somewhere and Ugh!.

Valeska Castillo

Valeska is popular in the T-Mobile commercial campaign in Spanish. She is also a respected actress who was born in Nicaragua but raised in the United States of America. Valeska entered the entertainment industry as a model, starting in Miami when she was sixteen. She has appeared in movies like Burn Notice, The Last Resort, The Glade, and Bachelor Part 2: The Last Temptation.
