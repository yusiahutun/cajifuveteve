Priyanka Chopra Wears Feathered Dress for Bachelorette Party

Publish date: 2024-04-21

Priyanka Chopra isn't playing around with her bridal style. The actress hosted her bachelorette party over the weekend, and she pulled out all the stops for her big night out.

Chopra absolutely stunned in a white dress and matching duster by Georges Chakra, covered in beads, sequins, and feathers over nude fabric. Although the figure-fitting midi dress fell to her thighs, the duster cascaded down to the floor like a curtain of feathers.

Priyanka took to Instagram to flaunt the luxe look, which she completed with white pumps and a snowy faux fur coat by Boss. "Featherweight Champion of the world... #Bachelorette #FauxNotFur," she wrote in the caption.

Meanwhile, Chopra's pals, including future sister-in-law Sophie Turner, wore red and black.

Turner even gushed over Chopra's look on her Instagram story. "Bride goals," she captioned one of the photos. The Game of Thrones star is engaged to Joe Jonas, older brother of Priyanka's fiancé Nick.

White feathers are a recurring motif in Chopra's bridal wardrobe. Earlier in the bachelorette celebrations, the actress wore a sweater dress covered in feathers, which she wore with matching socks, snakeskin boots, a cream coat, oversized square glasses, and a baby blue handbag.

And during her Breakfast at Tiffany's-inspired bridal shower in NYC last week, she wore a strapless gown by Marchesa with a skirt fully covered in feathers. She wore nude Christian Louboutin pumps and, of course, Tiffany & Co. jewelry for the fête at the Blue Box Café.

Gotham//Getty Images

If Chopra's pre-wedding outfits are this stunning, we can only imagine how epic her actual bridal look on the day of the wedding will be.
