One is sure to face ups and downs in the journey of life. So, naturally, people seek a partner to accompany them in both happy and terrible times.

Orange Is the New Black star Dascha Polanco is one of those celebrities who has experienced the roller-coaster ride especially related to her boyfriend. 

Let’s find out more about her companion.

Dascha is currently not seeing anyone. But, she was in a relationship with Xtassy, a music producer. 

There is no reliable information about how he and Xtassy met.

Besides this relationship, she has also struggled since a young age and raised her children without a boyfriend.

Dascha Polanco’s Struggle On Raising Children!

Did you know that the thirty-nine-year-old Dascha Polanco got pregnant at the very early age of eighteen?

In an interview with Cosmopolitan magazine in 2015, Polanco revealed her hardship when she got pregnant and how her boyfriend ran away from his responsibility.

She also talked about weight loss and revealed how her physique started transforming after conceiving a baby.

Dascha gave birth to her daughter Dasany Kristal Gonzalez in 2000 when she was a freshman at Hunter College. Later, Dasany portrayed the character of young Daya in Orange is the New Black.

Similarly, in an interview with Latina magazine in 2015, Polanco talked about how she struggled to raise her daughter as a single mother and her battle with depression following her mother's death.

As the new cover star of the June/July 2015 issue, Polanco said-

“I went through a large depression and started feeling emotionally isolated. But because my parents taught me that God is going to send you obstacles and you have to overcome them, it put me at ease. It's very interesting, the times I've felt that I can't breathe emotionally, and I react to things in a very impulsive manner – where it's anger or crying. And being able to speak to somebody about that was kind of a revelation that it was okay for me to feel depressed.”

She continued-

“I remember having to find somebody to stay with my daughter because I didn't want to stop school, and I couldn't stop working. It was raining, and I was like, "Who can I trust that is not my mom?" I realized you have to put your armour on; you have to become a warrior, because family is not there.”

Polanco then conceived a son named Aryam. Polanco says that her two kids have two different fathers. In an interview with Cosmopolitan Magazine in June 2015, she explained-

"I used to feel self-conscious about the fact that my kids have two different fathers. Being a statistic was something that was so negative to me. But now I'm like, 'Hey, listen, it is what it is. It didn't work out the first time; it didn't work out the second time. I have two kids who have two different baby fathers.'"

Polanco and her son’s father were engaged for around six years and ended their engaged relationship around two years ago. Polanco is believed to be the mother of three children. However, there is no information about her third baby.

Apart from her messy life, she has focused on her career more. 

More About Dascha Polanco:

A Dominican-American actress Dascha Polanco was born on December 3, 1982, to a mechanic father and a cosmetologist mother. She grew up in Sunset Park, Brooklyn, New York, with her siblings.

Polanco began her acting career in 2011 and has appeared in not so many films and television series. Polanco’s film credits include Gimme Shelter (2013), The Cobbler (2014), Joy (2015), and The Perfect Match (2016).

Likewise, she has appeared in television series like Unforgettable (2011), NYC 22 (2012), and Orange Is the New Black (2013-present). The mother of three, Dascha Polanco, enjoys a net worth of $5 million from her career.

Currently, Dasha is balancing her personal and professional life. She will be seen more in the coming days. Till then, stay tuned for more updates on her.
