Dylan Sprouse and Barbara Palvin secretly tie the knot just weeks after engagement

Publish date: 2024-08-24

Dylan Sprouse and Barbara Palvin secretly got married in a beautiful Hungarian wedding just weeks after they confirmed their engagement.

The Riverdale TV actor and Hungarian model are officially husband and wife! On Monday, a local Hungarian outlet published a report about the celebrity couple’s wedding and shared several pictures from the intimate ceremony. The exciting news comes a month after Dylan and Barbara announced their engagement.

Barbara Palvin and Dylan Sprouse are married

As reported by the local outlet Bors Online, Dylan and Barbara got married in the model’s home country in a chapel near Budapest, Hungary.

Pictures shared on social media site Twitter show Barbara in a white satin wedding gown with a long veil while Dylan sported a classic black suit with a white flower accessory.

As per one of the pictures in the chapel, presumably taken by one of the guests, Dylan’s twin brother and Hollywood star Cole can be seen as his best man next to the marriage officiant.

Dylan and Barbara announced engagement in June

The local report wasn’t the only giveaway about Dylan and Barbara’s wedding. Cole’s girlfriend model Ari Fournier shared a string of pictures from Budapest on her Instagram Stories, though there were no wedding pictures with the bride and groom.

One of Ari’s pictures was taken at Four Seasons Hotel Budapest, while another snap showed Dylan and Cole together, followed by the cheeky caption “family trip”.

The wedding news comes only a month after Dylan and Barbara publicly announced their engagement. The Riverdale star popped the coveted question back in September 2022.

The model and Hercules actress confirmed the engagement with a collaboration video with Vogue Magazine of the pair taking a trip to Tiffany and Co.

Fans congratulate couple on wedding news

As many fans patiently await official pictures of the couple’s dreamy wedding, many of their fans shared congratulrary messages and filled Twitter with their reactions.

“Wishing them all happiness,” one fan wrote. “They dated for 5 years, they have one of the most mature and healthy relationship out there fan wrote. “

“What a beautiful couple,” another one said. “Happy for them,” someone else added.

“Finally some good news in my feed after I saw three celebrity divorces so far,” referring to divorce reports surrounding Ariana Grande and Dalton Gomez.
