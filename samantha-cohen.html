Samantha Cohen – Career

Samantha Cohen is a famous American model. She is well known as the daughter of Michael Cohen. Michael is an American disbarred lawyer who served as an attorney for U.S. President Donald Trump from 2006 to 2018.

Hence, he was a vice president of the Trump Organization. Further, he also was the personal counsel to Trump.

He was often described by the media as Trump’s “fixer”. Furthermore, he served as co-president of Trump Entertainment and was a board member of the Eric Trump Foundation.

The foundation is a children’s health charity. Likewise, Cohen was deputy finance chairman of the Republican National Committee, from 2017 to 2018.

Moreover, he was sentenced to three years in federal prison and ordered to pay a $50,000 fine after pleading guilty to tax evasion and campaign finance violations.

Then, on February 26, 2019, he was officially disbarred by the New York Supreme Court, Appellate Division.

Later, he reported to the federal prison near Otisville, New York, on May 6, 2019. Furthermore, he was released from prison early due to concerns regarding COVID-19 on May 21, 2020.

Similarly, Cohen was observed dining at a Manhattan restaurant and was taken back into federal custody after refusing to agree to conditions of home confinement.

Nonetheless, he filed suit complaining his re-arrest was an attempt to prevent him from releasing a tell-all book about Donald Trump titled Disloyal: A Memoir.
