Last Shadow Puppets Announce European Tour

Publish date: 2024-09-09

LONDON (VIP-NEWS) — Following their surprise appearance on The Park Stage at last weekend's Glastonbury Festival appearance, The Last Shadow Puppets – that's Alex Turner and Miles Kane – have announced details of their upcoming tour.

The Last Shadow Puppets will be performing their debut live shows in August, followed by their first European and American shows in October and November.

The band will be joined by live musicians James Ford (drums), John Ashton (keyboards) and Stephen Fretwell (bass) and will be augmented by a full 16 piece orchestra at each show.

Tour dates:

August 19 – Portsmouth, Guildhall
August 19 – Oxford, New Theatre
August 19 – Leeds Festival
August 19 – Reading Festival
August 19 – Paris, Olympia
October 15 – Stockholm, Circus
October 15 – Copenhagen, Vega Main Hall
October 15 – Berlin, Tempodrome
October 15 – Brussels, Cirque Royale
October 15 – Amsterdam, Paradiso
October 15 – Glasgow, Academy
October 15 – Sheffield, City Hall
October 15 – London, Hammersmith Apollo
October 15 – New York, Grand Ballroom
November 3 – Mayan Theatre, Los Angeles
