Fall TV's Hottest Eye Candy!

Publish date: 2024-07-26

The fall television season is well underway and there are many gorgeous faces -- familiar and new -- gracing the small screen.

We've already met hunky new "Top Model" judge Rob Evans and "The New Normal's" adorkable Justin Bartha. Tonight, we're going to be treated to two fresh faces: Dean Geyer (above left) on "Glee" and Joanna Krupa (center) on "The Real Housewives of Miami."

Dean hails from Australia, where he competed on their version of "American Idol." He'll play Brody, a upperclassmen at NYADA who sets his eyes on Rachel (Lea Michele).

"Dancing With the Stars" fans may recognize Joanna from her stint on Season 9. Others might know her from her hot modeling career. The Polish-born beauty is one of four new Miami "housewives" brought on for season 2. Nevermind that Joanna isn't from Miami (she grew up in Illinois) or married -- she definitely brings the glamor and drama!
