Meet Navid Ali; Husband Of The Young and the Restless Star Mishael Morgan

Publish date: 2024-04-22

If there's anyone who really got a hold on the ever-stunning Mishael Morgan, then it's Navid Ali, the lucky man who has been married to the actress since 2012.

Melanin enriched skin and a great hairline with a sharp broad face, Navid Ali, a handsome regular guy, currently in his mid-thirties, is often recognized as the spouse of the television actress Mishael Morgan, best known for her appearance in the CBS' soap opera, The Young and the Restless.

Navid Ali; The Special Man In Mishael Morgan's Life

It's quite impressive how the Canadian tv star, Mishael Morgan, once a struggling actress, never let anything, especially stuff considering her career, get in the ways between her and her beau. The Trinidadian beauty reportedly met the love of her life, the only man she has ever loved so truly, aka Navid, way before she made it to the tv film industry.

Navid Ali on his wedding with wife, Mishael Morgan. SOURCE: Mishael Morgan Instagram

And, by the grace of God, everything went just fine. Not after briefly dating and getting to know each other, Ali, made the big move to settle down in life with his then-girlfriend and got on his knees to propose. Of course, the She's the Mayor actress happily said 'Yes.'

Speaking of their wedding, it only took in May 2012 as their presence was highly blessed by their parents, close friends, and relatives. The alum of the University of Ottawa often likes to share the pictures from their special day on the auspicious occasion such as their anniversary.

Mishael looked absolutely thrilling in a strapless white bridal gown accessorized by white matching diamond earrings for her man. Her groom, likewise, looked no less handsome in matching white shirts.

Responsible Father Of Two Kids Shared With Morgan

It seems as Mishael wanted to wait a little longer to start a family of her own to further enhance her career in the acting field. And, it also appears as her husband fully supported the actress. The couple only welcomed their first child in 2015.

Happy family of four of Navid Ali and Mishael Morgan. SOURCE: Pinterest

Niam Ali, Navid and Mishael's son entered their world on 9th August 2015. And, can we talk about the privilege of being born to a successful actress as Niam's baby shower was hosted by actress Christel Khalil who co-starred his mother in The Young and the Restless.

Likewise, making a family of four, Navid Ali became a father for the second time in November 2019 when his wife gave birth to their beautiful angelic daughter, Naliyah Ali. By this time, you must have noticed that both of their children are, interestingly, named after the first letter of Navid's name.

Adorable pictures of Niam and Naliyah can be often seen on Mishael's Instagram feed. The happy mother who holds a strong belief in Christianity also baptized her children as per the religion. Similarly, Navid, who is believed to be half Pakistani and half Guyanese also follows the same faith. His name in Persian translates to 'best wishes.'

Navid and Mishael's children: Niam and Naliyah Ali were both baptized. SOURCE: Mishael Morgan Instagram
