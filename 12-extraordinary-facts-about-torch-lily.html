FAQs

Q: How tall does Torch Lily grow?

A: Torch Lily can grow to be anywhere from 2 to 6 feet tall, depending on the specific cultivar and growing conditions.

Q: When is the best time to plant Torch Lily?

A: The ideal time to plant Torch Lily is in the spring or fall, as these seasons provide the best conditions for root establishment.

Q: How often should I water Torch Lily?

A: Torch Lily requires regular watering, especially during the hot summer months. However, it is important not to overwater, as this can lead to root rot.

Q: Can Torch Lily tolerate frost?

A: Some Torch Lily varieties, such as Kniphofia uvaria, are frost-tolerant. However, it is always best to provide winter protection, such as mulching, to ensure their survival in colder climates.
