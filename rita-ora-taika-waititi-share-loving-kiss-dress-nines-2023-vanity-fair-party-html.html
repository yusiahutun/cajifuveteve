Rita Ora and Taika Waititi share a loving kiss as they dress to the nines at 2023 Vanity Fair party

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Rita Ora and husband, Taika Waititi, shared a romantic kiss as the couple stylishly attended the 2023 Vanity Fair Oscars party in Beverly Hills on Sunday. 

The You Only Love Me hitmaker, 32, was an ethereal beauty in bridal white, corseted gown with a full tulle skirt, while the filmmaker, 47, was dapper in a tailored black suit.

The two lovebirds, who publicly confirmed in January that they had officially tied the knot last year, stopped by the star-studded CAA pre-Oscars event earlier Friday ahead of Hollywood's biggest night. 

The British-born singer put on a breathtaking display upon arriving at the Wallis Anneberg Center to join other A-listers in the industry, such as Sarah Paulson, Naomi Watts, as well as Chrissy Teigen and John Legend. 

The strapless white corset portion of the dress was securely fastened in the back, and molded to Rita's toned frame.

The bottom portion was a full, white skirt that was made of strands of tulle that were connected to the corset with silver hoops for an edgy flare. 

She accessorized the dress with an eye-catching, diamond necklace that wrapped around her neck and also contained green emeralds for a pop of color. 

The Fifty Shades Of Grey actress further glammed up her look by adding a pair of silver, dangly earrings that sparkled under the bright lights.

Silver bracelets were worn on her left wrist for a finishing touch to her extravagant jewelry for the special night. 

Her curly brunette locks were parted in the middle, and combed back to effortlessly fall down behind her. 

The singer's makeup was classically done for the event, and comprised of a layer of black mascara and liner as well as a shimmering silver shadow around her eyes. 

A rosy pink blush and contour was added to better accentuate her cheekbones, while darker, nude-colored matte tint was worn on her lips for a bold touch. 

The beauty turned her back towards the flashing cameras to showcase the corseted, prince-styled gown she had donned for the glitzy affair.
