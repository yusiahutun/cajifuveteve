Maisie Smith launches fabulous Abbott Lyon collection and these are the best pieces

Publish date: 2024-08-18

ABBOTT LYON has a vast array of stunning jewellery pieces to shop for every occasion, and the brand often collaborates with celebrities.

Abbott Lyon has previously teamed up with Olivia Attwood, Stacey Solomon, and Love Island's Gemma Owen, but has now joined forces with EastEnders actress Maisie Smith on an unmissable capsule collection.

  • Shop Maisie Smith x Abbott Lyon collection - buy here

The Maisie Smith x Abbott Lyon collection, also known as the Unique capsule, is available to shop online now at Abbott Lyon.

Speaking about her latest venture, Maisie said: "I am so excited to show you all my brand-new collection with Abbott Lyon. I’ve always loved their jewellery, so it’s been an absolute dream to work with them.

"The collection is full of beautifully personalised jewellery in a whole rainbow of colours, that you can mix and match to celebrate how unique we all are."

READ MORE ABOUT MAISIE SMITH

Abbott Lyon are also chuffed to have Maisie on board, as a spokesperson for the brand added: "As the sun begins to make an appearance, our customers are starting to think about their summer wardrobes, and we know that jewellery with a pop of colour is going to be the ultimate trend to elevate any summer outfit this year, whether you’re planning poolside pics or simply looking ahead to your staycation styles.

"The ‘Unique’ collection is all about bringing our customers jewellery to match their energy: the personalisation our customers know and love us for, with a kaleidoscope of colours to bring energy and vibrancy.

"We couldn’t imagine anyone better to team up with for this than the effervescent bundle of fun that is Maisie. Loved by fans of all ages for her colourful, quirky style and on-trend TikTok content, Maisie is the perfect partner to kick off our summer season here at Abbott Lyon.”

Maisie's fabulous collection includes necklaces, bracelets, bangles, as well as earrings and rings, plus hair accessories.

The range comes in both silver and gold, so you can match your accessories to your outfit, existing jewellery collection, and your skin tone, as it has been reported those with cool veins best suit silver jewellery, while warmer skin tones are best suited to gold jewellery.

However, you can also mix metals too.

The collection also features enamel charms, which can vary from letters for those wanting to wear their initials on their jewellery, as well as emojis and icons - a great way to add some colour to your outfit.

What we love about Abbott Lyon is not only does it offer stunning accessories but items with affordable price tags too.

The 22-pice collection ranges in price from £65 to £89, and with plenty of items to shop it can be overwhelming knowing what to buy, so we have shared our top favourites you will want to stock up on.

Wear on your own or stack rings, layer necklaces, the styling options are endless.
