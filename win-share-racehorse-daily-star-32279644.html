Win a share in a Racehorse with the Daily Star and RaceShare!

Publish date: 2024-02-20

The Daily Star has teamed up with RaceShare to offer 10 lucky readers 5 shares each in a racehorse called Robusto.

Robusto is a good looking horse, with loads of size and scope. He’s in training with Eve Johnson Houghton following his winter break and is looking ready to run soon.

With a Timeform rating of 91, he’s very much on an upward curve, and is pleasing Eve with how he’s training. The plan is he will start off and hopefully progress through the handicap ranks in 2024.

Robusto was an impressive winner of a Lingfield handicap when last seen, finishing strongly over a mile and a half to win comfortably by two lengths. That was his fourth win of the season in 2023 and he only finished outside of the first three once in five other starts this year.

  • Raceday TV

  • Exclusive monthly show

  • Preview events for the big Festivals

  • Regular video updates from Oli Bell with his insight on the racing world

For your chance to win 5 shares and become an owner of Robusto, simply fill in your details on the form below. Closing date for entries is 23:59pm on Sunday, March 17, 2024.

RaceShare aims to offer a unique racehorse ownership experience to not just existing horse racing enthusiasts, but anybody who wants to see what the sport is all about. They want to bring new fans into racing, and share the excitement and fun being a racehorse owner can deliver.
