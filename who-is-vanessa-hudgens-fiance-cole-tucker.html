Vanessa Hudgens is taking her relationship with Cole Tucker to the next level.

He is a shortstop and outfielder.

MLB-drafted since 2014, the 26-year-old baseball star currently plays as a shortstop and outfielder for the Colorado Rockies. He previously held those positions for the Pittsburgh Pirates from 2019 to 2022.

The athlete also boasts the special skill of switch hitting, where he's able to bat both lefty and righty.

The athlete first encountered Hudgens during a remote meditation session over Zoom, the actress revealed to Entertainment Tonight in April 2021. "Very random, yes," Hudgens told ET's Katie Krause at the time. "Zoom, you've got to love it."
