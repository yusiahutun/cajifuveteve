England player ratings in win vs Scotland Bellingham steals show despite Maguire own goal

Publish date: 2024-01-31

There was nothing but bragging rights on the line on Tuesday evening, but that didn't stop a full-blooded encounter between old rivals England and Scotland.

The Three Lions landed the first blow, as Phil Foden turned the ball home from a Kyle Walker cross, before Jude Bellingham landed the right hook a few minutes later.

The Real Madrid superstar was superb, taking his club form to the international stage, as he curled the ball past Angus Gunn.

Even a Harry Maguire own goal couldn't stop the Bellingham party, and he danced around the Scottish defence to set up Harry Kane for England's third. And as Scotland lick their wounds, Daily Star Sport ranks the English performance in the 3-1 triumph.
