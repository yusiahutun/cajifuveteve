Some of the famous festivals around the world include Caribana. It is a Canadian festival held in Toronto, Ontario every summer. It was invented by Caribbean immigrants to showcase their food, dance and music.

Toronto also hosts the Afrofest, which is the largest free African Music Festival in North America. Other black festivals include New Orleans' Essence Festival, Curlfest, Brooklyn Hip-hop Festival, American Black Film Festival, Afropunk Festival and Odunde Festival.
