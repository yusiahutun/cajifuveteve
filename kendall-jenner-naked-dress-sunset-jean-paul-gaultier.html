Kendall Jenner's Naked Dress Includes a Print of the Female Body

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Kendall Jenner’s latest photos are so striking they need no caption.

On Instagram yesterday, the supermodel shared a series of snaps of her lounging on a beige outdoor couch in a dreamy orange-and-gray dress. Behind her, blades of grass blew in the wind, and past those was the ocean.

In one photo, Jenner is seen looking off to the side while showing off her glowy natural glam and designer dress. In another, she is pictured standing up to showcase the piece’s full print.

Jenner’s dress—one of Jean Paul Gaultier’s statue dresses (similar styles here)—is made from a breezy, stretchy material and has halter straps, off-the-shoulder straps, and cutouts under the bra. The print, in burnt orange and warm gray, resembles the sunset setting behind Jenner, and features an abstract image of a female statue’s bust and torso surrounded by painterly strokes.

The model paired the piece only with two chunky silver huggie hoop earrings and rosy makeup. Her hair was down in a straight blowout, and she didn’t bother with shoes.

Jenner included no caption in the post, nor did she disclose her location. Gaultier left two hearts-on-fire emojis in the comments section.

The model’s beach getaway comes after a hectic New York Fashion Week, which the supermodel opted out of this year. (She has said before that she struggles with anxiety during fashion month.) It’s unclear if she will walk any shows during Paris Fashion Week, which is taking place early next month.
