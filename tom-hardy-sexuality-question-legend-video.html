Tom Hardy responds to sexuality question: 'What are you on about?' – video

At a Toronto film festival press conference for Legend, which stars Tom Hardy as London gangster twins Ronnie and Reggie Kray, a journalist for an LGBT news site asks the actor – in a roundabout way – about his sexuality. Hardy asks in return why the subject is being raised, before cutting off the exchange with a gentle ‘thank you’
