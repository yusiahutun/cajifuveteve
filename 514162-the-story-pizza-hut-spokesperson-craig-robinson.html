The story of the Pizza Hut spokesperson, Craig Robinson

Craig Robinson is a multi-talented celebrity whose career has spanned music, stand-up comedy, and acting. He gained great recognition as a cast member on the sitcom The Office. His humour and infectious personality made Craig a natural fit for Pizza Hut in 2016.

Early life

The comedian was born on October 25th, 1971 (52 years as of 2023) in Chicago, Illinois, United States. His mother, Marian Robinson, was a music teacher, and his father, Fraser Robinson III, worked as a city water plant employee and a Democratic precinct captain.

Craig is the youngest of three siblings. He has an older brother and sister named Chris and Michelle, respectively. His brother is a former basketball coach who has worked at colleges such as Oregon State, Western Kentucky, and Cal State Fullerton.

After graduating high school, the personality furthered his studies at Illinois State University, where he studied music and received a bachelor's degree in music education. He later studied at UCLA, University of California, Los Angeles and received a master's degree in education.

While in college, Craig continued to pursue his interest in comedy, performing at various open mic nights and comedy clubs. During this time, he honed his skills as a comedian and began establishing himself as a performer.

Career beginnings

Before transitioning to stand-up comedy and acting, Craig Robinson taught music at Benjamin Elementary School in Hammond, Indiana and Horace Mann Elementary School in Chicago. He then started taking classes at The Second City in Chicago to improve his stand-up gigs.

Comedy, acting, and commercials

The personality did open-mic sessions in New York, Chicago, and Los Angeles and won regional comedy competitions in his early career. He soon landed minor roles in:

  • Def Comedy Jam season 7, episode 2
  • Late-night TV shows like Jimmy Kimmel Live
  • The Bernie Mac Show
  • Friends
  • Walk Hard: The Dewey Cox Story

Robinson made a breakthrough in 2005 in the role of Darryl Philbin in the American version of the sitcom, The Office. The star was nominated for NAACP Image Award and Screen Actors Guild Award for his portrayal in The Office.

Music career

Robinson has released several songs and collaborated with famous musicians like Snoop Dogg. The star's most popular song is Take Your Pant*es Off. Robinson collaborated in a comedy act with his friend where they performed as musical duosL. Witherspoon & Chucky.

Pizza Hut spokesperson occasionally performs with The Nasty Delicious band. Chris Rob, David Sampson, Ethan Farmer, Asa Watkins, Winston Byrd, Lakecia Benjamin, Reggie Hines, and Bendji Allonce are his bandmates.

The band was featured in an episode of The Office and on the season finale of Last Comic Standing. He was also featured on the Hot Tub Time Machine soundtrack, singing Let's Get It Started and Jessie's Girl.

The commercial actor produced two songs for the film Miss March where he played the role of the hit rapper Phil. He has also performed with his band in Seth Rogen's Hilarity for Charity (2018).
