8 Amazon Holiday Party Dresses Perfect for Any Festivities This Season

Publish date: 2024-06-04

Now that Thanksgiving is over, I’m officially welcoming the holiday season with open arms. But who am I kidding? I’m not going to pretend my Christmas tree wasn’t up well in advance (wink, wink). Even more exciting? It’s finally time to start brainstorming all of your festive ensembles including glitter, velvet, and bows galore. So, keep reading to discover (or should I say unwrap?) eight holiday party dresses from Amazon I’d actually wear this season.

8 Best Holiday Party Dresses

Mirol Plaid Tunic Dress

To me, plaid screams “festive,” so this cozy tunic dress for Mirol is everything and more. It comes in sizes small to extra large and features a scoop neckline, fitted long sleeves, a round hemline, and a comfortable silhouette. My favorite part? This adorable getup includes pockets for keeping your hands warm and storing all of your little belongings. Wear it with sheer tights and knee-high boots for a polished finish.   

Dress the Population Cap-Sleeve A-line Midi Dress

If you’re on the hunt for something a bit more upscale, then consider Dress the Population’s A-line midi dress. Currently 20 percent off, the gown includes a sophisticated plunge neckline, short sleeves, a delicate tulle skirt, and an intricate lace hemline. This is perfect for Christmas galas, holiday fundraisers, and elevated occasions alike. Style it with nude heels and gold jewelry for a truly show-stopping moment.     

Halcyeen Long Sleeve Velvet Wrap Dress

If you wish to forgo the red and green, opt for this sexy velvet wrap dress from Halcyeen. The deep V-neck, body-hugging fit, and triangular bottom work together to create a sultry holiday look. Meanwhile, the velvet material adds just the right amount of lowkey festive cheer. Add in a pair of striking metallic shoes and a matching glitter bag, and you’re good to go.  

BBX Puff-Sleeve Chiffon Midi Dress with Belt

Sweet and fun, this BBX chiffon find is complimented by puff sleeves, dots, a high collar, and a flared bottom. The mesh design is accompanied by an attached slip, meaning you don’t have to worry about this dress being see-through. Not to mention, the BBX midi dress can be worn two ways: with or without the belt. So go ahead and choose between a tailored, cinched-waist look or a flowy, loose fit. Better yet, experiment with both styles.
