Directions

  • Step 1Melt butter in a large straight-sided skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion, and cook, stirring occasionally, until golden brown, 10 to 12 minutes. Add garlic, and cook 1 minute. Sprinkle flour over onion, and cook, whisking, until flour is golden brown, 1 minute.
  • Step 2Slowly whisk in stock and wine. Cook, whisking often, until reduced to 3 cups or until gravy reaches desired thickness, 45 to 50 minutes.
  • Step 3Strain through a fine mesh sieve; discard solids. Stir in parsley, chives, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper.
