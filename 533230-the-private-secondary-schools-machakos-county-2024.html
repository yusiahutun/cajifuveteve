The best private secondary schools in Machakos County in 2024

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Private learning institutions offer high-quality education. Many have excellent facilities that provide a conducive environment for learning. They have competed effectively with their public counterparts, giving them a run for their money. So, what are the best private secondary schools in Machakos County?

What are the best private secondary schools in Machakos County? Private secondary schools produce hardworking and responsible individuals trying to achieve excellence. Here are the private high schools in the county.

Best private secondary schools in Machakos County

Several private secondary schools in Machakos County have stood out because of their impressive performance. However, since they are privately owned, interested individuals can join by expressing their interest via an application form.

1. Machakos Academy Girls High School

Machakos Academy Girls is a Christian secondary school located in Machakos town. It is a girls' boarding and day institution and admits students from across the country. It is one of the best private high schools in Machakos County.

2. Carmel Girls Secondary School

Carmel Girls Secondary School is a girl's boarding school located in Mwala Sub County within Machakos County. During the 2020 KNEC examination, the school scooped the second position in the county with a mean score of 8.5.

3. Solomon High School

Solomon High School is a mixed secondary school located in Athi River, Machakos County. The school began in January 2001 as a pre-school. However, over the years, it expanded to primary, junior secondary and high school.

4. Tumaini International Girls High School

It is a girls' secondary school providing globally competitive education and training to young girls in Kenya. It has a conducive, quiet environment for learning away from town. The school is known for recording impressive results in the KCSE examination.

5. Star Sheikh Academy

Star Sheikh Academy is a girls' boarding school registered by the Ministry of Education. It is part of the Star Sheikh Group of Schools. It is located on the Nairobi –Mombasa road at Athi River a distance of 200 meters from Makadara market.

6. Pope Paul VI Junior Seminary School

Pope Paul VI Junior Seminary Secondary School is located in Central- Machakos near Machakos Town. It is a private mixed-boarding secondary, known for incredible performance in the national examinations.

7. Viraj International Academy

Viraj International Academy is one of the best private schools in Machakos County. It has been posting exemplary performance over the years. It continues to enhance its services and offers a number of co-curricular activities.

8. Tala Satellite High School

Tala Satellite High School is a private mixed day and boarding school. It is situated along the Katine Coffee Factory road in Tala town, Tala Location, Matungulu. It was established in 2012 and has steadily grown.

9. Mwangaza High School Masii

Mwangaza School is a girls' boarding secondary school located in Masii division of the Mwala subcounty. The school has adequate provision of good boarding and day facilities for its students. It is one of the best institutions in the county.

10. Uamani Sky View Secondary School

Uamani Sky View Secondary School is located in Matungulu Sub County. It is a private secondary school. It admits students from across the country.

The school has performed well over the years, posting impressive mean scores in KCSE exams. It is one of the most outstanding in Machakos County.

11. St Philips-Cleavers High School

St Philips-Cleavers High School is a full boarding school. It was established with a vision to become a leading learning institution that embraces prudence and integrity in its management. It is one of the best high schools in Machakos County.

12. Danana Girls Secondary School

Danana Girls Secondary School is located in the Kathiani constituency in Katani. It was established in 2001 with the admission of only five students. It has grown gradually into one of the most outstanding institutions in the county.

13. ABC Katelembo Secondary School

Wrapping up

The best private secondary schools in Machakos County have consistently posted amazing results over the years. These learning institutions are either mixed or single-gender, and some are sponsored by religious institutions.
