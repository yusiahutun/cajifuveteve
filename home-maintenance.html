Rent a Spouse for All Your Household Duties

Publish date: 2024-06-02

Sick of asking your partner to fix the drywall, mow the lawn, fix the leaky sink, take out the garbage, unload the dishwasher, or clean the kitchen? One company is willing to lend you a spouse who will (happily) cross all those things off your to-do list.

Sometimes Spouse, a company with locations all over Texas, will rent you a "husband" or "wife" to help out around the house. You can hire a spouse to house-sit, pet-sit, or clean your home, or even call in someone to handle home repairs, house painting, floor laminate, carpentry, or yard work. You know, all the things you've been asking your real-life spouse to do.

[facebook ]https://www.facebook.com/sometimesspousewaco/photo... data-width="800"[/facebook]

Every stand-in husband and wife from the service is fully insured, background checked, and professionally trained, according to KZTV Action 10 News. You can rent a spouse by the hour, day, or even week for bigger household duties. If you're looking for something more long-term, Sometimes Spouse also offers weekly and monthly plans for house cleaning and home maintenance.

Christy Ogle, the chief executive officer, started the company with her husband, Sterling, and best friend over dinner one night in Waco, Texas. Now, four years later, Sometimes Spouse has more than 40 franchises in Texas and New Mexico. "It's a very unique business model, I love that it's the handyman work and the cleaning work together," Sterling told KZTV Action 10 News.

So if you and your partner are looking to kick back without having to worry about tackling all those chores on your to-do list, you've found your answer. Sit back, relax, and let your rental spouse get to work.
