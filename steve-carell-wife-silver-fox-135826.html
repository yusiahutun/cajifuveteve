Steve Carell Is Truly Living His Best Life as a Silver Fox: My Wife Finally Said She Loves Me!

Publish date: 2024-07-17

Move over George Clooney, there’s another hot silver fox in town!

Steve Carell recently debuted his new gray hair and, frankly, the world just can’t get enough of his handsome look. During a sit-down on [The Tonight Show](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6WGPZmuKL4)_ earlier this week, the 54-year-old actor gushed about his silver fox status — and joked his wife is finally in love with him!

“You’re a studmuffin,” host Jimmy Fallon told the actor, to which he funnily replied, “Shove aside, [George Clooney].”

“Do you get invited to silver fox meetings? Do you go to a club and Anderson Cooper is there? And Andy Cohen?” Jimmy asked The Office star on Tuesday, June 27. “There’s a bracelet you can get,” Steve quipped. “Mine says ‘Steve’ and on the other side of the bracelet, it says, ‘hunk of man meat.'”

Steve on June 21, 2017.

“My wife finally said she’s in love with me… Very excited,” Steve added, referring to his spouse of 22 years, Nancy Carell.

There’s nothing I can do,” the actor joked during an interview with [Entertainment Tonight](http://www.etonline.com/news/220372stevecarellcommentsonhissilverfoxmakeoveritjustgeneticexclusive/) on Saturday, June 24.

“I’m bursting with pride,” he continued. “[The outpouring of support is] very nice.”
