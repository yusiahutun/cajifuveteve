12 biggest celebrity Photoshop fails ever, from Kim Kardashians missing belly button to Kylie Jen

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Photoshop fails show up in various ways, but the most obvious missteps include suspiciously warped furnishings in the vicinity of a celebrity’s curves, elements that are wildly out of proportion – and straight up missing body parts.

Kim K’s absent belly button in a recent swimsuit shot is a good example of the latter, which left fans scratching their heads at the discombobulating visual. Although it’s not the first time the Skims founder has been called out for bad editing, she’s also not the only one. Here are 12 more celebrity Photoshop fails …

The Met Gala 2022’s best and worst dressed stars, from Kim K to SJP

1. Ariana Grande’s weird hand

It’s hard to pinpoint exactly what went wrong with this 2015 photo, but Grande’s gigantic hand seems out of proportion with the rest of her body. The pretty singer and founder of REM beauty has always faced speculation about her physical transformation – with many questioning whether she had surgery at some point – but this photo has just been strangely edited.

2. Miranda Kerr’s wavy carpet

Fans were quick to point out the “curved carpet” in the former Victoria’s Secret model’s ‘gram. She posted the pic for an event in Tokyo back in 2013 looking drop-dead-gorgeous, as usual, but the wavy carpet beside her raised eyebrows since it implied she had edited her figure.

3. Zendaya’s manipulated torso

There are, of course, scenarios where the editing choices are out of a celebrity’s control. Zendaya was quick to oppose the exaggerated waist-nip and thigh slimming in a 2015 Modeliste magazine cover shoot and, being the authentic girl she is, even shared a before-and-after close-up to address unrealistic beauty standards.

4. Kendall Jenner’s wonky door frame

It seems every Kardashian has had their fair share of Photoshop fails, and in Kendall Jenner’s pic, the wonky door frame gives it away. Despite having an incredible figure, it seems the KUWTK star and supermodel just couldn’t resist making her waist even more itty bitty than it already is.

5. Bella Hadid’s curving cupboard

You’d almost miss what went wrong in this pic when distracted by Hadid’s figure. But nothing gets past the social media police, who noticed the distorted bathroom cupboard, pulled inward in the model’s attempt to accentuate her teeny waist.

6. Madonna’s TikToker body

Things got a bit weird in 2021 when Madonna literally plastered her face over another girl’s body. The 63-year-old singer posted the photo when promoting her album “Rebel Heart” but never addressed the fact that she allegedly borrowed TikToker Amelia Goldie’s body – and left fans plainly confused. Goldie called Madonna out, but never got answers or any acknowledgement. It was just so strange – but then again can be expected based on her previous “filter or fillers” rumours.

7. Airbrush galore for Kris Jenner and Gordon Ramsay

It’s OK to age gracefully … without excessive facetune. Although the chef and Kardashian momager both look great already, Jenner is known to blur out the wrinkles – as she did in this 2015 photo. It doesn’t take a genius to notice the OTT editing, but she was confirmed caught when fans saw Ramsay’s version of the picture from a different angle, without the Photoshopping.

8. Britney Spears’ warped chest of drawers

The pop sensation recently ended her conservatorship and is confirmed pregnant again, but her Instagram page is still rather … bizarre. When it comes to Photoshop fails, her recreation of the iconic Baby, One More Time music video look was not so iconic with its blatantly obvious warped waist resulting in the chest of drawers behind taking on a new shape too.

9. Beyoncé’s added thigh gap

You’d think Beyoncé wouldn’t need the extra touch up with her extremely fit physique, but the Crazy In Love singer has also fallen victim to the occasional poor edit. Fans were fast to notice the telltale staircase, which took the fall for her inserted thigh gap in 2014.

10. John Mayer’s cartoonlike selfie

Guys are also very much guilty of editing their pics. Although some fans believe Mayer was just being ironic in his extremely facetuned selfie from 2014, others weren’t sure and were left baffled by his choice of filter. We’ll leave it to you to determine the verdict.

11. Justin Bieber’s Calvin Klein campaign

When the before and after images of Justin Bieber’s #mycalvins promotion in 2015 came out, many were stunned with the amount of airbrushing and body enhancements added to the hero shot. Then again, who can be surprised at this point? No sorry expected.
