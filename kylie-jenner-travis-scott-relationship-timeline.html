Kylie Jenner and Travis Scotts Complete Relationship Timeline

Publish date: 2024-02-13

They say that the strongest relationships often blossom organically, unexpectedly, and seemingly out of nowhere—and for Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott, this has indubitably been the case.

At multiple points, the mogul and the rapper’s unlikely relationship has lacked a definitive title, though it’s evident that something special has always existed between the two. Since 2017, Jenner and Scott’s larger-than-life romance has amounted to more than just extravagant gifts and gestures (like their matching Lamborghinis and Scott’s bed of roses, for two), but also a slew of iconic appearances, and a universally adored five-year-old daughter and one-year-old son.

In January 2023, the two called off their status as a romantic item. Still, the co-parents continue to demonstrate the extent of their mutual support through thick and thin—including through breakup and makeup—and the ways in which a committed, loyal bond can transcend all the labels.

Below, see the highlights from Jenner and Scott’s mostly romantic co-parenting relationship, and continue to watch this space for updates.

June 2023: They are not working on getting back together.

Jenner and Scott’s on-again, off-again romance is remaining off for now. Despite fan speculation that the exes may be reconciling, TMZ reports that they are only interested in co-parenting their two kids, Stormi and Aire. The children live full-time with the makeup mogul.

The outlet also reports that while Jenner and Scott have gotten back together in the past, this breakup “feels more permanent,” and “they’re not working on getting back together.”

The news comes after Jenner and Scott were recently spotted attending their daughter’s pre-kindergarten graduation, and amid Jenner’s secretive budding romance with actor Timothée Chalamet.

April 2023: Scott posts a flirty comment on one of Jenner’s Instagram posts.

When the beauty mogul shared behind-the-scenes photos of a campaign shoot for her newest cosmetic product, Kylash, her ex left a supportive comment on the Instagram post, simply writing, “A beauty.”

The sweet comment left fans wondering if the two had reconciled, but a source for Us Weekly reveals that’s not the case.

“Kylie thought it was really sweet of Travis to show his support for her in such a public way,” the insider said. “That’s just who Travis is though. Even when they were a couple, he was never shy about showing big displays of affection and love.”

They added, “Even though they’re not together, they have an amazing bond and are still best friends. They have an open line of communication and a remarkable coparenting relationship. They’ve always made sure their kids know that they’re both there for them at all times and that will never change. Everybody knows that Kylie and Travis have had their ups and downs over the years, but they have deep respect and admiration for each other no matter what.”

January 11, 2023: It “is probably not the end” for Jenner and Scott.

While a source confirms to People that Jenner “is not with Travis right now,” they add that a reconciliation may still be on the horizon.

“She spent the holidays in Aspen with her family,” the insider tells the outlet. “They have split several times in the past and this is probably not the end of their relationship.”

The two reportedly have different priorities. The source explains, “Kylie is very focused on her kids and her business. She is not a big partier. Travis is the opposite. He likes to party. They definitely have different focuses. They always did.”

It has “never been an easy relationship,” and the source says Jenner “flips out” when cheating rumors emerge.

“There is a reason that they never got married,” the source continues. “It’s always been very up and down. And they have never even lived together. They have always had separate homes.”

January 7, 2023: They reportedly break up after spending the holidays apart.

The pair have split up, with US Weekly reporting that they called it quits around the time Jenner took their daughter Stormi to spend the holidays with her sister Kendall and her friends Hailey and Justin Bieber in Aspen.

"Kylie and Travis are off again, they were supposed to spend the holidays together, but she went to Aspen to be with her family and friends up there," the insider says. "This has happened so many times before, they're known to be on again off again, but always remain friends and great coparents."

The split comes nearly three years after the couple rekindled their romance in February 2020, and after they welcomed their second child together.

November 28, 2022: Jenner shares a rare close-up of their baby son.

Jenner offers an intimate glimpse into her family life with Scott via a post on Instagram featuring photos of her with the rapper and their two kids. The carousel includes one sweet shot of Jenner holding her baby son while sitting outside on the grass. "Highlights," she simply captions the post.

Instagram

October 2022: Scott denies cheating allegations.

After Instagram model Rojean Kar seemed to hint at a romantic relationship with Scott by posting videos from a music video shoot that he was allegedly on, the rapper denied the allegations on his Instagram Story.

"It’s a lot of weird s**t going on," he wrote. "An uninvited person was sneaking photos on what was supposed to be a closed set while I was directing a video. I’m saying this for the last time. I don’t know this person. I’ve never been with this person. So please stop with the continuous cyber games and the fictional storytelling."

He additionally posted a photo from his and Jenner's Valentine's Day celebration this year, writing, "If u wasn’t at this table on V day then u wasn’t with me."

Afterwards, Kar challenged Scott's claim that he doesn't know her.

"Okay, so what we’re not going to do is we’re not going to lie on me because…I’ve been good. I posted whatever f*****g Story you guys wanted me to post. I pretended I didn’t know you, went along with whatever f*****g narrative you guys wanted to no matter how much b******t I got from it," she said in a video captured by The Shade Room, per Entertainment Tonight.

"But to say you don’t know me and you’ve never been with me, when you’ve definitely been with me, when everyone’s seen you with me, when I have pictures and videos of you with me…c’mon. C’mon, sir," she added. "Even this Valentine’s Day, I saw you, I ran out the door, and you had every single girl I know blowing me up, like 'Trav‘s asking for you, come back.' Are we pretending that didn’t happen too? You cheat on that b***h every single night. The whole f*****g city sees it."

August 10, 2022: Scott celebrates Jenner's birthday.

Scott shares a sweet birthday tribute to Jenner on her 25th birthday. On his Instagram Story, he posts a series of photos and videos of the makeup mogul, writing in separate slides, "happy," "f*ckkkkking," "birthday mi 🤎🤎🤎🤎🤎🤎🤎🤎."

August 9, 2022: They are reportedly "doing fantastic" together.

The two are thriving, new sources report. The pair have been in a great place since giving birth to their second child—per Entertainment Tonight insiders—and are thriving more than ever romantically.

"Things between Kylie and Travis are going super well," a source tells ET. "They have really mastered being together and successfully co-parenting. It's really working for them and they are just living life. They're doing fantastic as parents of two."

The source adds, "Travis is very involved as a father and Kylie loves that. He really makes Kylie and his family a top priority, which is a big reason as to why they work as a couple. He is always going above and beyond as a dad and as a partner and Kylie appreciates his dedication to her and their little ones."

August 7, 2022: They get steamy in London.

Jenner makes a trip to London alongside daughter Stormi to support Scott during his performance in the city's O2 Arena. Jenner later shares a series of mirror shots with the rapper from backstage on Instagram. "Utopia with you," Jenner writes in the caption.

Ricky Vigil M//Getty Images

May 15, 2022: They attend the Billboard Music Awards with Stormi.

The family trio showed out on the red carpet in style at the 2022 Billboard Music Awards in Las Vegas wearing cool, coordinating earthy tones.

Matt Winkelmeyer//Getty Images

February 14, 2022: Scott showers Jenner with roses.

In another grand romantic gesture, the rapper surprises Jenner on Valentine's Day with a bed of dozens of pink and white roses surrounding an oversized pink bear and gingerbread house. Jenner later shares photos of the arrangement on Instagram.

"He is a romantic guy," a source tells People. "[Jenner] was teary-eyed about it. They also had a special dinner."

February 2, 2022: They welcome their second child.

The pair welcome their second child, a baby boy, whose birthday falls just a day after Stormi's. Jenner announces the news on Instagram with a black-and-white photo of the newborn's hand in Stormi's, alongside the caption, "💙 2/2/22."

kylie jenner/instagram

Jenner initially announces that their son's new name is Wolf—but weeks later, writes on her Instagram Stories that she and Scott have had a change of heart and are still deciding on something more fitting. In January 2023, Jenner finally announces their baby son's new name: Aire. She confirms the news on Instagram with a carousel of photos revealing the baby's face for the very first time.

November 2, 2021: Scott gifts Jenner and Stormi matching rings.

The rapper dedicates another gift to the special women in his life: matching rings for Jenner and Stormi. "Daddy got us matching rings 🤍⛈," Jenner writes on Instagram alongside a picture showcasing the new jewelry.

September 7, 2021: Jenner confirms her second pregnancy.

Jenner announces that she and Scott are expecting their second child via a sweet video montage on Instagram, featuring clips of her positive pregnancy test results, the baby's ultrasound, as well as the heartwarming moment that momager Kris Jenner first hears the news.

"Wait a second … are you pregnant?" Kris excitedly says to the mogul in one of the clips. "Stormi, we're gonna have a baby! This is one of the happiest days of my life!"

June 15, 2021: They walk the red carpet as a family.

The pair make a red-carpet appearance with Stormi at the annual Parsons Benefit in New York City.

Craig Barritt//Getty Images

From the looks of the photos and additional reports from sources, the pair seem "fully back on" at this point. "Although they always got along, there is a different energy between them now," an insider said. "Kylie and Travis are very close again. Kylie flew to NYC to support him. It's just great to see them back together."

May 21, 2020: Sources confirm they are dating again.

The two are easing their way back into a romance—per new sources—but going with the flow and prioritizing parenting. "They love each other. They enjoy spending family time with Stormi. They all travel together. Kylie and Travis also have date nights," the insider reveals. "They are not exclusive, but Kylie isn't seeing anyone else, either."

"They are not putting any pressure on their relationship," another source says. "They get along and things are great. They both want to be as present as possible for Stormi. Kylie and Travis have worked hard to create a great family situation."

February 1, 2020: They reunite for Stormi's birthday.

The pair publicly reunite for the first time since their split on parenting duty at StormiWorld 2, their iconic birthday bash for their two-year-old.

October 1, 2019: Sources confirm their temporary split.

The two take a break from their romance after two years of dating, People insiders report. The news comes after TMZ reported back in February that Jenner accused the rapper of cheating, prompting him to cancel his February 28 show in Buffalo, New York.

"They are taking some time but not done," a source close to Jenner says, per People. "They still have some trust issues but their problems have stemmed more from the stress of their lifestyles."

Despite the split, the pair remain on civil, even friendly, terms over the next two years as they continue to co-parent Stormi and coordinate family outings. They still publicly commemorate one another on Instagram for their birthdays (as well as on Mother's Day and Father's Day), and are also seen together on several night outings, including at an Oscars after-party on February 9.

Sometime in 2021, the two get back together, though lines remain blurred on when exactly the turning point took place.

"Even after they split, they always spent a lot of time together," a source later tells People. "They are great co-parents and love hanging out. ... It always seemed possible that they would get back together."

September 13, 2019: They grace the cover of Playboy.

Jenner and Scott pose for a steamy, eight-page spread in Playboy's Pleasure issue, which the rapper also creatively directs.

Throughout the cover interview, they open up about the ways their sex lives have evolved since becoming parents. "A lot of people claim that having a baby can hurt your sex life, but I feel like that's the opposite of our experience," the "Sicko Mode" rapper says in the interview.

Speaking to Scott, Jenner adds, "You remind me that motherhood and sexuality can coexist and just because you embrace your sexuality doesn't mean you have loose morals or you're not a good mother."

The mogul continues, "You can be sexy and still be a badass mom."

April 30, 2019: Jenner shares a heartfelt birthday wish to Scott.

In a romantic message to Scott on his 28th birthday, Jenner calls the rapper her "bestie & hubby" and alludes to the possibility of having another child.

"Watching you evolve into the partner, friend, son, and father you are today has been so fulfilling," she writes in an Instagram post, featuring a series of photos of the couple and their daughter, Stormi. "My real life bestie & hubby all wrapped into one. I love you and I’m so so proud of you. happy happy birthday."

Concluding the message, she writes, "Let's fuck around and have another baby."

February 10, 2019: They attend the Grammys.

The couple make their first awards show appearance as a couple at the 61st Annual Grammy Awards.

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

August 2, 2018: Jenner makes a cameo in Scott's music video.

The rapper features the young billionaire in his music video for "STOP TRYING TO BE GOD," a track from his third studio album, Astroworld.

July 17, 2018: They grace the cover of GQ.

The pair star in GQ's August issue, where they reveal intimate details of their relationship. "I don't even be looking at motherfuckers," Scott told the magazine. "I don't be looking at shit. Kylie actually likes me for me. Man, hell nah. Nervous for what? I'm on my own island. So hey, come over there to Astroworld. I'm not into all the other shit. I don't get involved. I'm over here. Kylie is different."

May 7, 2018: They make their Met Gala debut.

Jenner makes her first major postpartum appearance at the Met Gala with her rapper beau.

HECTOR RETAMAL//Getty Images

April 28, 2018: Jenner goes all out for Scott's birthday.

For the rapper's 27th birthday, which is actually on April 30, the new mom rents out the entire Six Flags park for family and friends to celebrate. "Rented out six flags for baby's birthday," Jenner writes on her Instagram Story alongside a video showcasing one of their many roller coaster rides of the day.

March 23, 2018: They get matching Lamborghinis.

The rapper continues to pamper Jenner—this time, with a bright orange Lamborghini Aventador, which matches with his own model in a darker hue. "Mom & Dad," Jenner captions a shot of their two cars on Instagram.

February 1, 2018: They welcome their first child.

Following six months of heavy speculation, the mogul officially confirms the rumors of her pregnancy with an open letter on Instagram announcing the birth of her and Scott's first child, daughter Stormi Webster.

In what would become an iconic statement, Jenner writes, "My pregnancy was one I chose not to do in front of the world. I knew for myself I needed to prepare for this role of a lifestyle in the most positive, stress free, and healthy way I knew how. There was no gotcha moment, no big paid reveal I had planned. I knew my baby would feel every stress and every emotion so I chose to do it this way for my little life and our happiness.

"Pregnancy has been the most beautiful, empowering, and life changing experience I've had in my entire life and I'm actually going to miss it. I appreciate my friends and especially my family for helping me make this special moment as private as we could. My beautiful and healthy baby girl arrived February 1st and I just couldn't wait to share this blessing. I've never felt love and happiness like this I could burst!"

August 10, 2017: Scott gifts Jenner a diamond necklace.

The rapper invests in Jenner big time with a new dazzling diamond necklace for her 20th birthday. In a low-key celebration, Jenner and Scott go out on a double date with older Kardashian sister Khloé and her then-boyfriend, Tristan Thompson, to see Halle Berry's Kidnap. The couples are also joined by several close friends, including Jenner's (ex) BFF, Jordyn Woods.

A source tells People that the Kardashian family "loves Travis" and has been on board with his romance with Jenner. "Kylie and Travis are doing great," the insider adds. "There's no drama with Travis. He's a cool guy. He treats Kylie with a lot of respect and love. Kylie is very happy."

June 2017: They get matching ink and rings.

The pair immortalize their romance with coordinated ankle tattoos of tiny butterflies, which the two proudly showcase to friends and family at Khloé Kardashian's 33rd birthday bash at TAO in Los Angeles, per a People source. "Their relationship seems to be getting stronger and they're closer than ever," they add.

Later the same month, the two are also spotted wearing matching sparkling bands around their fingers, further symbolizing their growing devotion to one another.

April 25, 2017: They pack on the PDA at the NBA Playoffs.

In an official public appearance later that month, the pair get cuddly and steamy courtside during a Houston Rockets and Oklahoma City Thunder game in Scott's Texas hometown. "They definitely looked like a couple," a People source says, adding that Jenner was "cuddled up" with the rapper and kissing his neck throughout the game.

Bob Levey//Getty Images

April 16, 2017: They hold hands at Coachella.

The pair are first spotted looking like an item at the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival in 2017, during which fans leak photos of the two holding hands. Per a People insider, the hangout is initially part of Jenner's jealousy ploy against Tyga—whom Jenner split from just a month prior—but the two ultimately hit things off for real, so much so that Jenner makes the spontaneous decision following the festival to join Scott on tour.

Later, in 2018, Jenner revealed these beginning stages of their relationship in a joint cover interview with the rapper. "He said, 'I'm going back on tour—what do we want to do about this?' Because we obviously liked each other," the makeup mogul told GQ. "And I was like, 'I guess I'm going with you.' ... And then we rode off into the sunset."
