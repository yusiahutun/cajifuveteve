Zendaya Stuns in a Grand Ball Gown at the L.A. Premiere of &quot;Challengers&quot;

Publish date: 2024-05-19

With her Challengers press tour looks, Zendaya has served up back-to-back aces, smashes, and straight-set wins. On Tuesday, for the last premiere of the tour, she went out with a bang in a Grand Slam champion–worthy look.

For the Los Angeles premiere of her sporty romance film, the Euphoria star arrived at the Regency Village Theatre in Westwood in a grand ball gown with a sexy spin. The Vera Wang masterpiece featured a lingerie corset bodice with thin straps, lace cups and details, defined ribbing, and garter straps, as well as a huge, billowing skirt with a trailing train that completely covered her shoes, composed of layers of slightly sheer, pale pink satin and gauzy black fabric at the center.

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Zendaya allowed her dress to take center stage, opting for minimal jewelry in the form of a glistening diamond ring and matching diamond stud earrings by Bulgari. She went ballerina-chic with her beauty, sweeping her blonde-highlighted curls back into a topknot with soft tendrils framing her face, and opting for pretty, rosy makeup complete with a glossy berry lip, soft pink blush, glowy highlighter, a subtle pink smoky eye, and a classic French manicure.

Eric Charbonneau//Getty Images

Later, the Dune star changed out of her massive princess gown and into something more slinky—returning, after several country-club-chic looks composed of tennis whites, to electric tennis-ball hues. This second piece was a form-fitting highlighter-yellow gown that featured a daringly low V neckline that plunged down to her navel, a halter neckline, a sultry open-back design, a floor-length skirt with a super-high center leg slit, and gathered fabric details that met in the center at a literal neon-green tennis ball.

Zendaya and longtime stylist Law Roach have, unsurprisingly, done it again and again, all tour long, from her vintage white Ralph Lauren dress once worn by Cindy Crawford, to her all-white ensemble that gave off “rich mom” vibes, to her glittery green gown emblazoned with an image of a figure striking a tennis ball, to her tennis-ball-skewered stiletto heels, and everything in between.
