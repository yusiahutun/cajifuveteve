Pregnant Kim Kardashian's edgy photo shoot

Publish date: 2024-03-27

Kim Kardashian got in touch with her edgy side shortly before becoming a mother. While heavily pregnant, the reality star posed for a series of high-fashion snaps for former Vogue editor Carine Roitfeld's CR Fashion Book. On the front cover, Kim is seen posing with the accessory du jour – grills. The sultry close-up sees the new mother parting her mouth slightly to reveal the gold addition to her usual pout.

In another shot, Kim puts her pregnant tummy on display in a puff-sleeved cropped jacket and there is also a snapshot of her sitting in front of a floral wall with her finger in a jar of jam that has been smeared all over her lips. "Pregnancy is something I always love," Carine told Women's Wear Daily  of the images. "It is a precious moment in Kim's pregnancy that people haven't seen. It's about hope and the future and a new baby."

Fashion maestro Karl Lagerfeld shot the pictures of the 32-year-old and the shoot was directed by Givenchy designer Riccardo Tisci. A teaser image of the front cover was released last month, showing the Covergirl wearing boyfriend's Kanye West's favourite bejewelled Maison Martin Margiela mask. Kanye is close friends with Riccardo and Carine.

Kim was delighted with her cover, sharing a shot on Instagram. "OMG, the COVER of Carine Roitfeld's @crfashionbook #issue3!!! Shot by @karllagerfeld! Thank you @riccardotisci17 #Fam #ThisIsCrazy!!!! #Dying!!!" she captioned the snap.Kim recently spoke about regaining her pre-pregnancy body. "As soon as I pop this thing out, I want to do Playboy or some nude shoot," she revealed in a teaser promo clip for Keeping Up With The Kardashians.
