She's usually seen with perfectly blow dried hair as she tames her natural waves to be seen on screen or as she goes about her usual showbiz lifestyle.

But it seems AnnaLynne McCord had enough of taming her locks on Monday as she headed out for a lunch date with a friend.

The 90210 star headed to Urth Caffe in Los Angeles with a girl pal showing off her wild hair.

She's a wild one: AnnaLynne McCord showed off her curly locks as she headed for lunch with a friend without blow drying her hair straight for once

Her locks appeared big and unstyled as she filled up on girly gossip and food.

She even let the ringlets flop in her face as she wore her smart casual ensemble.

The 25-year-old showed off her slim physique in patterned skinny jeans and knee high heeled boots.

Untamed: The 90210 star usually styles her hair straight (seen on the right) but went for the unkempt natural look on Monday afternoon at Urth Caffe

Matching the outfit with a black roll neck and flowing cardigan, she brightened up the ensemble with long coloured beads.

Engrossed in conversation and appearing barefaced she sat outside on an al fresco table as she talked animatedly to her friend.

She then left grinning widely as she put on her sunglasses and carried her brown leather handbag while waving to passersby.

Rest time: She took a break from filming scenes for her hit TV show by joining a girl pal for a spot of al fresco dining

It was a welcome afternoon off for the actress, who has been busy filming her latest scenes for 90210.

AnnaLynne and her castmates are filming well into season five of the hit show, in which she plays antiheroine Naomi Clark.

On the personal life front, AnnaLynne is currently dating Australian actor Dominic Purcell.

Spritely mood: AnnaLynne waved to passersby as she put on her shades and left the restaurant in a good mood

Last month she revealed that despite dating her beau since 2011, she has no plans to get married anytime soon.

She told Us Weekly: 'I am not getting married.

'It's like 2012 was the year for engagements. My two sisters got engaged, [my costar] Shenae Grimes got engaged.

'But I am very happy in my long-term, committed boyfriend-girlfriend relationship.'

Usual appearance: The 25-year-old actress sometimes wears her hair in its natural curl but usually has it styled on the set of 90210
