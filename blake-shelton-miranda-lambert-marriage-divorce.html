Blake Shelton and Miranda Lamberts Divorce

Publish date: 2024-06-03
  • Miranda Lambert and Blake Shelton met in 2005.
  • They married in May 2011, and announced their divorce in July 2015.
  • They've never confirmed why they split, but rumors of infidelity have always circulated.

Long before Blake Shelton and Gwen Stefani’s love story captured the hearts of Voice fans everywhere, Blake and ex-wife Miranda Lambert had the hottest romance in country music.

For a while it seemed as though country’s “it” couple was unstoppable. Together they experienced highs in both their relationship and careers, often collaborating together on hit songs.

But after six years of dating and four years of married life, the couple ultimately called it quits in July 2015. Now, we're looking back at Blake and Miranda’s relationship timeline—and what led to their divorce.

Blake and Miranda met in 2005.

Blake and Miranda had only met in passing before they were paired up to perform the David Frizzell and Shelly West hit “You’re the Reason God Made Oklahoma” during the CMT 100 Greatest Duets special in September 2005. Although Rolling Stone reported that their on-stage chemistry was palpable, Blake was married at the time, to first wife Kaynette Williams.

The country star later copped to his instant spark with Miranda on VH1’s Behind the Music. “I’ve never had that kind of experience with anybody,” he said at the time. “I was a married guy, you know? Looking back on that, I was falling in love with her, right there on stage.”

Tony R. Phipps

Five months later, Blake filed for divorce from Kaynette, but denied rumors that his blossoming relationship with Miranda was the cause. Blake and Miranda went public with their relationship at the 2007 CMT Music Awards, where they posed together for the first time on a red carpet.

They later married in 2011.

Blake proposed to Miranda in May 2010 during a romantic stroll through the woods near Miranda’s Oklahoma ranch.

Christopher Polk/ACMA2011

“Weddings are great and engagements are great and diamonds are especially great,” Miranda told People magazine at the time. “But I’m just excited to be married because I want to tell Blake I really can love him the rest of my life and be happy and make him happy.”

Christopher Polk

The pair wed on May 14, 2011 in a rustic ranch ceremony in Boerne, Texas. Some of country music’s biggest stars were in attendance, including Kelly Clarkson and Reba McEntire, who took the stage to sing at the reception.

Blake and Miranda announced their split in 2015.

After years of denying rumors that their marriage was on the rocks, Blake and Miranda announced their split in July 2015.

Christopher Polk/ACMA2012

"This is not the future we envisioned and it is with heavy hearts that we move forward separately," Blake and Miranda said in a statement at the time. "We are real people, with real lives, with real families, friends and colleagues. Therefore, we kindly ask for privacy and compassion concerning this very personal matter."

So why did Blake and Miranda get a divorce?

While the exes have never commented on the reason for their divorce, rumors have long swirled that cheating—on one or both of their parts—may have played a role. After the couple’s divorce announcement, People magazine refuted reports that Blake had strayed in his marriage, with a source calling him an “incredibly loyal and dedicated husband.”

In April 2018, speculation that Miranda had cheated on Blake picked up steam after he cryptically tweeted about “karma” amid accusations that Miranda had begun dating then-boyfriend Evan Felker while he was still married.

“Been taking the high road for a long time.. I almost gave up,” Blake wrote on Twitter. “But I can finally see something on the horizon up there!! Wait!! Could it be?! Yep!! It’s karma!!”

Blake’s 2016 song “She’s Got a Way with Words,” which takes aim at a cheating ex, also stoked rumors that Miranda had been unfaithful. Neither Blake nor Miranda has commented on the cheating allegations.

Where Are Blake and Miranda now?

In November 2015, Blake’s rep confirmed that he was dating his Voice co-star Gwen Stefani. The news came several months after the country star finalized his divorce from Miranda, and three months after Gwen filed for divorce from ex Gavin Rossdale.

Christopher Polk/E! Entertainment

Blake has said that Gwen “saved my life” during the aftermath of his split from Miranda. "Who else on earth could understand going through a high-profile divorce from another musician? You can't even imagine the similarities in our divorces,” he said in 2016.

Miranda, meanwhile, surprised fans in February 2019 when she revealed that she had secretly married New York City police officer Brendan McLoughlin. In November, Miranda made a rare comment about her split from Blake.

Jackson Lee

"I'm 35. I went through a divorce. I'm thankful that fans allowed me that time to do that," she said. "I'm just going through stuff everybody else goes through."
