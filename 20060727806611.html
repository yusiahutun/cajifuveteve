Caprice launches seductive new lingerie range

Publish date: 2024-04-23

Business-savvy Caprice has launched a new venture 

    Caprice is usually the one doing the modelling, but she brought in some help from a few fellow professionals this week as she launched her latest lingerie collection. Not just a pretty face, the Californian-born beauty is one of a number of business-savvy celebs tapping into the lucrative underwear market.

    The blonde cover girl was in Hamburg to publicise her 'Dessous' collection, which features a host of lacy and seductive designs complete with pretty bows and beads, for German catalogue Otto. 

    Her best-selling By Caprice Lingerie range, which is already sold in the UK, America and South Africa, is both designed and financed by her. "I have a passion for beautiful underwear and for making women feel confident and feminine," explains the model-turned-businesswoman.

    She joins Elle Macpherson, Kylie and J Lo who have all made millions from their underwear labels. With an eye to her future, Caprice has invested much of her hard-earned money into the project and says "This is my life now."
