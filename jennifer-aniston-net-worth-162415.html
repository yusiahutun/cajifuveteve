Jennifer Aniston's Net Worth: Here's How Much the Actress Makes

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Building an incredible fortune! Jennifer Aniston has racked up an impressive net worth thanks to her roles in iconic films and television shows. Keep scrolling to see how much money she makes. 

What Is Jennifer Aniston’s Net Worth? 

Jennifer has an estimated net worth of $320 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. At the peak of her Friends stardom, the sitcom star earned a cool $1 million per episode. But that’s not all! She is still cashing in on residuals from the show years after its successful decade-long run.  

The Just Go With It star is also making bank on the series The Morning Show with Reese Witherspoon which premiered in 2019. She owns the production company Echo Films, the studio behind several movies in which she has starred including Dumplin’ and The Switch. 

Though she is regarded as one of the most in-demand actresses in Hollywood, her start in the entertainment business came with some hesitation from her family. 

“Then Friends came. If there were any naysayers in my family, ‘This will never … you’ll never make a dime,’” she recalled to People in December 2019 about their initial thoughts. “Just watch me. Don’t threaten me that way. God knows now I’m going to make a couple of dimes.”

Is Jennifer Aniston Married? 

The superstar, who frequently vacations in Los Cabos, Mexico, has built quite a massive legacy for herself. Several outlets have estimated that her annual salary is more than $20 million. In 2011, she purchased a $21 million Bel Air mansion. She previously shared the home with her ex-husband Justin Theroux whom she split from in 2018. 

Jennifer was married once before to Brad Pitt from 2000 to 2005. She did not welcome any children during either of her marriages. The California native has been very vocal about her decision to not have children.

“We are complete with or without a mate, with or without a child … that decision is ours and ours alone,” she wrote in a piece published by The Huffington Post in 2016. “We don’t need to be married or mothers to be complete. We get to determine our own ‘happily ever after’ for ourselves.”

The Emmy winner enjoys her life surrounded by her famous friends like Courteney Cox and Jason Bateman and her adorable pups. When asked if she ever planned on slowing down her career in the future, Jennifer got candid. 

“I don’t think so. In fact, I’m about to speed up and not be free for two years,” she said during a December 2021 interview with The Hollywood Reporter. “But I’ll schedule it so that I get breaks in between, and not just go back-to-back to back. You need that time to recharge and be with your friends and your family and your dogs.”
