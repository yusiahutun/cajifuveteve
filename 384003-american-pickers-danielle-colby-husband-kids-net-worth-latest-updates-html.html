Career

Danielle started her career as a professional burlesque dancer. She created Burlesque Le Moustache, a professional troupe that consisted of nine dancers. The group toured eastern Iowa and western Illinois. Later on, she founded the Dannie Diesel's Bump 'N Grind Academy, a school for aspiring burlesque dancers in Chicago.

She has also pursued a career in fashion design, and for a brief period, she operated a retro clothing company known as 4 Miles 2 Memphis. Danielle was a member of the female roller derby team, the Big Mouth Mickies. She stopped her athletic career and stopped participating in the competitions after an injury.
