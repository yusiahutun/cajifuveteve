Rachel Bilson buys Pasadena home for $3.25m

Publish date: 2024-08-01

After splitting from longtime partner Hayden Christensen this summer, it looks like Rachel Bilson is ready for a fresh start.

The 36-year-old Nashville actress has just dropped $3.25 million on a stylish new home in the Pasadena area of Los Angeles.

Located in the upscale and scenic Linda Vista neighborhood the property has six bedrooms and four bathrooms, according to Trulia.
