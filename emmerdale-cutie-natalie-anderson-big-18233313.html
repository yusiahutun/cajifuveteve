Emmerdale cutie Natalie Anderson's big boob secret

Publish date: 2024-02-10

The babe makes her debut this week as Leyla Harding's busty sister Alicia Gallagher.

But we can exclusively reveal Nat needed a little extra help to boost her assets - and stunned her mum in the process.

The Yorkshire lass told us: "I showed my mum one of the first pictures I had taken as Alicia.

"The first thing she noticed was the size of my boobs, which wasn't hard because they're humongous.

"It's a big part of who the character is. Alicia is someone who needs to feel womanly and what better way to do that than show off a great chest.

"So I wear a padded bra with two ‘chicken fillets' sewn into each side. When I first saw them I couldn't believe them either.

"But that really is as far as I'd go.

"So many beautiful young actresses are going under the knife and most of them really don't need to.

"It saddens me when I read about girls having different cosmetic surgery procedures. You just have to love what God gave you."

Nat, 28, who played naughty Nurse Stella Davenport in ITV1's The Royal, has been happily wed to financial adviser James Shepherd for two years.

But the loved-up pair lived apart for almost a year while she starred in hit West End musical Wicked.

So when they got the call saying she had got the part of Alicia, Natalie couldn't wait to come home.

She said: "I've always loved Emmerdale and I can actually see the hills in the opening credits from my house.

"It was hard being away from James while I was in Wicked but he's always been so supportive of everything I do."

While her own domestic life is rosy, the same can't be said for her character Alicia.

Nat said: "Alicia is bound to get judged straight away because of the way she dresses. But there's a lot more to her than meets the eye.

"The sisters hate each other but it's going to be a while before viewers find out why.

"When I read the scripts I was like ‘Oh my God, well I didn't see that one coming!'."
