Who Are Maxx Morando Parents?

Maxx Morando was born to his father Dan Morando and his mother Amy Kaye. In July 2015, his father celebrated their 16th anniversary. So, his parents have been married for at least 20 years. By profession, his father worked in the entertainment industry. He was a supervising producer at ‘The Bachelor’. Dan lived most of his life in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania where he moved in 1993.

Maxx Morando Net Worth

Maxx Morando should have a net worth of less than $150 thousand.

He plays drums in a rock band named Liily. In an interview with 4See magazine, Maxx shared that when he and Sam (guitarist) were trying to think of a band name, it was their friend, and her name was Lily.

Liily is a four-man band composed of Dylan Nash the frontman, guitarist Sam De La Torre, and bassist Charlie Anastasis who are all between the ages of 21 and 23. The band members met each other at music school as kids.

But, they officially formed the band in 2016. As an introductory work, they released an EP titled ‘I Can Fool Anybody In This Town’ in 2019. Morando spoke about their time during the pandemic working on music. He shared, “It made us all focus on improving our creative muscle, and it taught us a lot of valuable lessons about writing”. They worked towards variety and dynamics in their songwriting, while the intense parts became even more potent.

In Liily’s eyes, rock music will thrive when it leaves behind categorization. They released their debut album ‘TV Or Not TV’ which was released on 22 October.

One of the popular songs of the band is ‘TV Or Not TV’ which has over a million views on YouTube. Other popular songs are Anvil, Early Booper, I Am Who I Think You Think I Am, Toro, and Wash among others. When asked about the influence of ‘The Walkmen’ he said, “I think for me… well, our Manager too is Peter Matthew Baeur, he’s in that band.
But I think for me personally the reason I play drums is because of Matt (Barrick), and I think we all enjoy the Walkmen, they’re a great band. Definitely an influence.”

Besides music, he also is also a fashion designer. During an interview with Vogue, his rumored girlfriend Miley had shared that he contributed to her fashion inspired by his cartoon characters.
