Kendall Jenner's Denim-on-Denim Look Includes a Dose of Sideboob

Publish date: 2024-05-15

Leave it to Kendall Jenner to put a risqué spin on all-denim fashion.

The supermodel shared photos from a new Calvin Klein campaign in which she posed in the brand’s fitted denim button-up jacket and a pair of matching high-rise straight-leg jeans. (See all the photos here.)

Jenner upped the ante by going totally topless underneath the denim jacket. In one photo, she slouches the piece down, to hug her around the shoulders. In other snaps, she poses in the ensemble while revealing a sliver of sideboob.

Fans immediately flooded the comments of her Instagram post.

“I’ve never seen denim look that good before,” one user wrote.

“It’s giving ‘your cute jeans’ vibes,” another user added, referencing one of the model’s iconic lines from Keeping Up With the Kardashians.

Gotham//Getty Images

Calvin Klein isn’t the only luxury brand the 818 founder is working with these days.

Jenner recently talked to Bazaar.com about joining L’Oréal Paris as the brand’s newest global ambassador.

“I’m so looking forward to immersing myself in this iconic brand and joining this sisterhood of such strong and powerful women,” she said. “Having the chance to embody the ‘I’m worth it’ mantra and remind women everywhere of their worth is something I am so excited by and don’t take for granted.”

In a statement, L’Oréal Paris global president Delphine Viguier-Hovasse said of the partnership, “At any other time, Kendall Jenner might have been as successful as she is now, but her worth and her choices and her image might have been defined or even controlled by others. But nobody defines Kendall Jenner but Kendall Jenner.”
