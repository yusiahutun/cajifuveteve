Bria Murphy Biography, Age, Height, Husband, Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-09-05

Bria Murphy is an American actress and model. She is well known for her roles in projects like The Escorts, The Perfect Match, Conflict of Interest, The Line, Who The F**k is Uncle Joe, and many others. Along with that, she is the daughter of worldwide-known actor Eddie Murphy. Recently in 2022, she is in the limelight for her wedding to Michael Xavier.

Early Life

Bria Murphy was born on 18th November 1989 in Sacramento, California, United States. The names of her parents are Eddie Murphy and Nicole Mitchell Murphy. She also has many siblings whose names are Eric, Bella, Miles, Shayne, Zola, and Christian. At a young age, she spent quality time with her family and grew up well around them. Bria was homeschooled until ninth grade. Later she went to a private high school in Sacramento where she completed her high school education. Further for her higher studies, she went to a private university to earn a Bachelor’s degree.

Bria Murphy Career

Bria started her career by working as an actor and model. In 2009 she appeared on America’s Next Top Model. Later in 2011, she appeared as herself on The Mo’Nique Show. After 2 years she was seen on Rip the Runway as a Model and on Entertainment tonight as well. In 2011 she was seen as an Angel in TV series named Love That Girl! Next year she was seen on a TV series named Baby Daddy. In 2013 she worked on many projects of TV series, short films, and TV movies.
