Zo Kravitz Is So Y2K in This Sky-Blue Tube Top and Ruched Skirt

Publish date: 2024-09-16

Zoë Kravitz was channeling some serious Y2K vibes when she showed up to support her dad, Lenny Kravitz, at his Hollywood Walk of Fame star ceremony on Tuesday.

For the event, the Big Little Lies actor wore an ensemble styled by Andrew Mukamal from the Saint Laurent Resort ’24 line, which featured a sky-blue bodysuit that looked like a turn-of-the-century tube top. The strapless suit was almost entirely backless, and it connected across Kravitz’s shoulder blades with just a thin strap of material.

As for the bottom half of the ensemble, Kravitz wore a black draped jersey skirt that featured a crossover-style waistline, creating a V along the Batman star’s waist. The ruched fabric added an appealing and elegant contrast to the more casual piece up top.

JOCE/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Rodin Eckenroth//Getty Images

To round out the fit, Kravitz sported a pair of maroon slingback heels, also from Saint Laurent. The sharp-pointed shoes were made of a shiny patent leather, and one side of the straps was composed of a light tan material. The multitalented star added a pair of black oval sunglasses, and accessorized with a diamond pendant necklace and delicate pearl earrings from Jessica McCormack.

Rodin Eckenroth//Getty Images

Kravitz did not attend the event alone. She was accompanied by her fiancé, Channing Tatum, who sat next to her during the ceremony; the two looked so in love as they chatted and laughed throughout the event. Tatum sported an all-black ensemble, with a cardigan buttoned up over a T-shirt, which he paired with matching slacks. He accessorized with a pair of square tortoiseshell sunglasses.
