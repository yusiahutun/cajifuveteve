12 Ways to Wear Slacks Like a Street Style Pro

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Models, actresses, and influencers have been stepping out in outfits with slacks that range from casual to elegant. Trousers have basically become the new jeans in the fashion world, versatile enough to be worn with everything from sweatshirts to cutout tops. Of course, you don't just have to take our word for it. There are plenty of great outfits out there to show you that work pants are back in a new and improved way—and totally ready to clock in whenever you'd like.

Keep scrolling for 12 outfits with slacks that give work pants a whole new meaning.

Try on a Blazer

Ensure your bottoms are far from stuffy or traditional by spicing them up with a statement blazer, like this bright pink number. Apart from experimenting with bold colors, you can also try out oversized silhouettes which will boast an equally fun result.

Get Trendy With a Bracket

Traditionally, wearing slacks was seen as playing it safe. However, this wardrobe staple can work to bring balance to a more risqué style, such as this on-trend bracket look that celebs adore.

Stick to the Basics

When in doubt, pair your cream trousers with a basic, but trendy, white tank. It's an easy combination, but it comes across as sleek and streamlined.

Treat Slacks Like Sweats

Looking to give those joggers a rest? Reach for a pair of slacks instead. Styled with your favorite sweatshirt or hoodie, this is loungewear 2.0.

Play With Prints

Swap out your solid-colored trousers for a pattern one instead. And while you're at it, go ahead and incorporate another print. This look mixes elements from distinct preppy and grunge camps to create a new and refreshing style.

Pop on a Sweater Vest

On paper, slacks and a sweater vest may not sound all that intriguing, but visually, the combination is striking. Be sure to incorporate some color into this grandpa-inspired aesthetic for a youthful spin.

Remember Those Cardigans

Cardigans aren't as effortless as those WFH-friendly crewnecks and hoodies, so maybe they've been MIA in your wardrobe as well. It's beyond time to break one out and style it like a shirt, unbuttoning it to your heart's desire.

Have Fun With Footwear

To be honest, these pants pair well with almost any type of shoe, so if your feet can't handle heels, go with sneakers or lug-soled boots, which will add a rugged, ready-for-anything vibe.

Mix Materials

If your slacks are black or neutral and somewhat simple, you can have fun with what's on top. Take things up a notch and throw on some leather to create a look that's far from predictable.

Enjoy a Colorblock Moment

Don't be afraid to experiment with color and dare yourself to pull an Emily in Paris by combining pieces you never thought would work together. You just might be surprised — and delighted.

Incorporate a Fun Accessory

If you're wearing your trousers with a basic top or tee and don't want to seem too blasé, you can always amp up your look with a colorful accessory.

Don't Overthink It

At the end of the day, you want to wear your slacks in a way that's most comfortable for you, while also sticking with your aesthetic. Sometimes, that simply means throwing on your favorite sweater and boots. It's the most classic pairing and one that looks great on everyone.
