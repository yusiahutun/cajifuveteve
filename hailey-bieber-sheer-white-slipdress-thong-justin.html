Hailey Bieber Nails the Sheer Trend in a See-Through White Slipdress

Publish date: 2024-05-22

This spring, sheer clothing has dominated both the runways and the sidewalks of the world's most fashionable cities. The provocative trend comprises translucent (and, at times, risqué) layers. From gauzy blouses to transparent skirts, the styling options are endless.

Hailey Bieber put forth her own take on sheermania during a low-key outing with husband Justin Bieber in West Hollywood this Sunday. For the date, she stepped out in a see-through white dress with lace trim on the bustier and hem, the latter of which fell right above her knees. Underneath, she wore a matching white bra and thong set. As for her accessories, the Rhode founder kept it classic with black Nike Air Max sneakers and white ankle socks, a black shoulder bag, Child of Wild hoop earrings, a gold chain necklace, a gold nameplate necklace, and black Gucci sunglasses. As usual, she kept her makeup simple with a glazed Rhode lip (her Passionfruit Jelly Peptide Lip Treatment restocks on April 27), and wore her sleek long bob down.

Justin also had some fun styling his look. The "Peaches" singer wore a red, blue, and white tie-dye T-shirt from Lollapalooza 1995. He finished the look off with a pair of baggy blue jeans, a blue-and-white baseball cap, and chunky white sneakers.

The model previously spoke about her attitude when it comes to dressing up for Harper's BAZAAR's September 2022 cover story. "I get photographed so much that I feel like sometimes I put pressure on myself," she said. "Even if I’m just throwing on jeans and a T-shirt, I want it to be a dope pair of jeans and a great T-shirt!"
