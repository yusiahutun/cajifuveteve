Meghan Markle told little Archie he's 'not getting' the camera he wants for Christmas

Publish date: 2024-05-05

Christmas is getting closer and closer, and there’s no doubt kids have been asking their parents for expensive gifts for months. Archie is no different, however, he’s proved even being a Prince doesn’t get you everything you want as his mother Meghan Markle tells him he’s not getting his ‘dream gift’.

Meghan Markle and Prince Harry welcomed their eldest, Prince Archie in May 2019, just one year before they left the establishment. They’ve now moved across the pond to their Montecito mansion, where they then welcomed Princess Lilibet. They’ll definitely be getting nicer weather for Christmas than the royals back home, that’s for sure.

Prince Archie won’t get his dream gift, says Meghan Markle

It looks like the four-year-old is already thinking of a career in photography, as Meghan Markle reveals he asked for a pricey camera as a Christmas gift.

In November, the Duchess took part in a Q&A after a screening for Netflix‘s short film “The After.”

At the event, Meghan revealed her eldest had taken inspiration from Misan Harriman, a celebrated photographer behind some of the couple‘s portraits, including their photoshoot announcing they were expecting a baby girl.

“The inspiration runs deep,” the mom of two shared. “Misan was showing him how to do photography the last time he was with us, and I bought Archie a camera, and he said, ‘But it’s not a Leica like Misan,’” she laughed.

It looks like Archie has inherited a cheeky side from his father!

Despite being so adorable, he can’t get everything he wants, as his mother said: “‘You are not getting a Leica! Not even for Christmas.'” Poor Archie!

The Sussexes release Christmas Card – but their children are nowhere to be seen

Sticking to the royal tradition, Harry and Meghan recently released their Christmas card, however, unlike William and Kate, they decided not to include their children.

Instead, the couple opted for an E-Card, as they deleted social media, with an image of them happily clapping at the Invictus Games.

Prince William and his wife Catherine on the other hand, opted for a family photo with all three kids. However, they may not have gotten the reception they planned, as some fans noticed an ‘awkward Photoshop fail‘.
