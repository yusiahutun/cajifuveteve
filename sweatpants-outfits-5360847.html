14 Sweatpants Outfits Inspired by Celebrities

Publish date: 2024-04-06

The modern-day way to wear sweats is anything but casual. Stars like Hailey Bieber and Jennifer Lopez have been spotted from coast to coast sporting chic sweats—and not just while running errands. From Jada Pinkett Smith's monochromatic 'fit to Dua Lipa's airport look, the trendy sweatpants looks ahead combine style and comfort.

Emily Ratajkowski

Emily Ratajkowski is the queen of making sweats look chic. Here she paired hunter green pants with a graphic tee—the epitome of effortlessly cool.

Sarah Jessica Parker

In character as Carrie Bradshaw on the set of And Just Like That, Sarah Jessica Parker mastered cozy layers over her charcoal-colored sweats.

Hailey Bieber

Tracksuits are definitely back—take it from Mrs. Biebs. There's something about her off-the-shoulder zip-up look that screams, "You don't have to try hard to look chic."

Irina Shayk

Grey sweatpants and a white tank top might not strike you as the typical going-out combo, but model Irina Shayk is here to prove otherwise.

Victoria Beckham

Not even Posh Spice can say no to sweatpants. She elevated her Adidas joggers by wearing them with a tailored coat and white sneakers.

Jada Pinkett Smith

This has to be one of Jada Pinkett Smith's best style choices yet. Not only did she coordinate her elevated sweats with a sleek turtleneck, but she also rounded out the look with a creamy-colored trench.

Priyanka Chopra Jonas

Who needs a top?! Priyanka Chopra Jonas looked stunning in sweatpants and a black sports bra underneath a coatigan while zipping through the airport in Mumbai.

Olivia Culpo

Matching sets took center stage in the last couple of years and they're not slowing down. Olivia Culpo took it one step further and paired her ensemble with a Dior tote and high-top sneakers.

Katie Holmes

Another fan of the graphic tee, Katie Holmes' off-duty look is groutfit (all-grey outfit) perfection—with a little pop of color for good measure.

Sofí­a Vergara

It's official: The wide-leg sweatpants trend is Sofía Vergara-approved. Her white Bottega Veneta crossbody bag is just the right accessory to give her laid-back look a lift.

Rita Ora

Blink and you might miss these sweats on Rita Ora. You heard us right—sweats! Her deconstructed joggers with a denim waist hit just the right spot to show a bit of skin below her colorful one-shoulder top.

Kate Middleton

Everyone loves sweatpants, and royals are no exception. Kate Middleton rocked high-waisted navy joggers while playing a game of walking touch rugby.

Jennifer Lopez

There's nothing like a Birkin bag to make any outfit look instantly more luxe, but JLo's rust-colored set is polished and eye-catching in its own right.

Eva Longoria

Eva Longoria's white-on-white set is so fresh and so clean, it makes us want to wear sweatpants all day, every day.
