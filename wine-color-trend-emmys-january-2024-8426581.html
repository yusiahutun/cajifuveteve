Wine-Colored Dresses Flooded the 2024 Emmys Red Carpet

Publish date: 2024-06-03

From marvelous merlots, brilliant burgundy, and romantic rosés, there were plenty of wine-hued gowns that spilled out onto the red carpet of the 75th Emmy Awards held in Los Angeles.

We found ourselves drunk in love over Rachel Brosnahan's and Taraji P. Henson's Versace gowns rendered in punchy purples. On the more dark, romantic side of the color spectrum, The Crown's Elizabeth Debicki donned a Dior Haute Couture velvet oxblood dress while The White Lotus star Meghann Fahy wore a maroon Armani Prive's creation. Furthermore, Yellowjackets star Juliette Lewis rocked a ravishing ruby sequined Moschino dress, and Selena Gomez followed suit in an Oscar de la Renta dress with sequined swirls.

See ahead for all the wine-colored looks that made a splash on the Emmys 2024 red carpet.

Selena Gomez in Oscar de la Renta

Gomez sizzled in an Oscar de la Renta gown with a sequined swirl pattern that was reportedly comprised of over 450,000 sequins. She continued to shine in Tiffany & Co. jewelry.

Rachel Brosnahan in Versace

The Marvelous Mrs.Maisel star served major star power in a beautiful burgundy Versace gown that featured a corseted bodice.

Elizabeth Debicki in Dior Couture

The Crown star looked absolutely regal in a deep oxblood velvet column dress from the house of Dior.

Meghann Fahy in Armani Privé

The White Lotus actress conveyed a rosy disposition in a wine-colored dress from Armani Privé dress and Bulgari jewelry.

Amy Poehler in Roland Mouret and Simon G. jewelry

Poehler rocked a rich ruby red Roland Mouret dress that was covered head-to-hem in sequins.

Taraji P. Henson in Versace

The actress not only stars in The Color Purple but also looks fantastic in it. Henson paired her look with Alexandre Birman shoes and Simon G. jewelry.

Juliette Lewis in Moschino

Lewis looked ravishing in a ruby red dress designed by Italian brand Moschino. The Yellowjackets star polished off her look with Hanut Singh jewelry.

Christina Applegate in Christian Siriano

Applegate didn't need to show up on the red carpet to leave us all in awe. The actress and advocate had audiences on their feet as she graced the Emmys stage in a custom velvet dress designed by her close friend Christian Siriano.

Kirsten Dunst in Chanel

Dunst donned a dark merlot colored dress on the red carpet from French design house Chanel. The look featured a faint print and subtle draping at the bodice.
