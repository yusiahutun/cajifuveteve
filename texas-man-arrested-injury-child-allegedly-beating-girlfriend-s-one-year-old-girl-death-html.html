Allante Edwards from Texas arrested after allegedly beating girlfriend's daughter to death

Publish date: 2024-07-25

Allante Edwards called police to the home where he was babysitting three children to report an unconscious person

An Irving man is charged with two counts of injury to a child after the one-year-old he was babysitting was beaten to death.

The two other children in his care also sustained injuries consistent with assault. 

Allante Edwards, 19, was watching his girlfriend's three children on Wednesday.

He called emergency services to the home on Rosebud Drive to report someone unconscious.

One-year-old Olivia Cangemi was found unresponsive. The toddler suffered brain swelling, bruising on her forehead and chest, rib fractures and abdominal trauma. She was taken to the hospital and put on life support. 

Her six-month old brother, who is Edward's biological child, and three-year old sister were also injured from an apparent beating.

The boy had bite marks covering his cheek. Edwards tried to pass this off as typical roughhousing.

Jennifer Cangemi posted a photo of Olivia while she was on life support on Facebook writing 'My heart is heavy and a piece of it is laying here with Olivia and will forever be there'

Jennifer Cangemi's (left) boyfriend Allante Edwards (right) said he had been the children's caregiver since January 2

Fox 4 Now reports Edwards told officers the bite marks on the baby boy were 'because that is what you do to kids because they're so cute.'

Police said Edwards has a history of domestic abuse.

Olivia was taken off of life support on Sunday afternoon. The little girl died shortly after and became an organ donor.

Police records showed Edwards had been the children's caregiver since January 2. 

Her mother Jennifer Cangemi told KHOU: '[Olivia] can live on through other children and she helped about three to four other kids.'

Olivia suffered brain swelling, severe bruising, rib fractures and abdominal trauma

'She was the most sweetest, smart, beautiful little girl I have ever seen in my life.' 

On Facebook, Ms Cangemi posted: 'My heart is not just broken its shattered. I love you with all my heart Livy. Please watch over us.'

In another post where she was lying next to Olivia who was on life support, Ms Cangemi wrote: 'Tay loves those girls like they are his. That is all I have to say. My heart is heavy and a piece of it is laying here with Olivia and will forever be there.'

She continued: 'I have to go home later to see her room which I'm dreading. God gained another angel and I pray she will look over us . I love you always Olivia.' 

Edwards is being held in Irving Jail on two counts of child injury. His bond is set at $100,000 and $25,000. 

A GoFundMe has been set up to help with Olivia's funeral costs.
