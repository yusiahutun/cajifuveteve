Christopher Mintz-Plasse is engaged to Britt Bowman after five years of dating onChristmas Eve

Publish date: 2024-06-24

Christopher Mintz-Plasse is walking into 2023 as an engaged man after proposing to his girlfriend Britt Bowman.

Following five years of dating, the 33-year-old Superbad star revealed he popped the question to his other half, who works as a a live-music photographer, on Christmas Eve with a beautiful diamond ring. 

'Life's over!!! ❤️❤️❤️,' he captioned a slideshow of sweet snaps, which began with a selfie of them kissing as she flashed her latest accessory to the camera.
