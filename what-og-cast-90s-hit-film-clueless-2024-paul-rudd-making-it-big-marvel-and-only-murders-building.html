What is the OG cast of 90s hit film Clueless up to in 2024? From Paul Rudd making it big in Marvel a

Publish date: 2024-07-29

1. Alicia Silverstone

Once Hollywood’s darling after playing Cher Horowitz, Alicia Silverstone navigated the roller coaster of fame, which led her to activism and a brief hiatus from the glare of the Hollywood spotlight.

Despite a bumpy ride, including her challenging role in Batman & Robin, Silverstone found her groove again in the theatre and embraced veganism, writing The Kind Diet.

She also got divorced from Christopher Jarecki and starred in Netflix’s Reptile. At 47, Silverstone appears to be focusing on acting, activism and her family life.

2. Stacey Dash

At 57, Stacey Dash, known as the chic and savvy Dionne in Clueless, leveraged her iconic role to build a diverse acting resume. Yet her bold step into politics caught many by surprise.

The move echoed her character’s fearlessness – but with a controversial twist. Dash has since returned to her acting roots and, like Silverstone, is taking care of her family, while steering clear of politics.

3. Brittany Murphy

Brittany Murphy charmed audiences as Tai Frasier, the sweet and quirky new girl who undergoes one of the film’s most memorable transformations. After her breakout role, her career soared in both film and television, but her journey took a heartbreaking turn with her sudden passing in 2009 at just 32.

Amid swirling speculation about her death, in 2021, a documentary What Happened, Brittany Murphy? exploring her life and career shed new light on her story, offering fans a deeper understanding of her struggles and triumphs.

4. Paul Rudd

Who would have thought that Paul Rudd, 54, our stepbrother-turned-love interest Josh, would become one of Hollywood’s most enduring and versatile actors? With roles in comedy, drama and superhero franchises, notably as Ant-Man in the Marvel Cinematic Universe to his memorable appearances on Saturday Night Live, Rudd has proven to be a timeless talent.Off-screen, he’s known for his down-to-earth personality and philanthropy, often taking part in charity events and maintaining a relatively private family life.

Currently, Rudd continues to play various roles in film and television projects, such as HBO’s Only Murders in the Building.

5. Elisa Donovan

Elisa Donovan, aka Amber, Cher’s fashion-forward rival, always showcased her knack for comedic timing. Post-Clueless, she lit up screens in Sabrina the Teenage Witch and a few indie films.

Off-screen, Donovan turned her talents to writing and advocacy, opening up about motherhood and her battle with eating disorders to help and inspire others.

Now, at 53, she juggles acting with motherhood and activism.

6. Donald Faison

Donald Faison, at 49, still lights up the screen with the same humour he brought to Murray in Clueless. After the film, he became a household name as Dr. Chris Turk in Scrubs, securing his star status on TV. Life off-screen has had its highs and lows, from fatherhood to finding love again.

Today, Faison stays busy with acting gigs and entertainment ventures, balancing a bustling career with his family life.
