Nina Agdal slips into an oversized blazer as she celebrates her 29th birthday

Publish date: 2024-03-08

She turned 29 on Friday. 

And Nina Agdal celebrated her birthday in style on Saturday as she rocked an oversized blazer for lunch in New York City. 

The runway model looked effortlessly chic as she arrived at Cipriani restaurant in Soho with her boyfriend, Jack Brinkley-Cook and friends.

She completed the look with a white top and cream handbag draped over her shoulder. 

Opting for practical footwear, Nina sported classic white trainers and she protected herself from the novel coronavius with a black face mask.
