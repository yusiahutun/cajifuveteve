'Trolls used to mock my big boobs but I now earn six figures from 34KK chest'

A curvy model who quit school after being harassed for her chest size is now having the last laugh - with her making a six-figure salary from her modelling online.

Born with a natural 34KK chest Anastasiya Berthier, 28, says she's learnt to love her curves - and wants other women to do the same.

"When I turned 13, my boobs just shot up in size," she said. "By 14, I was in a DD cup.

During her teens, Anastasiya faced torment and ridicule from her schoolmates.

Most of the jeers and mockery came from boys, but even some girls exhibited extreme jealousy. One girl even chased Anastasiya down the school corridor with a knife, threatening to cut off her breasts.

The trauma reached its climax when a male student, angered by Anastasiya's rejection of his constant advances, spread false rumours about her, saying she had a foursome with him and his friends in the school bathroom.

"I was a virgin at that time, and I was so upset and embarrassed by what he had told everyone about me," she added. "At that moment, I felt destroyed and dirty. It led me to a horrible depression."

The mounting pressure and emotional turmoil forced Anastasiya to leave a traditional school altogether, and she finished her education at home. For three years, she was homeschooled, often locking herself in her room, playing video games and engaging with friends only through online games. She didn't even go to prom.

"I was lonely growing up, but that changed when I discovered communities of women on social media who were celebrating their diverse body types," she said. "Their stories helped me see my own beauty and my own worth."

Inspired by the self-confidence of others, Anastasiya, who worked as a graphic designer and content writer before becoming a model, began chronicling her journey on social media as well.

"I started my Instagram page in 2018 as a professional account for my artwork but then began uploading pictures of me in swimwear," she commented. "The images attracted the attention of influencers, who reposted my pictures.

"My page started to grow fast, gaining more than 510,000 followers in just two years. Instagram blocked my main account for six months, so I created a new one. It did even better than the first."

Now boasting more than 1.7million followers on Instagram, Anastasiya has transformed a "flaw" into her biggest asset. Her account offers a blend of stunning photographs and creative narratives.

Far from being reserved, she wholeheartedly embraces the lighthearted quirks associated with her chest size, frequently infusing humour into her posts.

While Anastasiya loves engaging her followers with lighthearted content, she doesn't shy away from addressing weightier topics like cyberbullying and its real-life repercussions.

She said: "While most of my followers are supportive, I still get negative comments and experience slut-shaming. Trolls hide behind the anonymity of the internet and fake accounts and continue to harass me, always trying to give their opinion or even create forums to talk about my personal life.
