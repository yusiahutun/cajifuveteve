Ian Drennan is the ex-husband of American singer and songwriter, Caroline Polachek. They were married from 2015 to 2018.

What You Didn’t Know About Caroline Polachek’s Ex-Husband Ian Drennan

Here are some facts about Caroline Polachek’s ex-husband, Ian Drennan that are unknown to many people;

    • Ian Drennan is a music composure and a structural engineer who is based in New York City, United States.
    • Ian Drennan and Caroline Polachek’s wedding was featured on Vogue’s website as part of an article published in 2016 titled “The 15 Most Stunning Fall Weddings in Vogue.” They were number 12 on the list.
    • He celebrates his birthday every September 24, according to loads of wishes addressed to him on that day on a digital platform called Dropzone.com.
  • Ian Drennan has four official albums to his name. They are; The Wonderful World (2012), Flowers of Love (2015), MeMylmp (2020) and Passchendaele (2020).
  • Most of his songs can be found on a YouTube channel called Soap Library. However, his views are quite low.
