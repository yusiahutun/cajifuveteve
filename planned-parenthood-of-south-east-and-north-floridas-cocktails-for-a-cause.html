Planned Parenthood of South, East and North Floridas Cocktails for a Cause

Publish date: 2024-04-05

Amy Smith, Ira Pearlstine and Debbie and Dr. Howard Maunus

A gathering of 110 Planned Parenthood supporters came together at a waterfront home during the 11th annual “Cocktails for a Cause” fundraiser. Proceeds from the sold-out fundraiser will ensure access to reproductive health care and information in Martin County and surrounding areas.
