Roman's Girlfriend

His girlfriend is Anne-Sophie Flury, who he has said he will miss terribly while in the jungle.

Prior to the show, he told The Mirror: "I will be happy to get rid of social media but the thing I will miss most is speaking to my parents and my girlfriend."

The pair have been dating since early 2018 and are regularly pictured together in London.

Anne-Sophie also knows how to make some decent cash as she recently launched a product called Shu Gum, a cleaning compound that will make your shoes sparkle and not smell of odour.

Roman's famous parents

Roman's dad is none other than Martin Kemp, an English actor, director, musician and occasional television presenter, best known as the bassist in the band Spandau Ballet.

He also starred as Steve Owen in EastEnders, and is the brother of Gary Kemp, who was also in Spandau Ballet and is now an actor.

Roman's mum is also famous in her own right, Shirlie Holliman is a former English pop singer who found fame in the 1980s with Wham!

His mother was also part of the duo Pepsi & Shirlie.
