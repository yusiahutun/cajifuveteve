The 8 Best Lash Growth Serums of 2024, Tested and Reviewed

Publish date: 2024-06-04

There’s no denying the appeal of elongated, fluttery lashes. And what’s not to love? Longer lashes open up your eyes, making them appear larger, youthful, and more vibrant. Many femme-presenting people go a long way to get that alluring pop in the eye, be it with lash curlers, volumizing mascara, lash extensions, or faux lashes. But the most effective and longest lasting way to land the long lashes of your dreams is with a lash growth serum. With consistency and a few daily strokes of a hyper-targeted formula along your lash line, you can get gorgeous, healthy and fuller looking lashes in just a few weeks. 

If it sounds too good to be true (which to be honest, we thought at first as well), it’s not. We tested 30 of the most popular lash growth serums over a span of ten weeks to evaluate which ones made our lashes feel stronger, look longer, and appear more fuller and voluminous. We judged each lash growth serum based on how easy they were to use and how quickly the serum absorbed into the lash line, as well as the degree to which they strengthened and lengthened our lashes, and finally, the way our lashes looked overall after using the serum for ten weeks. To earn a spot on our list, each serum also needed to be safe for sensitive eyes.

What to Keep in Mind

Lash Goals 

Not all lash growth serums are created equal. Some are best for increasing strength, some target length, and others provide extra volume. Before you purchase a lash growth serum, you’ll want to be clear on what kind of results you hope to see. 

If doubling your lash length is your goal, then there are no better options than the Borboleta Lash Serum or the RevitaLash Advanced Eyelash Conditioner. If strengthening and improving lash health are your motivating factor, then the Lancôme Cils Booster Lash Revitalizing Serum and Grande Cosmetics GrandeLASH-MD Lash Enhancing Serum will impress you. Meanwhile if you’re looking for a little two-in-one action, then the Vichy LiftActiv Eyelash Growth Serum can smooth out fine lines while improving lashes and the Âme Pure Lash Boost™ Eyelash Growth Serum will enhance your eyebrows as well. 

Ingredients

The benefits you see from using a lash serum are brought to life by ingredients such as biotin, panthenol, and lipids. 

Biotin, found in the Borboleta Lash Serum, RevitaLash Advanced Eyelash Conditioner, and RapidLash Eyelash Enhancing Serum, “supports the natural production of keratin to encourage the looks of thicker and fuller lashes,” explains Dr. Mona Gohara, a board certified dermatologist in Connecticut.

Jasmine Imani, licensed esthetician and founder of Jas Imani Beauty, chimes in that if you hope to achieve strong and thick lashes, it is essential to keep them well hydrated. “Lashes that aren’t well hydrated cannot reach their full growth potential, and will shed more,” she says, adding that panthenol helps to enhance the shine and softness of lashes.

A balanced formula also typically includes lipids, which "target the anagen phase of the hair cycle to provide thicker, fuller looking eyelashes,” says Dr. Gohara. 

Usage

Instructions will vary based on the product and brand, and should be followed for best results. You can expect most brands to recommend applying once or twice a day. 

When applying in the morning, wait three to five minutes (or until the serum is fully absorbed) for it to dry before applying any makeup over it. In the evening, you should still wait a few minutes for the product to fully absorb to avoid transferring the product to the pillowcase.

Your Questions, Answered

Do eyelash growth serums work?

Yes, eyelash growth serums work, although there is “a natural built in stopping point” for your hair growth, says Dr. Gohara. This means that anyone who already has long lashes may not see a huge difference in length, even if a lash serum is applied consistently. Once your max or ideal length is achieved, continue to apply the serum regularly to maintain.

How do you apply eyelash serum?

Most brands will provide detailed instructions on how to apply their specific eyelash serum. Many serums come with an applicator (not unlike a slim liquid eyeliner), which you gently brush along your eyelids to deposit product. 

You want to make sure the product is applied to the lash line and wait the recommended amount of time to allow it to sink in. There are a few brands, like the Vichy LiftActiv Eyelash Growth Serum, which don’t come with an applicator. Instead, you use your finger to lightly dab along your lashes. Make sure to do so with clean hands to avoid any unwanted bacteria from entering the eyes. 

Are eyelash serums safe?

In general, “over the counter lash serums are safe to use,” says Dr. Castilla. “Although they are not FDA tested, cosmetic companies often do their own safety testing.”

To stay on the safe side, it’s important to closely read the ingredients list and check the packaging to see whether a dermatologist tested or approved the formula. The only lash serum approved by the FDA for lash growth is the prescription serum, Latisse, according to Dr. Castilla, so you’ll need to visit your doctor to determine whether you’re a candidate.

It’s also important to take appropriate precautions. Imani advises her clients to find products that work for their body. “If there are any signs of discoloration or irritation, stop using immediately," cautions Imani, who adds that that serums tend to spread easily, so if you get a little overzealous with your application, take a q-tip to wipe off excess product.

What helps eyelashes grow?

Lash serums target the anagen phase, which refers to the growth phase of the hair cycle. “This makes the hair appear thicker, fuller, and in some instances, darker,” explains Dr. Gohara. Lash growth serums work to enhance the natural growth cycle of eyelashes.
