Two times American Playboy cover model and aspiring singer Viking Barbie opens up about her rehab journey via an Instagram post on 19th May 2019. 

Getting Out of Jail And Heading To Rehab 

Kayleigh Swenson, aka Viking Barbie, compared her mugshot taken on 2011 to her recent picture of 2019 through an Instagram post. In the caption, she mentioned how 2011 was the toughest period in her life as at the time she was getting out of jail and battling her drug addiction. 

She also added that before moving to Rehab, her body was reacting badly due to drug addiction as her hair was falling, her skin was a mess, her nose was swollen, and she lost a few teeth. Swenson didn’t think she would make it out alive from that life, but she did, and now a few years later, she is better than ever. 

All about Viking Barbie’s Ink 

Barbie is not only showering the world with her beautiful music, but she is also known for showcasing her body art. She has turned her body into a canvas with several complex tattoos. 

According to Yahoo News, Viking got her tattoos made by renowned tattoo artist Sergio Sanchez. Barbie tattoos are the visual representation of the Seven Deadly Sins. Her inks consist of the Sins of wrath, vanity, gluttony, and lust.

Barbie states that her tattoos are a reminder of the struggles that she went through in her life. 

"My issues with excess and obsession was something that I've struggled with… This is why I wanted a reminder on my arms."

Similarly, Barbie also has a tattoo of “Daddy” on her body which she made for the love and respect for her father and famous wrestler Jeep Swenson.
