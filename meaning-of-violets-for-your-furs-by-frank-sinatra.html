Meaning of Violets for Your Furs by Frank Sinatra

Publish date: 2024-03-16

The Frank Sinatra song titled "Violets for Your Furs" is a beautiful and poetic composition that transports listeners into a winter wonderland filled with romance and enchantment. The overall theme of the song revolves around the magic of love and how it has the power to transform even the coldest of seasons into a warm and blooming spring.One standout lyric in the song is, "I bought you violets for your furs, and it was spring for a while, remember?" This lyric beautifully captures the transformative power of love. Despite being in the midst of winter, the act of buying violet flowers for the person he loves brings forth a sense of spring. It signifies how love can thaw the icy barriers and fill the air with the freshness of a blooming season.Another important lyric in the song is, "The snow drifted down on the flowers, and it melted where it lay. The snow looked like dew on the blossoms, as on a summer day." This lyric evokes the image of delicate snowflakes covering the vibrant violet flowers. It symbolizes the merging of the two seasons, winter and summer, as the snow melts and resembles the dewdrops that typically adorn blossoming flowers during warmer months. It highlights the idea that love can bridge the gap between different seasons, bringing elements of warmth and growth even in the midst of cold winter.Moving on, the lyric, "Then you pinned the violets to your furs and gave a lift to the crowds passing by," adds a touch of glamour and elegance to the song. It accentuates the way love can not only transform the individuals involved but also uplift the spirits of those around them. The simple act of wearing violets on her furs draws attention and admiration from the passersby. It suggests that love has the power to radiate positivity and captivate the attention of others, creating a contagious sense of joy.In a more introspective moment within the song, Sinatra sings, "You smiled at me so sweetly, since then one thought occurs that we fell in love completely the day that I bought you violets for your furs." This lyric reflects on the profound impact that a single gesture of love can have on a relationship. It suggests that the act of buying violets for her furs was a turning point, a catalyst for falling in love completely. It beautifully emphasizes the transformative power of love, how a simple act can spark an everlasting fire that illuminates one's entire world.Overall, "Violets for Your Furs" is an enchanting song that weaves together unexpected themes within its tender lyrics. It explores the transformative power of love, its ability to thaw the cold, merge the seasons, captivate hearts, and create lasting connections. Through the imagery of violets, snow, and furs, the song takes listeners on a journey of love and romance, reminding us of the magic that can be found even in the coldest of winters.
