She's currently trying to ramp up her music career.

And Cheyenne Tozzi is the epitome of a rock chick in her latest photo shoot.

The Australian model posed on a New York rooftop recently, dressed in leather motorcycle jacket and puffing on a cigarette.

Rock chick: Cheyenne Tozzi is the epitome of rock chick in her latest New York photo shoot

Looking like a good girl gone bad, she cling to her chic biker jacket which is slung over her shoulders as she hold her cigarette between her teeth.

In another, the beauty hides behind her blonde tresses while sitting cross-legged, showing off a pair of ripped black jeans.

While in the Big Apple, Tozzi also took some time out to attend a friend’s wedding along with fellow models Montana Cox, Luci Taffs and Alexandra Spencer.

It’s been a whirlwind few weeks for the blonde beauty.

Hell for leather: The 25-year-old dons a leather motorcycle jacket and puffs away on a cigarette 

The Face judge told the Daily Mail Australia on Thursday that she has been jetting between Stockholm, New York, Los Angeles and Chicago, posing for fashion shoots and recording tracks for her band, Van Hoorn.

In Stockholm, she shot with cult fast fashion retailer Gina Tricot and in LA she attended the Stance Open House event along with Australian artist and model Rose Ashton and pro surfer Laura Enever.

Cheyenne plans to design a capsule collection for the brand that will launch in 2015 and follows model Bambi Northwood Blyth’s collaboration collection that is set to launch next month.

She also stopped off in Chicago with fellow bandmate Josh Mullane to work with the producer for The Kills, Bill Skibbe on new music.

Shady lady: A pair of black Ray-Bans complete the Van Hoorn rocker's look

Painted lady: Cheyenne also shows off her various arm tattoos in the shoot 

The 25-year-old has been dating Josh’s brother, music manager Tyson Mullane for over two years and he's currently focused on launching his girlfriend's singing career.

She joked to the Daily Mail Australia on Thursday that she and her beau keep waving at each other from planes crossing the sky as they’re on completely different work schedules at the moment.

Cheyenne has since returned to Australia since the shoot to spend time with her ailing 91-year-old grandfather, who is in hospital.
