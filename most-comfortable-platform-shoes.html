The Investment Platform

The Row Zippered Platform Leather Combat Boots

The Investment Platform

The Row Zippered Platform Leather Combat Boots

For those looking to invest this season, the Row is the brand to indulge in. You’ve probably seen your favorite fashion girls opting for this platform boot season after season, styling it with knitted dresses and funky maxi skirts alike. This boot is set on a 2.2-inch block platform heel, with an easy zip-up front and smooth leather.

Materials: Leather

A Scandi Platform

Everlane The Puffa Clog

Everlane The Puffa Clog

Many people believe you can achieve great heights without sacrificing comfort, and Everlane agrees with that notion. Wear the Puffa clogs with everything, from denim skirts to baggy trousers. As one reviewer puts it, “These clogs feel fantastic. The heel is so comfortable. I have wide feet and was able to wear these without any discomfort.”

Heel height: 3.25"

Materials: 100% leather upper

Sizes: 5–11
