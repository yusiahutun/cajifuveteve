The Real host Jeannie Mai talks bout her past drug use and her 'really dark time'

Publish date: 2024-09-18

The Real host Jeannie Mai opened up about her past drug use on Tuesday's episode of the daytime talk show, revealing it was during a 'dark time' in her life.

The 40 year old Mai's co-host Adrienne Bailon asked, 'what made you curious to ever want to try drugs,' with Mai jokingly responding, 'I love that she put me out there on live TV.'

As the studio got a laugh out of it, Bailon added that she was 'curious' before Mai opened up about her past.

'I definitely experimented, and I know now at 40 looking back that I had a really dark time in my life when I split from my family and moved out,' she began.

'I lived in San Francisco -- like, every drug you can imagine was there -- and honestly, it replaced the joy that I didn't have,' Mai added.

Mowry-Housley asked Mai if she actually knew that the drugs she was doing were bad for her and she said she was 'rebellious so I knew I was doing bad.'

But she eventually realized that even to this day, 'I still pay consequences for being naughty. I still have to check myself and go, 'Was that really worth it?'"
