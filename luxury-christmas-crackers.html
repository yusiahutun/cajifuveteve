Luxurious Christmas Crackers, Set Of Six

Luxurious Christmas Crackers, Set Of Six

For a modern, eye-catching Christmas table, opt for these electric blue and hot pink geometric crackers from Harvey Nichols, which are packed with festive favourites, including gold and silver paper crowns and jokes. Each contains a genuinely covetable gift, from a miniature raspberry jam or a champagne stopper, to chic tin of HN-branded mints or a turned wooden honey stirrer (just the thing for the perfect swirl on top of your Boxing Day porridge or pancakes).

Harrods Guilty Pleasures Crackers, Set Of Six

Harrods Guilty Pleasures Crackers, Set Of Six

No-one does luxurious Christmas classics quite like Harrods. The department store's crackers are always a Bazaar favourite, and this year is certainly no different. These traditional gold creations are filled with surprisingly practical presents, including some British-made kitchen utensils that'll surely come in handy.

Whitebox Cocktails Margarita Crackers, Set Of Six

Whitebox Cocktails Margarita Crackers, Set Of Six

Christmas is a time for indulgence, so why not splash out this year on a set of cocktail crackers? Each one of these contains not one but two canned Margaritas (made to a classic recipe of fresh lime, tequila and triple sec), so you and your guests can celebrate the big day in style. All you need to do is supply the salted-rim glasses. After all, whoever said Christmas was all about champagne and Eggnog?

The Evergreen Crackers, Set Of Six

The Evergreen Crackers, Set Of Six

These intricately decorated, traditional crackers from Fortnum & Mason are at the higher end of the scale price-wise, but they're honestly worth it: each contains a hand-picked gift from the famous Piccadilly store, including tea strainers, champagne bottle stoppers and tea towels, all housed in elegant, teal-blue wrapping.

Harrods Luxury Gilty Pleasures Christmas Crackers, Set Of Six

Harrods Luxury Gilty Pleasures Christmas Crackers, Set Of Six

For a Christmas dinner to remember, consider Harrods' most luxurious crackers. The cleverly named Gilty Pleasures set contains six crackers, each one filled with an extra-generous gift, from an Aspinal of London leather accessory to a Trudon fragrance and even a scented candle. And there's more: each recipient will also find a £50 Harrods voucher hiding in their cracker: slightly more exciting than a paper hat, no?

Disco Daze Christmas Crackers, Set Of Six

Disco Daze Christmas Crackers, Set Of Six

The ideal set of crackers for foodies, this hot-pink selection from Harrods looks incredibly festive. In each glittery cracker, your guests will find the traditional joke and hat, along with a British-made kitchen utensil they'll find useful long after Christmas – from an egg cup and a bottle opener, to a cookie cutter and a cocktail stirrer.

Bamford Silent Night Cracker

Bamford Silent Night Cracker

This beautiful, navy and gold cracker from luxury brand Bamford is as much of a treat inside as it is outside. A lavishly chunky addition to any table, each one contains a limited-edition set of five mini hand and body washes in Rose, Jasmine, Sage, Geranium and Citrus scents. Christmas Cottage Winter Foliage Crackers, Set Of Six

Christmas Cottage Winter Foliage Crackers, Set Of Six

Wonderfully festive with their holly and mistletoe patterns and crimson velvet bows, these John Lewis crackers look far more expensive than they are. Each one contains the traditional hat, a motto and a present – as well as a particularly pleasing snap. What's more, all John Lewis crackers are recyclable, including the paper hats and mottos, and have plastic-free contents.

Silver Glitter Crackers, Set Of Six

Silver Glitter Crackers, Set Of Six

The queen of tablescaping is undoubtedly Alice Naylor-Leyland, whose Mrs Alice label outfits the chicest dining rooms in town. Her glittery crackers, each adorned with a hand-tied, olive-green velvet bow, are just as stylish as you'd expect. They're filled with the usual festive trinkets, including a paper party hat, a joke and a small gift. If you want to match your table for real festive flair, pair them with the brand's Heidi Green Tablecloth, Green Lace Dinner Plates and Frosted Fir Trees to create a proper winter wonderland.
