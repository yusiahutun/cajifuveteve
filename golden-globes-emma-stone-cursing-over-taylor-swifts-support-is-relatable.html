Golden Globes: Emma Stone cursing over Taylor Swift's support is relatable

Publish date: 2024-06-25

Emma Stone and Taylor Swift’s friendship is real, and their public displays of affection is relatable to fans.

The 81st edition of the Golden Globes was filled to the brim with stars. From Selena Gomez and Taylor Swift to Jennifer Lawrence and Emma Stone, Hollywood‘s favorite icons attended the ceremony at Beverly Hilton in Los Angeles. Emma Stone also snagged the Best Performance by an Actress in a Motion Picture — Musical or Comedy award for her phenomenal performance in Poor Things. The movie star was cheered by her peers including Taylor Swift and Jennifer Lawrence, who were stoked for her.

Emma Stone reacts to Taylor Swift’s big cheer at the Golden Globes

Taylor Swift and Emma Stone’s friendship is too relatable for fans.

The 34-year-old music star was seen cheering for the Poor Things actress when she won the Best Performance by an Actress award. The Cruel Summer crooner’s standing ovation for Emma proves how proud she is of her close friend.

Later, Emma was asked about Taylor Swift’s reaction to her win in a backstage interview when the movie star couldn’t help but make a joke.

She quipped: “What an a–hole, am I right?”

Gushing over her friendship with Tay Tay, she added: “I’ve known her for almost 20 years and I was very happy she was there. She was also nominated tonight which was wonderful and, yes, what an a–hole.”

Friends who never fell apart

When it comes to decade-long friendship, Emma Stone and Taylor Swift can be pure goals. The duo met in their teen years and became great friends over time.

Swifties also believe the decade-long friendship of Emma and Tay Tay has resulted in the hitmaker writing songs about her. The track When Emma Falls In Love in Speak Now Taylor’s version is speculated to be about her.

When asked about the song recently in an interview, the celebrity passed the ball in Taylor’s court saying: “You have to ask [Taylor]!”

She also shared her feelings about their friendship that began two decades ago, saying: “We just kept in touch ever since and became great friends.”
