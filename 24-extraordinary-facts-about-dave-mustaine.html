Formation of Megadeth

In 1983, Mustaine formed the legendary heavy metal band Megadeth. Known for their technicality and aggressive sound, Megadeth went on to become one of the “Big Four” bands of thrash metal.

Pioneering Guitar Riffs

Mustaine’s distinctive guitar-playing style and inventive riffs have made him one of the most influential guitarists in the metal genre. His shredding solos and fast-paced riffs have inspired countless aspiring guitarists.

Mustaine has been nominated for a total of 12 Grammy Awards throughout his career. He finally won his first Grammy in 2017 for Best Metal Performance with the song “Dystopia.”
