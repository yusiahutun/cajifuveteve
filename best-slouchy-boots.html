Our Must-Have Slouchy Boot

Manolo Blahnik Calasso Slouchy Boots

Our Must-Have Slouchy Boot

Manolo Blahnik Calasso Slouchy Boots

Manolo has taken influence from feminine details and utilitarian styling to create a series that can be worn throughout every season. With its almond toe and walkable high block heel, it’s clear the Calasso is an everyday boot to invest in.

“The Calasso style is the perfect slouchy boot. I had taken inspiration from utility styling, and it comes in a range of different colors and textures, like leather and suede, and hits mid-calf," Blahnik says. “[It is] a simple silhouette that is subtle and practical.”

Sizes: 3–13

Heel height: 3.5"

A Boot With a Slight Slouch

Inez Mila Olive Calf Boots

A Boot With a Slight Slouch

Inez Mila Olive Calf Boots

Inez creative director Hannah Spinelli loves to mix and match aesthetics when wearing the Mila boots. “Lately I’ve been wearing our Mila boot under a loose-fitting midi slipdress that has side slits,” she says. “I love the unexpected contrast of delicate silk with the substance of a slouch boot. It feels right in line with the current ‘wrong shoe theory.’”

Sizes: 6–10.5

Heel height: 2.8"
