Keke Palmer and Usher tease 'killer' music collab as 'Boyfriend' drama heats up

Publish date: 2024-05-19

Keke Palmer has teased a collab with Usher, less than a month after her relationship drama with boyfriend Darius Jackson about her sheer dress at an Usher concert.

Darius previously received criticism for publically criticizing Keke’s sheer dress after a video emerged where Usher surprised Keke with a serenade of his single There Goes My Baby.

Keke Palmer teases Usher collaboration

Taking to her Instagram, Keke posted a teaser trailer of Usher’s new music video for his song, Boyfriend, in which she stars in.

In the snippet, Usher can be heard singing: “Somebody said that your boyfriend’s looking for me,” while Palmer can be seen strutting down the hallway with blue highlights in her hair.

The clip, posted to the actress’ Instagram, states that the full video will be premiering tomorrow (August 16), so fans don’t have long to wait!
