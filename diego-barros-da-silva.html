Biography

Diego Barros da Silva was born in Cuiabá, Brazil on June 19, 1986. Singer best known as one half of the Brazilian sertaneja duo Henrique & Diego. He and bandmate Luiz Henrique Teixeira have scored major hits with such tracks as “Suíte 14,” “Esqueci Você” and “Raspão,” all of which topped the Brazilian Hot 100 chart. His song “Festa Boa” features Gusttavo Lima. He attended the samba school A Estrela do Oriente.
