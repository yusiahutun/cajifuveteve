Salma Hayek Is a Vision in a Figure-Hugging Maxi Dress at Saint Laurent Show

Publish date: 2024-04-07

Salma Hayek was among the A-list attendees at Saint Laurent's fall/winter 2023 show—and she made sure to bring her sartorial A game.

The Magic Mike star looked gorgeous in a form-fitting knit maxi dress in a rich shade of forest green. She layered the mock turtleneck dress under a sophisticated cropped black blazer, then finished the look with a pair of towering black platform boots. To accessorize, she opted for statement jewelry, wearing a long gold chain necklace with an attached crystal pendant, diamond stud earrings, and a few chunky gold rings. She coordinated with a glossy black rectangular clutch with gold hardware.

As for her glam, Hayek went all out. The actress matched the color palette of her outfit to her beauty look, wearing shimmering green eye shadow and dark green eyeliner, plus a lustrous rosy pink lip. As for her dark brown shoulder-length mane, she styled her hair into a half-up, half-down 'do.

Pascal Le Segretain//Getty Images

Pascal Le Segretain//Getty Images

The actress has been having a fashion-filled season thus far.

Last week, she sat front row at Bottega Veneta's show during Milan Fashion Week, where she looked regal in a sparkling gold knit midi dress and a textured black fur coat. She matched her accessories to her footwear, stepping out in dark red platform Mary Janes and a maroon crinkled tote bag.

Swan Gallet//Getty Images

During Gucci's runway show, she opted for a celestial theme in a midnight blue midi gown with gold star embroidery all over. She finished the look with a metallic silver clutch and silver platform sandals.
