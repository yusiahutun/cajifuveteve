Lana Del Reys Age, Height, Weight, Parents and Siblings

Publish date: 2024-05-23

If pop music has Taylor Swift, indie pop has Lana Del Rey. Indeed, the Summertime Sadness singer has taken the world by storm with her dreamy, psychedelic songs and cinematic music videos.

Her influence on the music industry is undeniable, as she paved the way for lyrical exploration on matters that society might consider taboo.

Unfortunately, though, she also paid a hefty price for that. If she doesn’t make headlines for her sold-out concerts and record-breaking albums, she often does so by getting involved in heavy controversies.

Still, that doesn’t stop her from attracting many fans around the world. So, if you’re just getting started in your Livvie life, here’s everything you need to know about Lana Del rey from her age, height, weight, parents, and siblings.

How Old is Lana Del Rey?

Lana Del Rey, born Elizabeth Woolridge Grant, is 36 years of age. She was brought into the world on June 21, 1985 in Manhattan, New York. Her family later moved to Lake Placid where Lana would go one to spend the rest of her childhood. 

Unlike most female popstars today, Lana did not become a professional musician until she was in her mid 20s. She may have dabbled in songwriting and music producing here and there in her teenage years, but that was not her sole focus in life.

She even went to university and got her metaphysics degree before she became a superstar. At age 26, she got ticket to the mainstream when her song Video Games went viral on YouTube. She’ll be celebrating the first decade of her career this June!

This just goes to show that no one is ever too old to start new things!

What is Lana Del Rey’s Height?

Based on the consensus of multiple reports and anecdotal evidence of Livvies who happened to see Lana up close, it can be deduced that Lana is relatively above the average height of American women.

Her estimated height is 5ft6 or 167.6cm, although a quick Google search will show you that some people claim that she’s 5ft7 or 170cm tall. If the latter number is true, that means that Lana is as tall as the following celebrities:

  • Margot Robbie
  • Elizabeth Olsen
  • Kendrick Lamar
  • Jack Black
  • Meryl Streep
  • Sarah Paulson

What is Lana Del Rey’s Weight?

Lana Del Rey is known for her slim body type. She revealed in an interview with Harper Bazaar that she takes spin classes for her workout routine.

However, there was one point in her life where she was noticeably gaining weight. A lot of fans attributed that to nicotine withdrawal, as they believe the artist has been trying to quit smoking. These days, however, it seems that the American artist is now back in shape.

There’s no way to tell how much she actually weighs, but reports claim that her weight is somewhere around 55kg to 60kg, or 121lbs to 132lbs. 

Who Are Lana Del Rey’s Parents and Siblings?

Lana Del Rey was born to Scottish parents Robert England Grant Jr. and Pat Grant as the eldest daughter of the family. She has a younger sister, Caroline Grant, and a younger brother, Charlie Grant.

The Grant family was considerably wealthy, since Lana’s father was the founder and owner of “Web Media Properties” – an online media publishing company based in Florida.

And as for the Grant siblings, they have maintained a very close relationship with one another, even going as far as collaborating in several projects.

Charlie once appeared in Lana’s self-directed film called Tropico, while Caroline (A.K.A. Chuck) offered her photographic skills to Lana for her promotional photos. Needless to say, Lana has a very supportive family.
