50+ farewell invitation messages for colleagues and friends

Publish date: 2024-02-06

Never forget to draft farewell invitation messages in preparation for a colleague and friend who is leaving. Goodbyes are celebrated to commemorate sheer delight while you dearly wish them a prosperous future. So, what are the best invitations for your friend and colleague's farewell?

TABLE OF CONTENTS

Do you know how to send farewell invitation and what to send? Whether it is a colleague leaving or a friend moving to a new city, organising parties for them can make all the difference. Therefore, farewell invitation messages would come in handy.

Farewell invitation messages

What should I write in a farewell invitation? A good invitation message is all you need to convey the required information. Here are some of the best invitation messages for any farewell party.

  • After years of dedication and commitment, it’s time to bid farewell to (name). Come to his farewell party.
  • This opportunity only arises once, so let’s make the most of it by commemorating (name’s) departure party and hoping and praying for her success.
  • The day is arriving when we will bid goodbye to our beloved and senior colleague, Mr. (name). We hope you all are present on his big day.
  • Please attend the graduation celebration in honour of (the class)’s goodbye as the final class from (institution title), making it a day to cherish.
  • We should arrange a day off to commemorate the goodbye party of (name), reflect on the good old times, and keep this day memorable.
  • Having you at (Name) farewell party will be a great pleasure. I hope you’ll have the time to be with us on [Date]. I am looking forward to seeing you!
  • Mr. (Name) is leaving! Everyone join me at my house on (Date) at (time) to bid him goodbye and good luck.
  • Let’s enjoy this very day with most of our emotions as it marks the farewell celebration for (name), who will graduate from (university title) upon (Date, time, and place).
  • (Colleague's Name) is a good friend and officemate, so on his/her last day in the office, our group has decided to surprise him/her with a farewell party. Please join us at (colleague’s name) farewell party to be held in (place) strictly at (time). Thank you! Your presence is highly appreciated.
  • Time and tide wait for none. Similarly, it didn’t wait for our colleague (name). It’s time we bid him farewell.
  • Our youngest colleague Ms. (name), will leave us to pursue her higher studies in Germany. Let us all join together to bid her farewell.
  • Bidding farewell has always been challenging, but your presence will uplift the energies of everyone.
  • Kindly attend and take part in this celebration since it is the first departure party for (name) and contribute to making this very day memorable for both yourself and him/her.

Farewell party invitation message for friend

Organising a farewell party for your friend would be a good idea. So, how do you make a farewell invitation? You can pick a farewell party invitation message from the options below.

  • This text carries a farewell party invitation for my friend who lives nearby. Come and join me in bidding farewell to our colleague as she retires from service.
  • Hello! Please be a part of our farewell party for our dear friend (name), our dear colleague, as he/she says goodbye.
  • Please free yourself and come to our farewell celebration for the Johnsons. Carry no gifts since, on this day, none of that matters except your participation.
  • To a cute friend, we invite you to the farewell party at Plaza tomorrow. Do come and bid farewell to our friend who is moving abroad for work.
  • Let’s all together wish him/her all the best! The farewell party will be held at (place) strictly at (time). Have a great day!
  • A new beginning arrives at the end of someone’s journey. Let us all join hands to bid farewell to
  • Finally, it’s the day we bid farewell to the best leader we had in this office, Mr. (name). We were looking forward to your graceful presence on that day.
  • Come and make this day unique for (name) by celebrating /her/his farewell party and wishing her/him a bright future and prosperity.
  • Towards our friendship, let’s raise a glass tonight! Make the goodbye for (name) memorable by arriving on time.
  • Dear friend, I invite you to the farewell party held on the occasion of bidding farewell to my colleague. The party will be held at my residence, and I expect you to attend.
  • After working with him for more than 30 years, Mr. (name) is considering retirement. Come let’s all participate in saying goodbye to him.

Farewell meeting invite for colleague

Colleagues are probably the people you spend most of your time with. Therefore, it is not easy when one is leaving the company. These are the best messages for a farewell party for your beloved colleagues.

  • We’re celebrating the momentous occasion of the goodbye party of (name); thus, we’re inviting you to join us in celebrating something important.
  • As Mr. (Name) retires after spending his entire life devoted to the company, let’s all come together to say goodbye.
  • We hope to see you at the office canteen at our dear colleague's farewell party tomorrow. “Goodbyes are not the end. It’s just the start of a new beginning”. Please join us!
  • Please accept this invitation to join us at this spectacular farewell party to honour (name) at her course completion from (institution or school name) so that we may wish her the best in her future endeavours.
  • The departure ceremony for our dear friend and confidant, Mr. (name), is today; therefore, let’s enjoy it with our passion and soul.
