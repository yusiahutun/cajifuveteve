125 Best &quot;I Miss You&quot; Quotes for That Special Someone

Publish date: 2024-04-18

No matter the reason you and your loved one are apart, you know there's no feeling quite like the longing that comes from that separation. It's such a uniquely intense and even overwhelming emotion that it can be difficult to describe, which is why you might want to leave it to the pros! Below, you'll find 125 of the most profound, poignant, and passionate missing you quotes from well-known wordsmiths including poets, songwriters, and novelists. They're perfect to share with your favorite faraway folks, whether you're looking for something sweet to write in a note to go with that long-distance relationship gift, or you want to add a special personalized message to the DIY Christmas card you're sending to family across the country.

We also have a whole section dedicated to that singular pang that comes with missing your ride or die, and we've gathered up some wonderful short quotes perfect to send in a text or post on Instagram, too. If you've lost someone, along with reading these uplifting Bible verses about death, you may find comfort from some of the quotes below. That includes Alfred Lord Tennyson's lovely, haunting sentiment, "If I had a flower for every time I thought of you…I could walk through my garden forever." Read on for more quotes that put into words what you might not quite be able to—and then be sure to pass them along to the people you miss most.
