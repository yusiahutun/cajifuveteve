Courteney Cox's insane Malibu beach house where Ed Sheeran stayed rent-free

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Courteney Cox has lived in her Malibu beach house for over a decade, and we can see why she loves it so much! With private guest cottages on the site, even Ed Sheeran has stopped over – he revealed that Courteney let him live there rent-free when he was working on a new album in 2013.
