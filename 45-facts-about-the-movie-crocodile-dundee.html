FAQs

1. Who stars in the movie Crocodile Dundee?

The movie stars Paul Hogan as the charismatic and adventurous character Mick Dundee, alongside Linda Kozlowski as Sue Charlton, a journalist from New York.

2. Is Crocodile Dundee based on a true story?

No, Crocodile Dundee is not based on a true story. However, it was inspired by some real-life Australian bush characters.

3. Where was Crocodile Dundee filmed?

The movie was primarily filmed on location in Australia, particularly in the Northern Territory and Queensland.

4. Are there any sequels to Crocodile Dundee?

Yes, there are two sequels to Crocodile Dundee: “Crocodile Dundee II” released in 1988 and “Crocodile Dundee in Los Angeles” released in 2001.

5. What is the famous quote from Crocodile Dundee?

The most famous quote from the movie is “That’s not a knife. That’s a knife,” which is said by Mick Dundee during a confrontation.
