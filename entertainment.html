'RV' Cast: See the Stars of This Hilarious Film Today

Publish date: 2024-03-11

Robin Williams, Cheryl Hines, Josh Hutcherson and JoJo brought the hilarious 2006 comedy, RV, to life, about a disconnected family who embark on a family vacation, trapped within the confines of a recreational vehicle — and this hilarious cast of characters made it a classic!

Bob, played by Robin Williams, works for a soda company. When a company event goes awry thanks to his daughter’s friend, his boss, played by Will Arnett, insists he travel to Boulder, Colorado so they can acquire another brand — or he risks being fired.

Throwing a wrench in their initial plans for a trip to Hawaii, Bob tells his family they’re taking a trip to Colorado, hiding the real reason for their destination. From hilarious mishaps on their travels to eccentric faces they meet along the way (including a guest appearance by Criminal Minds’ Matthew Gray Gubler), RV is a family classic, tied together with a hilarious cast.

Here, take a look at what the RV cast has been up to since the 2006 release.

RV cast, then and now

Robin Williams as Bob Munro in the RV cast

Robin Williams led the RV cast as family patriarch Bob Munro, but prior to this role in this hilarious comedy, he brought us years of joy in classic roles like Mork in Mork & Mindy, and in films like Dead Poets Society (1989) Hook (1991), Mrs. Doubtfire (1993), Jumanji (1995) and Good Will Hunting (1997).

Related: Robin Williams Movies and TV Shows: Reflecting on the Comedy Icon’s Remarkable Career

After giving us a good laugh in RV, he went on to star in movies like Night at the Museum (2006), August Rush (2007), Old Dogs (2009), and The Crazy Ones, a series in which he starred in.

In 2014, he starred in The Angriest Man in Brooklyn, his last movie to be released prior to his tragic death in August of 2014. To this day, we still remember him for the countless laughs and joy he brought us on the screen throughout his career.

Cheryl Hines as Jamie Munro in the RV cast

Cheryl Hines is another piece of the RV cast known for her comedic prowess. Playing the role of Robin Williams’ wife in the film, she is perhaps best known for her work in the widely revered sitcom Curb Your Enthusiasm alongside Larry David.

However, additional work she’s done over the years in the wake of her RV role includes series like In the Motherhood, Suburgatory and The Flight Attendant, as well as some voice acting work. Since 2014, Hines has been married to presidential candidate Robert F. Kennedy Jr.

Josh Hutcherson as Carl Munro in the RV cast

Josh Hutcherson played the role of Carl Munro, the youngest child of the Munro family. Prior to RV, Josh Hutcherson had already had an impressive resume as a child actor, known for movies like Kicking & Screaming (2005) and Little Manhattan (2005).

After RV, however, his fame catapulted, not only for his work in popular films like Bridge to Terabithia (2007) and Journey to the Center of the Earth (2008), but for his pivotal role in The Hunger Games film franchise as Peeta Mellark opposite Jennifer Lawrence.

After his Hunger Games fame, he began producing and directing short films and music videos. In recent years, he’s continued acting, his most recent works being The Beekeeper (2024), Five Nights at Freddy’s (2023) and 57 Seconds (2023). He also starred in the series Future Man from 2017 to 2020.

JoJo as Cassie Munro

JoJo additionally had an established career prior to her role in RV as the eldest daughter of the family, Cassie Munro. She already had forged a music career for herself, and also had a starring role in the 2006 teen romantic-comedy Aquamarine.

After RV, her music career continued to flourish and later that year, she released one of her most iconic singles, “Too Little Too Late.”

Over the last few years, JoJo has continued her work both on screen as an actress and off the screen as a musician. She released her sixth album in 2021 and also appeared on The Masked Singer. In 2023, she made her debut on Broadway in the role of Satine in Moulin Rouge.

Kristin Chenoweth as Mary Jo Gornicke

Kristin Chenoweth was a hoot in RV as Mary Jo Gornicke, the matriarch of the Gornicke family who meets the Munros on their travels. You probably could recognize Chenoweth for her voice alone, as it sure has done her well over the years.

The screen actress is also famed for her work on the stage, known for Broadway roles in musicals like You’re a Good Man, Charlie Brown and Wicked to name a few. On the screen prior to RV, you could see her in The West Wing as Annabeth Scott.

After RV, she’s had significant television roles in Pushing Daisies, GCB, Glee, Trial & Error, and most recently, Schmigadoon!. As for film roles, you could find her in Deck the Halls (2006), Four Christmases (2008), Descendants (2015), Holidate (2020) and more. She is also the author of a memoir, A Little Bit Wicked: Life, Love, and Faith in Stages.

Jeff Daniels as Travis Gornicke

Prior to his role in RV as the goofy Gornicke dad, Jeff Daniels is probably best known for his work in Dumb and Dumber (1994). After RV, he continued to receive acclaim for his work in Lookout (2007), Mama’s Boy (2007), Traitor (2008) and more.

In recent years, he can be seen in series like American Rust, The Comey Rule, The Looming Tower and Godless, as well as movies like Adam (2020), Guest Artist (2019) and Allegiant (2016).

Chloe Sonnenfeld as Moon Gornicke

Chloe Sonnenfeld played Moon Gornicke in the RV cast, the daughter of the Gornickes who caught the eye of Josh Hutcherson’s character, Carl.

After RV, her acting roles were few and far between, but she can be seen in movies such as Men in Black 3 (2012) and Approaching Normal (2013). She is the daughter of filmmaker and director Barry Sonnenfeld.

Hunter Parrish as Earl Gornicke

Hunter Parrish played the older Gornicke son Earl in RV. After RV, he had roles in films like Freedom Writers (2007) and 17 Again (2009), while also acting in the popular series Weeds.

Additional guest roles he had were in shows including The Good Wife, The Following, Quantico, This Is Us and Ratched. His most recent role was in 2023 in the series The Other Black Girl.

Alex Ferris as Billy Gornicke

Alex Ferris played the youngest member of the Gornicke clan, and has continued to act in numerous small roles over the years. Some of the films and series you could see him in after RV include The L Word, JPod, Smallville, The Time Traveler’s Wife (2009) and Diary of a Wimpy Kid (2010).

More recently, he’s done work in 37-Teen (2019) and Cedar Cove. He also did extensive voice work on the series Martha Speaks.

Will Arnett as Todd Mallory

The hilarious Will Arnett has kept us in stitches ever since his role in RV. From hilarious movies like Blades of Glory (2007) to bringing to life some of the most iconic TV characters in series like Arrested Development and Bojack Horseman, Will Arnett has cemented his status as a comedy icon.

His most recent roles have been in The Morning Show, Twisted Metal, Mulligan and Murderville.
