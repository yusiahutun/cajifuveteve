Katharine McPhee files for divorce from Nick Cokas

Publish date: 2024-06-19

    Katharine McPhee has finally filed for divorce from her husband Nick Cokas. The news comes seven months after the actress was pictured kissing her former director Michael Morris. "After six years of marriage, and having been separated for the past year, Katharine McPhee has filed for a dissolution of marriage from Nick Cokas," her rep confirmed to ."

    The two have an amicable relationship and will remain friends. They ask that people respect their privacy during this time."
