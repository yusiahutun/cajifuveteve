Where's our invite? Kanye West and Kim Kardashian spend a quiet moment on their balcony in Rio de Janeiro as Will Smith parties upstairs

Smith and his chums were obviously infected by the party spirit of the carnival raging away beneath them on the streets of Rio de Janeiro.

Like Kim and Kanye they had flown to the hotspot for the massive bash.

But unlike the quiet pair they were making the most of the party.

By contrast pregnant Kim and her boyfriend looked almost glum as they stood quietly taking in the spectacle beneath them.

Kanye was dressed down in jeans and a T-shirt while Kim was all glamour in a white dress.

Kim, 32, rested her head on her hand as she snapped photos, before the pair took out their phones.

Eventually they were distracted from their reverie by the noise upstairs, with Kanye obviously recognising his fellow star Smith's distinctive tones.
