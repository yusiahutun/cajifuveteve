Rachel Weisz dazzles in strapless gown with a twist during star-studded night out

Publish date: 2024-06-07

Daniel Craig's wife stepped out on the red carpet in Cannes on Saturday night 

    Rachel Weisz never puts a foot wrong when it comes to fashion, and proved so once again on Saturday night as she stepped out in Cannes. 

    The Hollywood star - who is married to Daniel Craig - looked phenomenal in a strapless midnight blue gown embellished in sequins, as she posed on the red carpet at the 6th Cannes International Festival. 

    Rachel styled her brunette hair in loose waves and opted for a natural makeup look, with a bold lip. 

    While actress enjoyed a night out in France, Daniel stayed at home looking after their young daughter - who they keep out of the spotlight.

     The pair welcomed their child in 2018 but very little is known about her. In addition, Rachel shares her son, Henry, with her former partner, director Darren Aronofsky, while Daniel is also a father to 31-year-old daughter Ella from his previous marriage to actress Fiona Loudon.

    The pair split their time between London and New York City, and are notoriously private when it comes to their family life. 

    Rachel opened up to about the idea of being famous, and how she considers herself.

     "Celebrity – it really doesn’t mean anything to me. And it's no work at all to keep not showing up at events. It's no work at all, to keep a private life. Life can be demanding, life in a family can be complicated but I don’t even know what 'celebrity' means. I don't think of myself like that," she said.

    On balancing her busy work life with being a parent, she added: "When you have kids you do have to concentrate more on not bringing it home. And it is a skill to learn, that discipline. I think personally, it’s a skill that’s made me better at my job and better at my life – keeping the two very separate."

     Previously, Rachel also revealed why she kept her marriage to Daniel a secret. She told More magazine: "He's just too famous. It would be a betrayal. You have to protect your marriage.
