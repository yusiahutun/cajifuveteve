Melanie Collins is an American sports broadcaster and host of the multiple shows belonging to CBS and FOX.

She has left no stone unturned in perfecting her sports broadcasting: she has reported for the Winter Olympics, FIFA World Cup, NCAA Football National Championship, Super Bowl, Stanley Cup Playoffs, NFL Draft, NBA Finals, NCAA Basketball Final Four, and The World Series.

Melanie's Career, Education, Salary

Melanie is one of those few people who know what they are doing and grab success.

However, she chose sports in WPSU-TV. With the passion and drive she had, she successfully managed to bag her show with Steve Jones called “Let’s Talk Sports Nittany Lions Fans.”
