Gaither family: members, songs, religion, net worth, latest updates

Publish date: 2024-10-03

Gaither family, also known as the Gaither Vocal Band, is a gospel vocal group named after its founder and leader, Bill Gaither. The group started by recording contemporary Christian music in the 1980s. At the moment, it is famous for Gaither Homecoming videos.

Gaither family is one of the best gospel bands in the United States. The Grammy award-winning group has performed in some of the most prestigious venues in the world. It has also graced the likes of Sydney Opera House, The Kennedy Center, and Carnegie Hall.
