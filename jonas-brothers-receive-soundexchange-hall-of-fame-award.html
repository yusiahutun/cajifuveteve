Jonas Brothers Receive SoundExchange Hall Of Fame Award

Publish date: 2024-09-07

The Jonas Brothers were presented with their SoundExchange Hall of Fame Award (Photo credit: Devin Kasparian)

WASHINGTON, DC (CelebrityAccess) – The Jonas Brothers have been recognized with the prestigious SoundExchange Hall of Fame Award, acknowledging their status as one of the most streamed acts in the organization’s two-decade history. The announcement was made by SoundExchange, a leading music tech organization shaping the future of the music industry.

Michael Huppe, President and CEO of SoundExchange, said, “The Jonas Brothers have consistently reinvented themselves over the years and have built an iron-clad fanbase from their ability to churn out memorable hit songs. It’s been incredible to watch the trio call their own shots as artists and come back to music as a group, still on top. We are honored to present them with the SoundExchange Hall of Fame Award.”

Nick, Joe, and Kevin were formally presented with the SoundExchange Hall of Fame Award by Tiara Guy, Associate Director of Marketing and Industry Relations at SoundExchange. The special recognition was given before the brothers’ scheduled appearance at Capital One Arena in Washington, D.C.

This accolade reflects the enduring popularity and impact of the Jonas Brothers in the music-streaming landscape, underscoring their significant contributions to the industry over the years. The SoundExchange Hall of Fame Award is a testament to the trio’s ability to captivate audiences and maintain their position at the forefront of the music scene.
