Katie Waissel appears to throw shade at her ex Andy Speer as she details raising their son alone

Publish date: 2024-04-02

Katie Waissel has seemingly thrown shade at her ex Andy Speer as she detailed raising their son Hudson as a single mother. 

The former X Factor star, 34, mentioned the father of her two-year-old son in the comments section of her latest Instagram post when responding to a fan who asked where the tot's father was.

Replying to the fan on Monday, Katie referenced her ex who she says left her at '8 months pregnant' and she cryptically said: 'The reality is far from what people perceive.'

The fan made the enquiry under a video of Katie talking about her son being the inspiration behind her fitness, in which she remarked: 'I wanted to be the best I can be for my son because he only has me'.  

As the fan asked: 'Where is his father? I thought you two were friends?', Katie replied: His father is in New York.'

Posting a picture of her then three-month-old on Instagram, the fitness fanatic wrote: 'There are times I look at my son and my heart breaks for him because he is not part of a ‘whole’ family which I dearly wanted him to be.

'I, as his mum, want to save and protect him from all of the questions he has to come when he is older as to why it’s just him and I. 

'However I want him to look back on this photo (and many others) and see that he and I thrived and with each day he grew, I grew, we grew together. 

'I am who I am, and I promise to be the best I can be for the two of us. Him and I.' 

Katie's words came after she gave an interview with Closer magazine when she detailed what went wrong between the couple, who were dating for a year before Katie discovered she was pregnant. 

'Hudson is my only love now': Katie said in October that her son is the only love in her life now and she's in no rush to start dating anyone anytime soon

She said: 'We were in a highly-strung environment and I had a lot of pregnancy hormones so we did row.

'Then one day, I was on our iPad and saw he'd been looking at studio apartments. I was six months' pregnant and suddenly realised I was disposable to him.'

While she didn't rule out a reunion with the personal trainer, she said the trust has gone after he left her three times during the final three months of her pregnancy. 

In October last year, the star said that her son is the only love in her life now and she's in no rush to start dating anyone anytime soon.

Talking to The Sun, she said: 'Hudson is my only love now. Not because I am bitter or stubborn or not open to meeting someone new. I just want to make sure that I am in a good place before I get into a relationship.' 

Katie said her relationship with Hudson's father, personal trainer, Andy, broke down when she was eight months pregnant and so she moved from the US back to the UK.  

She said: 'The baby wasn’t planned but it was a ­beautiful moment. However, I think the bigger I got, reality kicked in for Andy, so he left me when I was eight months pregnant. I guess his anxieties got the better of him.' 

Katie said she was determined to put her sadness over the situation to one side for the sake of her son.

She said Andy 'hasn't allowed himself to be involved' and lives in New York, but she tells him the door is always open for him.
