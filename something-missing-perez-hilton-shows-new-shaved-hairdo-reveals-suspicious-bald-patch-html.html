Perez Hilton shows off new shaved hairdo but reveals bald patch

Publish date: 2024-03-29

He proudly showed off his full head of hair during this year's Celebrity Big Brother. 

But stepping out on Tuesday with his son, Perez Hilton had heads turning for all the wrong reasons as he revealed his new shaven hairdo, which appeared to expose a bald patch.

While his head had clearly been shaved evenly to roughly a Grade 2, the front part appeared significantly thinner than the back.

What's going on hair?: Perez Hilton revealed his new shaven hairdo, which appeared to expose a bald patch, on Tuesday

It is not known if the troublemaker wore hair extensions during his time in the UK reality show house.

In the early days of his success, the celebrity gossip blogger hid what appeared to be a receding hairline, sometimes with the use of wigs and other times by combing his hair to cover the hairline.

Perez was seen on St. Patrick's Day leaving a children's class in West Hollywood with his son Mario (which is also his own birth name).

What a difference: The gossip blogger is pictured last month in Celebrity Big Brother

Family time: Perez was seen on St. Patrick's Day leaving a children's class with two-year-old son Mario, and the pair were both dressed in festive green

The 36-year-old got into the festive spirit and dressed himself and his two-year-old in green T-shirts for the Irish holiday, personally wearing one with Kermit printed on it.

Perez also sported khaki trousers and green trainers with yellow laces, while little Mario wore red trainers and grey shorts. 

While he is now back in Los Angeles, last month the blogger and his son reunited in New York City after he was evicted from the Celebrity Big Brother house in the UK.

Perez, who was booted out days before the finale won by glamour model Katie Price, insisted in a TV interview shortly afterwards that producers cleverly edited footage of him to ensure he came across in a negative light.

Turning heads for the wrong reasons: While the 36-year-old's head had clearly been shaved evenly to roughly a Grade 2, the front part appeared significantly thinner than the back

During his time on the series, he fell out with almost all of his housemates, but said during an appearance on daytime talk show Loose Women that he had been 'naive' about how his 'court jester' routine would come across to the audience.

Perez added: 'I regret, not actions in the house, but regret forever entering. Knowing the experience now, knowing what I’ve seen coming out of it. I wish I could go back in time and not do it.

'I am a big child, I’m a parent but I’m a big child and I am naive and I thought, "Gosh people are going to see me being fun and silly."'
