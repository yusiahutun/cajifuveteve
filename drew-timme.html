Drew Timme- Professional Career

Timme spent the majority of his freshman season as a vital reserve for the Bulldogs, as most players do. Drew was named to the All-Freshman team of the West Coast Conference. On February 8th, 2020, he set a season-best with 20 points in a 90-60 victory over Saint Mary’s. Later, in the Bulldogs’ 2020 West Coast Conference Tournament championship game triumph over Saint Mary’s, he tied for the team lead with 17 points. He shot a team-high 62.1 percent from the floor and averaged 9.8 points and 5.4 rebounds per game.

Timme was chosen to the Preseason All-West Coast Conference squad as well as the Karl Malone Award watchlist during his second season. He had a total of 28 points and 10 rebounds in a 90-67 win over Auburn on November 27th, 2020.
