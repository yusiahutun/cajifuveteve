Nuru Okanga and his wife Emelda continue to serve couple goals to the envy of many.

The political activist, who held his first-ever football tournament in his home constituency together with his wife, thrilled spectators with a lovely dance.

Why did Okanga cancel wedding?

The vocal content creator revealed he was to wed his beautiful wife in October but has been forced to cancel the plans.

"I met around 240 kids at the tournament, and they are in need of stationery and shoes so as not to walk bare feet. I call upon well-wishers to chip in we help these future leaders," he said.

Okanga, who recently announced that he would be vying for an MCA seat in the next general election, added that:

"I had planned to wed in October, but I decided to help the children first and rescheduled the wedding."

Okanga pampered his wife with love, and he said:

"She is very calm and supportive. She loves me the way I am. She always encourages me to work hard."

What is Nuru Okanga's reaction on government banning TikTok?

Okanga revealed the highest amount he ever made from TikTok was KSh 70,000, thanking his fans for supporting him.

Reacting to news that the government was planning to ban TikTok, he said;

"Do these leaders know that some people's livelihoods depend on TikTok? Let them go for those with dirty content, not everyone. Wanataka kukata watu makucha (They want to close the cash tap for may)," he said.

Okanga revealed through TikTok he was able to buy cement which he used to upgrade his life, and was now looking forward to buying tiles for the house through the trendy app.

Nuru Okanga reveals women are after him

In a past interview with TUKO.co.ke Okanga revealed he got a new wife after parting ways with his first wife in late 2023.
