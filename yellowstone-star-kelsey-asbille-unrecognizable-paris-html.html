Yellowstone star Kelsey Asbille is unrecognizable in Paris

Publish date: 2024-09-18

She plays a Native American mother-of-one who lives in Montana on her hit show Yellowstone.

On the Paramount+ series, Kelsey Asbille Chow has on plaid shirts, fitted jeans and cowgirl boots as she resides on the sprawling Dutton Ranch.

That is where she spends time with her husband Kayce Dutton (played by Luke Grimes) and her son Tate Dutton (played by Brecken Merrill) as well as the big boss John Dutton (played by Kevin Costner).

The 31-year-old beauty - who is of Chinese descent - often goes makeup free to play Monica with her hair barely brushed.

But the beauty was glammed to the nines on Tuesday. The TV actress was done up in a black outfit for the Saint Laurent Womenswear Fall Winter 2023-2024 show as part of Paris Fashion Week in Paris, France.

The French connection: Kelsey Asbille Chow was seen glammed up at the Saint Laurent show in Paris on Tuesday

Making a chic statement: Her short hair was styled just so with a part on the side. Kelsey added oversized earrings that made a statement, left. On the right she is seen on Yellowstone

 She wore a black coat over a long black V neck dress that gathered at the side.

Her short hair was styled just so with a part on the side. Kelsey added oversized earrings that made a statement. 

Her makeup was chic with bronze eyeshadow and mauve colored lips.

Yellowstone is part way through its fifth season and likely to return in November. There has been talk the show may end at the conclusion of season five.

On the hit series she plays Native American Monica who was born on a reservation.

She is the granddaughter of Indian elder Felix Long,

Monica's ancestors lived on the northwestern plains for centuries before finally ending up on the Broken Rock Indian Reservation.

She herself grew up on a ranch and had a brother named Robert, who was revenge killed by her husband.

Cropped: After Monica lost her baby during a car accident, she cut her hair off; seen in season five

Monica worked as a teacher then as a professor before home schooling her son.

On the show Monica gets pregnant with her second child but loses the baby when she runs into a buffalo and another car.

Kayce stands by her side and promises to give her another pregnancy soon.

The last episode of season five of Yellowstone shows Costner, who plays John Dutton, asking her to move with Kayce and Tate to one of the homes on his ranch to help him out.

She agrees. 

Kelsey also starred as Mikayla in the Disney XD sitcom Pair of Kings. From 2005 to 2009, she had a recurring role as Gigi Silveri on the drama One Tree Hill. She portrayed Tracy Stewart in MTV's Teen Wolf from 2015 to 2016.
