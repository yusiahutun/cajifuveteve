UFC star Jessica Eye launches OnlyFans page and vows it'll be full of 'adult only' content

Publish date: 2024-08-04

UFC star Jessica Eye has launched an OnlyFans page and vowed it’ll be full of “adult only” content.

The American, who competes as a flyweight in the UFC, has also fired back at trolls who criticised her latest venture on social media.

A number of UFC figures have started pages on the x-rated site, and now Eye has announced on Instagram that she is joining them.

Alongside a picture of her in underwear on a bed, ‘Evil Eye’ wrote: “Hey everyone, I’m super excited about my new adult ONLY content page. Click the link in the BIO to join now.”

Eye’s post received over 5,000 likes, but she still received criticism for her involvement with the site from a select few online.

The 15-9 star responded by writing on Twitter: “People will have a opinion no matter what I the truth is.”

Nevertheless, she also received plenty of support over her decision to sign up, with one user posting: “OnlyFans doesn't equal porn. It equals a way to monetize your content.

“Could be training footage, could be bikini photos. Everyone's content is different. These girls are going to make more money on OF than they will in the UFC... and they'll do both. Watch.

“So let me get this straight... If you do ESPN's the Body Issue, it's ok, but if you post the same photos on OnlyFans, it's frowned upon? Yall are a joke.”

Eye is set to return to the Octagon for her next bout against Jennifer Maia on July 10.

She is not heading into the fight in the best of form, having suffered three defeats in her last four contests.
