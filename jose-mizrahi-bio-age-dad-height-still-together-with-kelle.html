M/LF Manor: Is Jose Mizrahi Still With Kelle Mortensen?

As of 2023, Jose Mizrahi and Kelle Mortensen are on good terms with one another. But it’s unclear if they’ve gone their separate ways or continued their relationship outside.

Fantasies came true when M/LF Manor premiered on January 15, 2023. Older women who also happen to be mothers were dating younger men — almost half their ages! However, the MILFS weren’t simply looking for a good time, but rather a true love who’d be with them when they are even older.

Jose was one of the “young men” looking for love on the show. And luckily, he got to choose between three M/LFs, either Kelle, SoYoung, or Shannan. And as you may already know he gave his heart to Kelle.

Kelle Mortensen is a real estate broker and the owner of Legacy Broker Inc. Born and raised in Lansing, Michigan, Kelle started her career working in sales at a very young age. To fulfill her childhood ambitions, she made then the decision in 1997 to emigrate to California with her infant kid. As a single mother trying to make ends meet, this shift became a welcome uphill struggle. With unrelenting determination and desire, she managed to land a job in outside sales and quickly rose to the position of top-earning salesperson, generating over $15 million in annual billing for a business that generated $80 million in revenue in just 4 years.

Now, coming back to Jose’s time on M/LF Manor, though he went for Kelle, she wasn’t the only one he seemed to have chemistry with. Sparks were flying between Jose and Shannan as well. Moreover, Jose’s mother, Pola Mochon, also gave green light to Shannan.
