Ty Haney Husband

Ty Haney, the wife of Midland’s frontman Mark Wystrach, has welcomed their second child, a baby boy on 5 December 2021. Her husband announced the arrival of the child on his Instagram.

The singer wrote, “I present to you, our Lil hombre… “Champie” was born happy and healthy yesterday at precisely 5 o’clock! Thanks for all of your prayers and positive thoughts for Ty and our son…They’re doing great and Im overwhelmed with joy! Btw, he was 8lbs 13 oz!”

The couple had announced the expecting of their second child in October 2021. She shared on her Instagram that she and her husband are expecting a baby boy in December. “Sunny manifested a baby brother like I did this STRIKEEEE!” she wrote in the caption. “Been busy making eyeballs….and a new biz, both coming down the lane December!.” The post came with a video of her and her daughter in a bowling alley.

Ty Haney is also a mother of their two-year-old daughter Sundance “Sunny” Leon. She was born in November 2019. PageSix reported that she experience complications during the recent birth of her and musician husband’s first baby. A representative for the couple told PageSix that they were expected to make a full recovery in no time.

Ty and Mark married on 8 October 2019 in a low-key outdoor ceremony on the gorgeous landscape of Laguna Gloria in Austin, Texas. She announced the wedding on her Instagram that she captioned, “Made it Official. Love you Mr. Husbandry @markaroo.”

The married couple got engaged in May 2019. The country singer proposed Ty at her father’s farmhouse in Boulder, Colorado.

Previously, Ty dated Austin restauranter Larry McGuire who she met in 2009 in New York. The couple appeared in the 2016 WSJ Magazine feature as “the couple who is redefining [Austin’s] success.”

Ty Haney’s Relationship With Elon Musk

Before her husband Mark, Ty Haney was rumored to be dating SpaceX founder Elon Musk. After splitting with actress Amber Heard, he reportedly started dating Tyler, founder, and CEO of athletic-apparel startup Outdoor Voices. PageSix first revealed they were dating in 2016 when they were spotted together at Miami hotel the Delano.

A source also confirmed to Page Six that Ty and Elon ‘have been dating for a few weeks.’

But, Ty spoke with PageSix and revealed that she and Tesla founder were friends and not dating. Also, a representative for Elon expressed a similar opinion on the nature of Ty and Elon’s relationship back in May 2018.

Speaking with Business Insider, Ty revealed she looked up to Elon as an inspiration. She told the news outlet, “He’s extremely creative. It’s insane just to see how ambitious of a thinker he is. I went into the rocket factory and they are building f—— rockets. That is insane.”

How Old Is Ty Haney?

Ty Haney is 33 years old as of Dec 2021. She celebrates her birthday every year on 3 October.

Ty Haney New Business

Coming from the world of fashion, Ty Haney has now joined the Crypto business. She stated on her Instagram story in May 2021 that she was working on a new business endeavor with a team based on blockchain, the technology that underpins cryptocurrencies.

In the story, Ty wrote, “Hi! Realizing, you may be wondering why I’m ‘suddenly’ so into the blockchain, crypto, etc. Fair. (laughing emoji with teardrops on eyes) I’m currently working on a new company with a very talented group of people that is built on blockchain.” She ended the post with, “It’s essential that the technology and transactions that run on it are green. I also think there are going to be numerous valuable consumer uses-cases for crypto in the future. More soon.”

Outdoor Voices was started by Haney in 2014, shortly after she graduated from Parsons School of Art and Design in New York City, where she studied business. She just had one ambition at the time, which was for her company to become the world’s leading athletics brand.

Ty Haney Net Worth

Ty Haney accumulated a total net worth of $3 million. Outdoor Voices has grown fast in recent years, raising $34 million in a Series C round in March 2021, bringing its total funding to roughly $57 million in 2018. The company’s valuation had dropped from $110 million in 2018 to 40 million in 2020.

Jenn and Mary Ellen created a bright clothing line called Fresh Produce Sportswear shortly after Ty was born, and Ty’s father started a garment-production company in Boulder to fulfill Fresh Produce’s orders.
