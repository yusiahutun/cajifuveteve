Bynes has been given full access to her millions, it has been claimed.

The troubled starlet is splashing the cash after deciding to stop saving, according to reports.

Amanda, 27, apparently demanded access to her money – including the funds tied up in investments – a few months ago.

‘While Amanda was growing up her parents and lawyers controlled all of her money, they put it away in investments so that she would be able to have it when she got older,’ a source told Radar Online.

‘Amanda made a lot of money starting with Nickelodeon and then other TV shows and movies, but she lived like a regular girl when she was young.

‘However, a few months ago she demanded that she have complete access to all of the money so she could spend it, not save it anymore.’

The Easy A star recently told In Touch that she is as rich as the Olsen twins, Mary-Kate and Ashley, who are believed to have a net worth of $300million. 

Splashing the cash: The actress, who tweeted this photo on Sunday, has allegedly decided to stop saving and start spending

However a source claims that in reality Amanda ‘is worth $5 to $6 million’.

‘Amanda knows she has a lot of money. Before she wasn’t restricted from getting to it, but a lot of it was tied up in investments and banked for her future, but she insisted that she be able to use all of it as she sees fit and there is nothing anyone can do to stop her from spending it all,’ the first insider added.

The former star of The Amanda Show continues to display bizarre behaviour and recently shaved off half of her hair.

And it has been claimed that she is taking an equally erratic approach to her spending.

‘Amanda can do whatever she wants for a while with all of her money but it isn't going to last forever if she spends the way she has been lately,’ the source said. ‘She's rich but she could find herself in financial trouble pretty soon.’
