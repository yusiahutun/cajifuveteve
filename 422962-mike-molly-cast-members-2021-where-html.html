Mike and Molly cast members in 2021: Where are they now?

Publish date: 2024-08-02

If you have watched many sitcoms, you will realise that it is rare to find one that guarantees you a laugh for every episode. Mike and Molly was that sitcom that made people laugh for its entire 127 episodes. The writing made it a superb, non-stop laughing affair. But achieving such a milestone could not have been easy if it were not for the talented Mike and Molly cast.

Mike and Molly ran from 2010 to 2016 for six seasons. Although the show did not dominate primetime, it became a popular culture sensation filled with the never-ending debate about weight and weight loss. Regardless, Mike and Molly cast were well developed, and each one of them brought a uniquely funny and quirky personality to the sitcom. But what happened to them after the cancellation of the show?

Mike and Molly characters went on to work as actors and actresses in other projects. Here is what some of them did between 2016 and 2021.

1. Billy Gardell

After appearing in 127 episodes of the show, Billy Gardell, who played Mike, immediately went to star in a movie called Undrafted. He also took on several recurring roles on shows like New Girl and Angie Tribeca. His first lead role after Molly and Mike was in 2017 when he played Colonel Tom Parker in Sun Records. As of 2021, Billy has another lead role in the sitcom TV series Bob Hearts Abishola.

Is Billy Gardell married in real life? Bill Gardell married Patty Knight in 2001. The couple has a son, William III, born in 2003. In 2011, Bill hinted that he might have to retire once his son becomes a teenager to spend more time with him.

2. Melissa McCarthy

Melissa McCarthy played Molly in the sitcom for 127 episodes. After the show, she went on to work as an actress, screenwriter, and producer for several movies, including The Boss (2016), Life of the Party (2018), and Superintelligence (2020). In 2021, she will be featuring in several TV shows and movies, including Nine Perfect Strangers (2021 TV series), Thunder Force (2021), and The Starling (2021).

3. Reno Wilson

Reno Wilson played Officer Carlton McMillan in the show for 127 episodes. In 2019, he took on the role of Louis Armstrong in the drama film Bolden. Wilson then landed a regular role in 2018 in the crime comedy-drama television series Good Girls. However, the show got cancelled in 2021 after four seasons.

4. Katy Mixon

Katy Mixon appeared in all 127 episodes of the show, playing Victoria Flynn. Her immediate job after Mike and Molly was in the neo-Western crime drama film Hell and High Water, and she received a Critics' Choice Movie Award nomination for Best Acting Ensemble for portraying Jenny Ann. Also, the actress played Katie Otto for 96 episodes in the sitcom American Housewife until its cancellation in 2021.

5. Swoosie Kurtz

Swoosie Kurtz also appeared in all 127 episodes of Mike and Molly, playing Joyce Flynn-Moranto, mother of Molly and Victoria. Between 2017 and 2020, she took on a recurring role as Beverly Burns in the television sitcom Man with a Plan. In 2021, Swoosie landed a leading role in the sitcom television series Call Me Kat, portraying Sheila.

6. Nyambi Nyambi

Nyambi Nyambi played the role of Babatunde, aka Samuel, for 118 episodes. Mike and Molly was his big break in the world of acting. After the show, he joined The Good Fight as a law firm investigator and became a series regular during season two. The Good Fight is now in its fifth season, with the sixth season renewed for 2022.

7. Louis Mustillo

Louis Mustillo played Vincent "Vince" Moranto for 112 episodes of Mike and Molly. After the show, he did not involve himself in many projects. However, he played Humphrey in the 2018 TV show, Strange Angel. In 2021, he appeared in Season 11, Episode 14 of the police procedural drama television series Blue Bloods as Lenny.

8. Rondi Reed

Rondi Reed portrayed Margaret "Peggy" Biggs, Mike's mother, for 84 episodes of Mike and Molly. In 2016, she landed a recurring role as Edith Schiff in the period drama television series titled Masters of Sex. Reed continued to work on recurring roles in several TV shows, including the 2021 television sitcom Mom as Jolene in Season 8 Episode 18.

9. Cleo King

Cleo King became a series regular in Mike and Molly, portraying Rosetta McMillan, Carl's grandmother. After 57 episodes, she played Mrs Watkins in the 2017 supernatural horror film The Bye Bye Man. King also played several roles in TV shows, such as Grey's Anatomy (2019) and Hoops (2020). In 2021, she appeared in one episode of 9-1-1: Lone Star as Cynthia Strickland.

10. David Anthony Higgins

David Anthony Higgins has not been as active as the rest of his co-actors in Mike and Molly since 2016. Between 2016 and 2017, he only appeared in three episodes of three different shows, including Mom, School of Rock, and Stan Against Evil. In 2020, he landed another recurring role as Jerry in the television sitcom B Positive.

Why was Mike and Molly cancelled?

CBS, the network that aired Mike and Molly, opted not to renew the seventh season. The decision came after CBS and Warner Bros TV, the producing studio, failed to negotiate a new license agreement. As a result, the series concluded on 16 May 2016 after six seasons and 127 episodes. Most of the cast of Mike and Molly now work on various projects, both on TV and in films.

Did the cast of Mike and Molly get along? The cast of Mike and Molly got along well from the beginning to the end of the show. Some of them even cried the whole week when it was clear that CBS would not be renewing the series for the seventh season. But the group loved one another and continued to talk even after the conclusion of the sitcom.

What happened to the Captain on Mike and Molly?

Captain Patrick Murphy is a character in Mike and Molly played by Gerald McRaney. He only appeared in six episodes of the show between 2012 and 2013. After Mike and Molly, Gerald moved on to other projects, including House of Cards, NCIS: Los Angeles, and 24: Legacy. In 2017, he won the Primetime Creative Arts Emmy Award for Outstanding Guest Actor in a Drama Series for playing Dr Nathan Katowski in the drama television series This Is Us.

Are Mike and Molly married in real life? Unfortunately, the two are not married in real life. However, Billy Gardell, who played Officer Michael Biggs, has been married to Patty Knight since 2001, even before the show began in 2010.
