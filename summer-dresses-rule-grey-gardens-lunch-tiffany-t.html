Summer dresses rule at the Grey Gardens lunch for the Tiffany T collection pre-launch

Publish date: 2024-03-28

WHAT: Tiffany T collection pre-launch lunch party

WHERE: Grey Gardens, East Hampton, New York

HAPPENINGS: The historic Grey Gardens, home of American fashion designer and entrepreneur Liz Lange, was the setting for Net-a-Porter and Tiffany & Co.’s lunch party celebrating the exclusive pre-launch of the Tiffany T collection extension.

Flower-power finds expression in high jewellery pieces

It was summer dresses galore at the Grey Gardens luncheon. Sports Illustrated Swimsuit model Nina Agdal looked fresh in gingham check while American author Jessica Seinfeld wore an off-the-shoulder green polka dot dress and a statement belt.

Fall in number of free-spending Chinese tourists hits Tiffany & Co.

Award-winning shoe designer Tabitha Simmons and lifestyle blogger Athena Calderone opted for statement prints while Jamaican-born street style photographer Tamu McPherson kept things minimalist in a simple cream slip dress. Fashion designer Rachel Zoe exemplified her bohemian aesthetic in a ruffled white floral printed number.

STYLE Edit: Reed Krakoff brings inspiration to Tiffany & Co.

Other attendees included American model and actress Dree Hemingway, former Argentinian model and co-founder of Àcheval Pampa, Delfina Blaquier, Jessica Joffe, Olivia Chantecaille, and former Harper’s Bazaar editor and designer Amanda Ross.

Guests to the exclusive pre-launch celebration accessorised with pieces from the extended Tiffany T collection. The extension of the T collection includes pieces in 18 carat yellow, white and rose gold with inlaid stones, such as tiger’s eye, turquoise, pink opal, black onyx and mother-of-pearl, before being further embellished with diamonds.
