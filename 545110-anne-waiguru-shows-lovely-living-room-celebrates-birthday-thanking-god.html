Anne Waiguru Shows Off Her Lovely Living Room as She Celebrates Birthday: &quot;Thanking God&quot;

Publish date: 2024-09-17
  • Anne Waiguru was surprised with cake and flowers by fellow governors as she turned a year older
  • The Kirinyaga county boss then headed home for a low-key celebration, sharing images of her living room
  • Many Kenyans noticed the luxurious abode, while others noted that the politician looked ageless

Kirinyaga governor Anne Waiguru has celebrated turning a year older in style.

Inside Anne Waiguru's home

The politician's birthday on Tuesday, April 16, coincided with a meeting by the council of governors.

Images shared by Waiguru showed her compatriots surprising her with a birthday cake and flowers.

Numerous governors handed her bouquets, including Kisumu governor Anyang Nyong'o.

She eventually cut a cake they bought for her as her colleagues watched on, smiling.

“Lovely birthday surprise after a very tough COG meeting. Thank you. I am really honoured to serve alongside you all. God bless you abundantly,” she captioned the images.

Waiguru then retired to her luxurious home to spend the rest of her day. She posted images of lounging on a grey chair with blue pillows.

Before she was, a cup of tea and arrow roots set atop a classy wooden table with patterned legs.

Behind Waiguru was a fireplace with numerous family photos, including of her husband, Kamotho Waiganjo.

Waiguru's living room has exposed rafters painted white against a white V-shaped ceiling.

The governor praised God for blessing her with the gift of life, writing:

“And He makes everything beautiful in his time... Ecclesiastes 3:11. Thanking God for another year.”

Why does Waiguru, hubby run two homes?

Previously speaking about her marriage, Waiguru said she and her husband Kamotho Waiganjo had been together for 10 years and they run two homes.

The county boss said her family meets for breakfast every Saturday morning to maintain their bond in the midst of their busy schedules.

However, she told NTV that they do not live separately.
