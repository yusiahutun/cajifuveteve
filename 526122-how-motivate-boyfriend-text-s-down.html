  • You don't have to get everything right. Success is made of failure and lessons a long way.
  • It is tough for you, but you have been through worse and overcome. I am rooting for you, baby.
  • I am sorry you must go through everything. I would do anything to make you feel better.
  • I am sending you lots of hugs and kisses to make you feel better.
  • Remember that whatever happens, I am behind you. Nothing will ever change my love for you.
