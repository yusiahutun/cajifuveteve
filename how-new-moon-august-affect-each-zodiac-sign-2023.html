The New Moon in Leo Will Affect Every Zodiac Sign 2023

Publish date: 2024-04-20

Authenticity means having the courage to lead from the heart. Your enthusiasm is contagious, and with Leo season in full effect, there’s no better time to discover and reconnect with your passions. That said, before we discuss how the new moon in Leo of August 2023 will affect each zodiac sign, it’s important to hold space for your inner child and your heart’s desires. What brings you joy? Do you feel confident exploring your natural curiosities? Renowned Swiss psychiatrist and psychotherapist Carl Jung was a Leo sun, and he really couldn’t have said it better: “The privilege of a lifetime is to become who you truly are.”

Majestic and high-spirited, Leo’s zodiac archetype is a representation of creation, fertility, individuality, leadership, and creative expression. Governed by the sun—the center of the universe and our greatest source of energy—it’s no wonder Leo natives find comfort in being the center of attention. Their sparkling charisma and sunny disposition is more than often associated with fame and royalty, but there’s so much more to this fire sign than meets the eye. On the surface, it’s easy to assume their confidence stems from an egotistical place, but their ability to glow from the inside out is part of their celestial mission. The source of all creation, the sun is symbolic of our divinity and soul purpose in astrology… and when in a high vibration, Leo brings sunshine, energy, and vitality to the people around them. This, in turn, inspires and encourages others to be their most authentic selves, as they are accepted and celebrated in their presence. 

Don’t have Leo prominently placed in your birth chart? You’re still not off the hook, as we all have a little bit of each sign in our birth chart. For instance, check which of the 12 astrological houses belong to Leo in your birth chart. Believe it or not, this is the area of life where you’re capable of shining as bright as the sun. Whether it be your sixth house of health and fitness or perhaps your committed relationship sector, this is where you’re most likely to take the lead, and be unapologetically yourself. With all of that being said, August’s new moon will renew itself via 23 degrees of Leo on August 16 at exactly 5:38 a.m. ET. 

The new moon phase is an incredibly fertile time, so don’t be discouraged by the moon’s lack of visibility. On the contrary, in addition to marking the beginning of the lunar cycle, the new moon occurs when the sun joins the moon, and via the exact same degree of a zodiac sign. In this case, the moon will renew itself in Leo, so be sure to reflect on what you would like to manifest within the next six months, specifically when it comes to themes of creativity, leadership, passion projects, and self-expression. Moreover, it’s important to consider the effects of Venus retrograde in Leo, as it will be traveling just a few degrees behind this new moon. However, with the planet of relationships, finances and value systems lingering in the shadows, this could trigger the desire to remain incognito, and work on something behind the scenes.

Venus retrograde and the new moon will be at odds with change-maker Uranus, forming quite the shocking and volatile square, which is where things are bound to get a little weird. Look at it this way: Venus has been retrograde since July 22, asking us to re-assess and re-evaluate everything from our financial stability to our relationship dynamics. Self-love is also a big one for Venus retrograde in Leo, so keep that in mind. Meanwhile, the new moon is presenting us with a new beginning, where we can take the stage, and manifest our heart’s desires. Uranus, on the other hand, is adding a layer of disruption and unexpected changes to this new beginning, specifically when it comes to our sense of security. However, despite the chaos we’re likely to endure under this lunation, the rebel planet is shaking things up in our favor, and liberating us in the process. 

There’s a silver lining though, as disruptive Uranus will simultaneously harmonize with Mercury & Mars in Virgo, bringing some much-needed grounding and support. For instance, where Uranus is prone to bringing unexpected changes, Mercury & Mars are providing us with the mental focus and problem-solving skills needed to simplify things moving forward.
