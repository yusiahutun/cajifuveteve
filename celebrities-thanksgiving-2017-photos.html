Thanksgiving 2017: All the best photos of stars giving thanks

Publish date: 2024-04-02

    Celebrities certainly have a lot to be grateful for, and they showed it to the world in honour of Thanksgiving 2017. From pre-holiday celebrations to parade performances to cozy family dinners, stars like Jennifer Lopez, Drew Barrymore and more took to social media to give thanks for all they have! Click through to see how Hollywood commemorated turkey day.

    Along with a fun video in the kitchen, Viola Davis shared a heartfelt post to Instagram for Thanksgiving. The ABC star wrote: "My favorite holiday by far because I get to reflect about what I love about my life. Usually, I focus on what needs to be BETTER! Ahh....but God has blessed me to fear vulnerability but still have the courage to seek it. Understanding that my heart, my truth, my authenticity is my greatest asset. AND my flaws, my mistakes are to be forgiven....with grace....God's grace...daily. Happy Thanksgiving from my family to yours...with LOVE ❤❤❤"

    Photo: Instagram/@violadavis

    Keeping up with being grateful! Kim Kardashian, her mum Kris Jenner and her grandma MJ were surrounded by family as they celebrated Thanksgiving in a gorgeous looking space. The reality star, who looked fabulous in a two piece that flaunted her tight tummy, captioned her holiday post: "So thankful!"

    Photo: Instagram/@kimkardashian

    Wear a cape for extra space! Reese Witherspoon was strategic about her Thanksgiving fashion, when she chose a plaid cape from her line Draper James so she could fit more food. "My 'Is it time to eat yet' dance. #Thanksgiving #WearingTheCapeForExtraSpace" the star wrote on Instagram alongside a boomerang of her dancing.

    Photo: Instagram/@reesewitherspoon

    Victoria Beckham showed some serious Thanksgiving spirit! The fashion designer got creative as she celebrated Thanksgiving while "dressed up" as a turkey for the occasion. Taking to Instagram stories, the former Spice Girl posted a photoshopped image of herself sitting on the floor in a feathered bodysuit. She captioned the fun post: "Happy Thanksgiving VB X." The mother-of-four looked stylish in the image, with her brunette hair styled in a sleek, straight do, and a vibrant red lipstick added a glamorous touch to her fake look.

    Photo: Instagram/@victoriabeckham

    Pulling back the curtain of her Thanksgiving prep, Oprah gave fans a glimpse behind the scenes of her cooking time! The star looked thrilled as she got ready, boasting that she had four turkeys being prepared for the special day, and a delicious looking dish with white truffles.

    Photo: Instagram/@oprah

    Beyonce wished her fans a wonderful Thanksgiving with several photos of herself modeling her clothing line. The 36-year-old superstar used the hashtag #THICCsgiving as she danced around wearing a 'Have A THICC Holiday' purple crewneck, which is available to buy on her website Beyonce.com. Queen Bey wore the top with a pair of high-waisted faded blue jeans and a white winter overcoat.

    Photo: beyonce.com

    Double celebration! Thanksgiving happened to fall on Miley Cyrus's birthday in 2017 and she was pumped for it. The singer posted to Instagram throughout the day and the night before, showing off some killer balloons, writing: "So close to b-day time! Stoked for a day full of Tofurkey & loved ones! Ain't it ironic? #VeganTurkeyBaby"

    Photo: Instagram/@mileycyrus

    Joe Manganiello and his little nephew Rafi showed their thanks for a couple of very fun hobbies: music and games! "Just two dudes sharing an appreciation for the finer things in life like D&D and Nirvana," Joe wrote along with the cute family pic on his Instagram.

    Photo: Instagram/@joemanganiello

    Zen-giving! Looking peaceful on a beautiful beach, Zac Efron spent his Thanksgiving day reflecting on everything that he is grateful for. "‪Feeling thankful for all the love and support I receive from my fans, family and friends. Happy Thanksgiving all. #gratitude ‬#thanksgiving," the actor wrote along with the photo on Instagram.

"Happy Thanksgiving from paradise ?," she wrote along with the picture.
