Itziar Ituño is a talented and popular Basque Actress who has worked in more than 50 films and TV series. She is also known for her role named Inspector Raquel Murillo in series named Money Heist or La casa de Papel which is also on Netflix. On her Instagram account, she has 4.8 million followers.

Early Life

Itziar was born on 18th June 1974 in Basauri, Basque Country, Spain. The details about her parents and siblings have been kept private and away from the media. She decided to choose acting as her career since she was young. She has completed her high school studies, but the name of the school has been kept private. For her higher studies, she studied acting from the Basauri Theatre School and also went to the University of the Basque Country where she graduated in Urban- Industrial and Political sociology.

Itziar Ituño Career

In 1995 and 1996 she worked in various short films named Al, Ben & Gail, La Leccion, Ricardo, and Sex-Machine. In 1997 she made her film debut by working in Agur Olentzero agur!. Next year she was seen in a short film named Otxate. In 1999 she made her debut series by working in Goenkale where she had the role of Nekane Beitia. From year 2000 to 2016 she was seen working in many TV series and films like Finlandia, Zuek hor, Kaixo2016, Vaya Semanita, Teilatupean, Kilker dema, Arkadia, Silencios, Entereza, Barbara, Sabin, Loreak, Morir, Igelak and many more.
