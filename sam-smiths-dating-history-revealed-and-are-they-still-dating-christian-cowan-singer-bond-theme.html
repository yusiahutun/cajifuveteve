2. Charlie King

In 2015, Smith and reality TV star Charlie King from The Only Way is Essex had a discreet six-month romance.

4. Andy Newton Lee

In April 2016, Smith was rumoured to be involved with Hollyoaks actor Andy Newton Lee after a close encounter at Coachella. Despite the buzz and speculation surrounding them after Coachella, neither Smith nor Lee publicly confirmed rumours about their fling.

5. Brandon Flynn

In October 2017, Smith’s relationship with 13 Reasons Why actor Brandon Flynn became public after they were seen kissing in New York City. Their nine-month relationship was marked by public affection and warm acknowledgements in interviews and social media. However, reports of their break-up emerged by June 2018, evidenced by Smith removing Flynn’s photos from their Instagram.

Despite the split, Smith stressed that the separation was amicable, with no hard feelings, per The Sunday Times Style magazine.
