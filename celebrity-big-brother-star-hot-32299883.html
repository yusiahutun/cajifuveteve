Celebrity Big Brother star had hot tube scene with adult actress but girlfriend walked in

Publish date: 2024-06-13

Celebrity Big Brother star Stevi Ritchie recalled a moment he got in trouble on the reality show.

Stevi starred on the show back in 2015 when he went in as one housemate with his ex-girlfriend and fellow X Factor star Chloe Jasmine. But things got heated when his girlfriend walked in on him in the hot tub with a porn icon.

Despite Stevi confessing it "looked bad" when he was sat with "Queen of Porn" Jenna Jameson and Teen Mom's Farrah Abraham, he assured us "nothing happened". But he said he could understand how it could be misconstrued on the camera.

"But it probably doesn't look good when I'm sitting with a porn star in a hot tub and then my girlfriend at the time walks in. A little awkward, but it was totally innocent. She also had a boyfriend that was a cage fighter, so absolutely not."

The couple famously came to blows quite a lot on the show and at the time Chloe had some stern words for him. When Stevi asked if she was angry with him due to the hot tube, she said: “Darling, course I’m not in a mood with you. I’m sure other girls would be a bit weird with you being in a hot tub with two retired porn actresses. I’m not that kind of girl.”

Stevi replied: “Babe, I’ll tell you what it is – I thought it was only going to be Austin [Armacost] and James [Hill]. Honestly, I’ve done absolutely nothing.” The TV star also spoke about his past relationship on the show and said it was "difficult" at times to navigate - but he "doesn't have any regrets".

The pair ended up breaking up for good a year later and going their separate ways after previously getting engaged before entering the CBB house. Despite having a turbulent CBB journey, Stevi said he'd still be up for going back in.

He added: "I would definitely love to go back in for an All Stars version - though if I went in, they would probably put Chloe back in. That would be interesting, I would be civil, she's a great girl and I have no bad blood against her."
