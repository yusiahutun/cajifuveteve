Pregnant Erin O'Connor Poses Nude

Publish date: 2024-06-19

Picture: Nick Knight/SHOWstudio/Instagram

Eight-months-pregnant Erin O'Connor has been captured nude in a striking new portrait for photographer Nick Knight. Following in the footsteps of Natalia Vodianova, Eva Herzigova and Gisele Bundchen, who have all had maternal moments in recent months, Erin O’Connor displayed her prominent bump in the shoot for Knight's SHOWStudio this week.
 
The photographer promptly shared the results on Instagram with the caption: “The wonderful Erin O Connor, 8-and-a-half months pregnant. Beautiful. I will be publishing a small portfolio of the images later this week on SHOWstudio. Thank you @ezzaoconnor xxx.”
 
The image quickly gained more than 3200 likes (and counting) and it’s easy to see why. The 36-year-old first time mother looks radiant and at ease so close to her due date. We can’t wait to see the first images of her newborn.
