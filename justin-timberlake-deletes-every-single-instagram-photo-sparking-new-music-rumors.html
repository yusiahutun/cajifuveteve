Justin Timberlake deletes every single Instagram photo sparking new music rumors

Publish date: 2024-07-09

Justin Timberlake has cleared his Instagram of pictures, and people wonder whether this might mean new music is on the way.

Aside from sporadic singles and songs released from his work on the animated movie Trolls (which he stars in alongside Anna Kendrick), Justin hasn’t released much since his 2018 album Man of the Woods. However, a new JT era could be upon us as he has cleared his Instagram clean of pictures and changed his profile picture on the platform.

Fans speculate something might be coming

On X (formerly Twitter) fans have been wondering if new music could be on the horizon from Justin Timberlake. After not producing any new projects in more than half a decade, something might be arriving soon. Perhaps he was just taking his time to make it the best it could possibly be.

However, others weren’t convinced and had some pretty harsh words about a potential comeback from the celebrity.

One penned: “Singers doing this before the release of a project is so cringe.”

Another wrote: “Let’s hope it stays this way [prayer hands emoji].”

A third said: “He can delete the account too. We won’t miss him.”

Justin Timberlake could be releasing new music

In the digital age, artists tend to clear their social media feeds when they start new projects (whether it be a film, TV show, or new music) and this seems to be what Justin Timberlake is doing. A new album from the 42-year-old would be his sixth solo release … but could it be solo?

In the past year, Justin has reunited with his former NSYNC bandmates numerous times, including the MTV Video Music Awards last year. They presented the Best Pop Award to a shocked Taylor Swift who asked them if they were “doing something,” which is a valid question considering they appeared without any other obvious reason.

However, a few days later the five men released a single for the latest Trolls film. But is a full-on music reunion on the cards?

What has Justin said about an NSYNC reunion?

Fans may be disappointed to learn the Hollywood star hasn’t said too much about a reunion. However, he did post on Instagram (before, of course, deleting it) about reuniting at the premiere of the Trolls film.

He teased in the caption: “So proud to celebrate the new @trolls movie and album with the crew. Gotta say this is my favorite so far [prayer hands emoji] Has been good to be back with my boys [eyes emoji].”

It had previously been claimed the boyband member was “single-handedly” stopping a reunion.

A source told Radar Online a few years ago: “Lance Bass and the other guys in the group would love to have a reunion tour and record new music but Justin is the one stopping it. He has worked so hard to establish himself as a solo artist and has no interest in going back into the past.

“If the guys want to do it without him, they are welcome, but Justin will not be part of it.”
