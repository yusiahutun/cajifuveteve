Tara Thomas Signs A PR Deal With Reign of Unity And NFL Alumni Partners

Publish date: 2024-03-01

ATLANTA (CelebrityAccess) — Veteran Atlanta-based public relations executive Tara Thomas announced that she has been named as the official Public Relations representative of Reign Of Unity and its affiliate NFL Alumni Partners.

Through the partnership, Reign of Unity will leverage Thomas’ expertise and connections in the entertainment industry to further promote their initiatives and events.

“We are thrilled to welcome Tara Thomas and her agency as our official Public Relations representative. Her extensive experience and proven track record make her the ideal partner to elevate awareness for our initiatives,” stated Liffort Hobley, Reign of Unity’s Director of Community Engagement.

“Joining forces with Reign Of Unity and its affiliate NFL Alumni Partners is a privilege. I am committed to promoting their significant work and believe that together, we can drive positive change in our communities,” Thomas added.

Reign of Unity fosters social and emotional development by addressing a range of significant challenges such as poverty alleviation, gender equality, education, health security, and food security.

For children, Reign of Unity provides interactive workshops and activities that help to teach communication skills, empathy, conflict resolution, and teamwork. For adults, Reign of Unity offers opportunities to become mentors and leaders, serving as a positive role model for younger generations while honing one’s own interpersonal skills.

The inaugural event will be the first all-female 7 on 7 Flag Football Day Camp during Super Bowl week. Advance registration is mandatory due to limited spaces, open exclusively to middle and high school students.
